# Napapijri

## Overview
Napapijri is an Italian fashion brand that has embraced sustainability as a core part of its identity, particularly known for its innovative outerwear and winter clothing. The brand combines technical performance with eco-conscious materials and circular design principles.

## Sustainability Practices
- **Recycled Materials**: Uses recycled polyester, nylon, and other fibers in its products
- **Circular Design**: Focuses on creating products that can be recycled or repurposed at end-of-life
- **Innovation**: Invests in research for sustainable materials and production techniques
- **Transparency**: Provides information about materials and production processes

## Product Range
- Eco-conscious parkas and winter jackets
- Fleece hoodies and sweaters
- Puffer jackets with sustainable insulation
- Outerwear for men, women, and children
- Accessories including bags and hats

## Key Initiatives
- Use of recycled materials in iconic Rainforest jacket
- Commitment to reducing carbon footprint across supply chain
- Focus on durability to extend product lifespan
- Exploration of bio-based and circular materials

## Materials
- Recycled polyester and nylon
- Sustainable cotton alternatives
- Eco-friendly insulation materials
- Responsibly sourced wool and down (where used)

## Certifications
While specific certifications may vary by product line, Napapijri focuses on material innovation and sustainable production practices rather than relying solely on third-party certifications.

## Pricing
Napapijri positions itself in the premium outerwear segment, with jackets typically ranging from $150 to $500+ depending on style and materials. The brand offers periodic sales and maintains a direct-to-consumer online presence alongside retail partnerships.

## Availability
Available through:
- Official website: www.napapijri.com
- Selected retail partners worldwide
- Brand stores in major cities

## Brand Story
Founded in 1987 in Italy, Napapijri (which means "Arctic Circle" in Finnish) began as a producer of travel bags and has evolved into a global lifestyle brand known for its distinctive outerwear and commitment to sustainable innovation.
