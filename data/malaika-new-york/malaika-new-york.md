## Overview

Malaika New York creates unique zero waste garments with zero waste packaging. The brand uses innovative rectangular patterns that reduce clothing waste to just 15-20% compared to the industry standard of 30%.

## Features

- Zero waste garments
- Zero waste packaging
- Rectangular pattern innovation
- 15-20% waste vs 30% industry standard

- Unique designs
- Waste reduction focus
- Sustainable pattern making
- Environmental commitment

## Sustainability Practices

Malaika New York has developed innovative rectangular pattern-making techniques that reduce textile waste to 15-20% compared to the typical 30% industry waste. The brand uses biodegradable bags for 70% of its products and maintains zero-waste packaging practices.

Offers zero-waste designed clothing with unique silhouettes created through innovative pattern cutting.
