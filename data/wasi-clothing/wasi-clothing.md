## Overview

Wasi Clothing is a California-based sustainable fashion brand conceived by founder Vanessa Acosta after years of feeling undervalued, overworked, and underrepresented as a Latina in the fashion industry. The brand meshes Acosta's identity with her commitment to sustainability.

## Sustainability Practices

- Sustainable textile options sourced directly by the founder
- Conscious packaging throughout the supply chain
- Every part of the brand is intentional, from sourcing to representation
- Respect for the sacredness and magic of Bolivian ancestors

## Design Aesthetic

- Latin American meets cottagecore meets 70s revival themes
- Bolivian cultural representation highlighting an underrepresented community

## Product Range

- Clothing with culturally inspired designs
- Accessories and household items reflecting the brand's heritage and sustainability values
