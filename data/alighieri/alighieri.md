## Overview

Alighieri is a UK-based jewellery brand creating ultimate heirloom pieces that transcend time and trends, known for vibrant, chunky gold chains adorned with rough wabi-sabi style charms and pearls.

## Sustainability Practices

- **Local manufacturing**: All production based in the UK
- **Recycled metals**: Uses 100% recycled metals including bronze and silver before plating and casting with gold
- **Recycled packaging**: Sustainable packaging materials used across all shipping
- **Carbon Impact report**: Publicly available sustainability reporting

## Design Style

Heavy, sentimental pieces promoting simultaneous feelings of decadence and grounding, with wabi-sabi style rough charms and pearls.
