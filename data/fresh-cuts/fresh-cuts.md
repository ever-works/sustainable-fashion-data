## Overview

Fresh Cuts is an Irish owned ethical clothing brand and retailer offering garments for men and women with focus on socially responsible supply chains.

## Materials

- Cotton
- Organic cotton
- Bamboo
- Recycled cotton
- Recycled polyester and rayon

## Social Responsibility

- Zero tolerance policy for child labour, forced labour and excessive working hours
- Some suppliers are Fair Wear Foundation members
- GOTS logo for organic cotton
- Soil Association Organic logo

## Retail

- Online store
- Physical store in Dublin 2
- Also stocks clothing from other ethical brands: Armed Angels, Dedicated, Mud Jeans, Monkee Jeans

## Sizing

- XS to XXL
