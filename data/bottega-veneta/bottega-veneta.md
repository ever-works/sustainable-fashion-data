## Overview

Bottega Veneta is an Italian luxury fashion house recognized for its commitment to durability and innovative sustainable practices. The brand made notable appearances at the Spring/Summer 2026 runway with unique recycled materials, including a recycled fibre-glass sweater.

## Sustainability Highlights

- Uses unconventional recycled materials such as fibre-glass in fashion items, one of the least common materials in the fashion world
- Products designed and crafted to last for generations, emphasizing durability over fast-fashion cycles
- Focus on timeless, well-made luxury products that reduce long-term waste
- Incorporating unique sustainable solutions that push industry boundaries

## Product Philosophy

Bottega Veneta's approach to sustainability centers on longevity and craftsmanship. Items are made to last for generations, encouraging a mindset of investing in durable, well-made pieces rather than disposable fashion.
