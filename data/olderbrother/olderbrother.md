## Overview

Olderbrother is a Portland brand that combines a laid-back attitude and playful aesthetic with progressive sustainable production practices to create ethical clothing for men and women.

## Sustainability Practices

- All garments are dyed with natural dyes
- Materials are carefully sourced from responsible suppliers
- Produces ethical clothing for both men and women

## Hand Me Down Series

- Re-dyes and recycles old garments
- Creates unique, one-of-a-kind pieces
- Reduces textile waste through upcycling

## Aesthetic

Known for a laid-back, Portland-inspired aesthetic with playful design elements and colorful, naturally-dyed garments.
