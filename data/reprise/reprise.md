## Overview

Reprise is an activewear brand focused on creating clean, plastic-free alternatives to conventional synthetic fitness clothing. The brand uses natural fibers like TENCEL Lyocell and organic cotton to produce activewear without microplastics or toxins.

## Materials Used

- TENCEL Lyocell
- Organic cotton

## Certifications

- OEKO-TEX Standard 100 (for TENCEL materials)
- Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS) certified dyes for leggings
- Fair Trade Certification (on some materials)
- WRAP (Worldwide Responsible Accredited Production) for ethical manufacturing and labor standards

## Size Range

XS to 6XL

## Notable Products

- Aspen Leggings
- Maris Flare Leggings
- Selene Zip Up
- Biker Shorts
- Half Zip Cotton Sweatshirt
- Tank tops

## Pricing

Premium activewear pricing, reflecting sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing practices.
