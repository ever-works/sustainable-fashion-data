## Overview

Founded in 2004, Frugi has become the UK's leading ethical and organic children's clothing brand, operating in over 30 countries.

## Sustainability Features

- Majority of clothing from organic cotton
- Uses recycled materials
- GOTS certified
- Sustainable materials throughout
- Large range of eco-friendly products
- Ethical production

## Materials

- Organic cotton (majority)
- Recycled materials
- Sustainable fabrics
- Natural rubber

## Product Range

Extensive range including everyday clothing, swimwear, accessories, outerwear, and even natural rubber boots for children.

## Global Reach

Available in over 30 countries, making ethical children's clothing accessible worldwide.

## Heritage

Over 20 years of ethical fashion leadership, pioneering organic and sustainable practices in children's clothing industry.
