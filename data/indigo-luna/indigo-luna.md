## Overview

Indigo Luna is an Australian brand creating eco-conscious yoga and lifestyle clothing. The brand focuses on sustainable materials, ethical production, and designs that support movement and comfort for yoga and everyday wear.

## Features

- Australian eco-conscious brand
- Yoga and lifestyle focus
- Organic materials
- Recycled fabrics
- Ethical production practices
- Comfortable, functional designs
- Movement-friendly cuts
- Sustainable manufacturing
- Versatile activewear

## Sustainability Practices

Indigo Luna uses organic and recycled materials throughout its collections, maintaining ethical production practices and minimizing environmental impact. The brand creates versatile pieces suitable for both yoga practice and everyday lifestyle wear.

## Product Range

Offers yoga pants, leggings, tops, sports bras, and lifestyle clothing designed for comfort and movement.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable Australian activewear.
