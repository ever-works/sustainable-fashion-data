## Overview

Founded in 2012 by Natalie Patricia, Harvest & Mill sources and manufactures organic cotton clothing entirely in the USA with complete supply chain transparency.

## Sustainability Achievements

- 70% less greenhouse gas emissions than other sustainable brands
- 53% less overall energy use
- 50% less water usage
- 100% sewn within 20-mile radius of SF Bay Area studio
- Undyed, low-impact fabrics
- Low-waste cutting techniques

## Made in USA

- Grown in USA
- Milled in USA
- Sewn in USA
- Complete domestic supply chain
- Supports American farmers and workers
- Local sewing communities

## Materials

- 100% organic cotton
- Undyed fabrics
- Low-impact materials
- Heritage mill textiles

## Product Range

Men's organic cotton basics including t-shirts, underwear, boxers, and shorts with focus on simplicity and quality.

## Seed to Stitch Model

Direct collaboration with American farmers, heritage mills, and local sewers to rebuild domestic supply chains with ecological and ethical practices.
