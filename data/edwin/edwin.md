## Overview

Edwin is an innovative Japanese denim brand known for its exclusive fabrics and unique technology. The brand combines Japanese craftsmanship with contemporary European designs and fits to create high-quality selvage denim collections for men and women.

## Features

- Exclusive fabrics and unique denim technology
- High-quality selvage denim for him and her
- Combines Japanese craftsmanship with contemporary European designs and fits
- Wide range of fits and styles
- Available at select sustainable retailers

## Sustainability

- Works with organic cotton
- Uses natural dyes in production
- Focus on quality and durability to extend garment lifespan
