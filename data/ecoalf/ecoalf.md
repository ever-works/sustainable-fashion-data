## Overview

ECOALF is a pioneering Spanish sustainable fashion brand that has been creating clothing from recycled materials since 2009. The brand transforms waste including ocean plastic, recycled cotton, and recycled wool into stylish, contemporary fashion.

## Features

- Founded in 2009
- Recycled materials throughout
- Ocean plastic recovery and use
- Recycled cotton and wool
- Spanish sustainable brand
- Contemporary designs
- Circular fashion leader
- Upcycling focus
- Men's and women's collections

## Sustainability Practices

ECOALF leads the circular fashion movement by creating products from 100% recycled materials. The brand actively participates in ocean cleanup through its "Upcycling the Oceans" project, transforming marine litter into high-quality fashion and accessories.

## Product Range

Offers jackets, coats, sweaters, t-shirts, footwear, bags, and accessories for men and women, all made from recycled materials.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for innovative recycled fashion.
