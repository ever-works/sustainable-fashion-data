## Overview

Buck Mason creates classic American menswear with an emphasis on quality, domestic manufacturing, and timeless design. The brand focuses on wardrobe staples built to last, reducing the need for frequent replacement.

## Features

- Classic American menswear
- Domestic manufacturing focus
- Timeless, versatile designs
- High-quality construction
- Wardrobe staples and essentials
- Heritage-inspired aesthetic
- Durable materials
- Made in USA options
- Focus on longevity

## Sustainability Practices

Buck Mason prioritizes sustainability through timeless design and quality construction that extends garment life. The brand emphasizes domestic manufacturing when possible, supporting American workers and reducing transportation emissions while creating classic pieces designed to transcend seasonal trends.

## Product Range

Offers men's t-shirts, button-downs, jeans, chinos, outerwear, knitwear, and accessories in classic styles and neutral colors.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing reflecting quality materials and construction, with t-shirts around $35-60 and shirts $90-150.
