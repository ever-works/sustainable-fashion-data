## Overview

Sunday Monday is a home textiles and art company founded by Nisha Mirani, based in Jaipur, India. The brand is rooted in traditional block printing and hand dyeing techniques from the Bhuj region in India's Gujarat state.

## Sustainability Approach

- Human-centric and minimal production approach
- Craft-forward with traditional block printing techniques
- Production is guided by natural constraints rather than consumer demand schedules
- Only one or two small collections released per year
- Supports artisan communities in the Bhujodi crafts park, which was established after the 2001 Gujarat earthquake

## Products

- Mosaic Table Runner
- Sundial Bandana in Canyon
- Home textiles and art pieces

## Brand Philosophy

Mirani, an Anthropology studies graduate from Brown University, discovered her passion for textiles during a visit to her family in Gujarat. During the aftermath of the 2001 Gujarat earthquake, she formed personal connections with artisans in Bhujodi and decided to build upon the block printing art she discovered there. Rather than focusing on consumer demands, Sunday Monday creates through the constraints of resources and weather, launching collections when products are ready rather than adhering to traditional fashion schedules.
