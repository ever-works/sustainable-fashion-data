## Overview

The Ethical Silk Company creates all-natural, breathable sustainable silk pajamas using ethically harvested mulberry silk, also known as peace silk or ahimsa silk, which allows silkworms to complete their natural lifecycle.

## Features

- Peace silk (ahimsa silk)
- Ethically harvested mulberry silk
- No harm to silkworms
- All-natural materials
- Breathable sleepwear
- Sustainable silk
- Cruelty-free production
- Luxury silk pajamas
- Ethical sourcing

## Sustainability Practices

The Ethical Silk Company uses peace silk (ahimsa silk), which is harvested only after silkworms have naturally emerged from their cocoons, ensuring no harm comes to the insects. This ethical approach to silk production creates cruelty-free luxury sleepwear.

## Product Range

Offers silk pajamas, nightwear, and sleepwear made from ethically sourced peace silk.
