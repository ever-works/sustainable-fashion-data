## Overview

Hemp Blue is a denim brand specializing in high-hemp content jeans that maintain the comfort and style denim lovers expect. Their products are strong, breathable, biodegradable, and designed for style-conscious consumers seeking sustainable alternatives.

## Key Features

- **High Hemp Content**: Jeans with significant hemp percentages for durability
- **Comfort Meets Style**: Maintains familiar denim fit and aesthetic
- **Breathable Fabric**: Natural breathability of hemp for all-day comfort
- **Biodegradable**: Natural fiber composition means jeans decompose at end of life
- **Strong & Durable**: Hemp fiber is significantly stronger than cotton
