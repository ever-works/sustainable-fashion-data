## Overview

Harris Tapper is a women's ready-to-wear brand established in 2018 by Lauren Tapper and Sarah Harris Gould. The New Zealand label features sleek, modern basics with a pared-back minimalist approach and sustainable production.

## Features

- Established in 2018
- Founded by Lauren Tapper & Sarah Harris Gould
- New Zealand brand
- Sleek modern basics
- Minimalist aesthetic
- Pared-back approach
- Sustainable production
- Women's ready-to-wear
- Contemporary design

## Sustainability Practices

Harris Tapper combines minimalist design with sustainable production practices, creating timeless basics designed for longevity. The pared-back approach reduces excess while maintaining quality and style.

## Product Range

Offers women's modern basics and essentials with sleek, minimalist designs.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for New Zealand minimalist fashion.
