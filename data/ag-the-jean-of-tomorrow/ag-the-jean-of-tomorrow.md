## Overview

AG is a premium denim brand known for creating 'The Jean of Tomorrow' — a fully biodegradable denim line. This innovation uses a blend of hemp, Tencel, and organic cotton to create jeans that can decompose naturally at end of life, representing a holistic approach to sustainable fashion.

## Key Features

- **Fully Biodegradable Denim**: Jeans designed to break down naturally at end of life
- **Hemp, Tencel & Organic Cotton Blend**: Sustainable fiber combination for comfort and durability
- **Lifecycle Thinking**: True sustainability approach that considers the entire product lifecycle, not just production

## Materials

- Hemp: provides strength and durability
- Tencel: adds softness and drape
- Organic Cotton: ensures familiar denim feel

## Sustainability

- Biodegradable end-of-life solution reduces textile waste
- Replaces conventional resource-intensive materials with plant-based alternatives
- Considers full lifecycle impact of garments
