## Overview

Dye Lab is a small craft brand founded in 2021 by fashion consultant Rukky Ladoja. It specializes in dyeing techniques to create products that blend practicality with artisanal charm.

## Sustainability Practices

- Operates solely on a pre-order basis, minimizing overproduction and waste
- Embraces slow fashion principles
- Uses tie-dye techniques inspired by the Yoruba people of Nigeria
- Minimizes waste in production

## Design Philosophy

The brand showcases tie-dye colors and designs inspired by the Yoruba people of Nigeria, creating pieces that combine practicality with artisanal charm.
