## Overview

Vesica Piscis is a vegan footwear brand based in Spain that uses a high proportion of lower-impact materials and manufactures its shoes using renewable energy on a made-to-order basis to reduce waste.

## Features

- 100% vegan footwear
- High proportion of lower-impact materials
- Manufactured using renewable energy
- Made-to-order production to reduce waste
- Sizes 36-46
- Based in Spain
