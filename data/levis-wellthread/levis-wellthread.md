## Overview

WellThread is Levi Strauss & Co.'s sustainability-focused collection, designed to minimize environmental impact while maintaining the iconic quality and style the brand is known for.

## Key Features

- **Recycled Materials**: Utilizes recycled cotton, polyester, and other sustainable fibers to reduce reliance on virgin resources
- **Water Reduction**: Implements advanced dyeing and finishing techniques that significantly reduce water consumption
- **Circular Design**: Products designed with end-of-life in mind, supporting garment recycling and reuse programs
- **Responsible Denim**: Includes sustainable denim options with organic cotton blends and reduced-impact washes
- **Transparency**: Part of Levi's broader sustainability reporting and supply chain transparency initiatives

## Sustainability Focus

WellThread represents Levi's commitment to addressing the fashion industry's environmental challenges through:

- Reduced carbon footprint across the product lifecycle
- Minimized water waste in cotton cultivation and garment finishing
- Use of hemp and organic cotton blends
- Participation in Levi's SecondHand resale program for circularity

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing consistent with Levi's mainline products, with select WellThread pieces available at a premium.
