## Overview

Econica offers women's wear, accessories, and lingerie made of cashmere, bamboo, and organic cotton. All products are made in Canada with sustainable materials, and plus sizes are available.

## Features

- Made in Canada
- Cashmere materials
- Bamboo fabrics
- Organic cotton
- Women's wear and lingerie
- Plus sizes available
- Sustainable materials
- Canadian manufacturing
- Size-inclusive

## Sustainability Practices

Econica uses sustainable materials including cashmere, bamboo, and organic cotton, all produced in Canada. The brand's commitment to plus sizes makes sustainable fashion more accessible while supporting local manufacturing.

## Product Range

Offers women's clothing, lingerie, and accessories made from cashmere, bamboo, and organic cotton in inclusive sizing.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Canadian-made sustainable cashmere and bamboo products.
