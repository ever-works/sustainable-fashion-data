## Overview

Woolly is an apparel brand specializing in merino wool clothing designed for everyday wear, workouts, outdoor activities, and travel. Merino wool is positioned as one of the cleanest fabric options, offering natural temperature regulation and odor resistance.

## Materials Used

- Merino wool

## Certifications

- Responsible Wool Standard (RWS) covers animal welfare for sheep, responsible land management, and traceability from farm to finished product
- OEKO-TEX Standard 100
- Woolmark quality certification
- BSCI-audited factories for labor standards

XS to XXL
