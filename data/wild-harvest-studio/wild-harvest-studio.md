## Overview

Wild Harvest Studio is a women-led, small-batch clothing studio based in New England, USA. Each piece is cut, sewn, hand dyed, and made to order, celebrating the earth's natural bounty through plant-dyed organic natural fibers.

## Product Details

- **Occasions**: Casual wear, loungewear, underwear for women
- **Production**: Handmade-to-order by an independent maker
- **Dyeing**: Plant dyes (including indigo)
- **Materials**: Organic natural fibers
- **Location**: New England, USA
- **Investment Level**: $$$

## Sustainability Focus

- Uses only organic natural fibers and plant dyes
- Each piece is small batch and one of a kind
- Dedication to honoring ecological diversity
- Made-to-order production minimizes waste
