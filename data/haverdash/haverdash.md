## Overview

Haverdash is an online fashion subscription service that allows users to rotate through clothing styles via a rental model. For less than $60/month, subscribers receive 3 styles delivered to their door with free shipping and unlimited rotations throughout the month.

## Features

- Unlimited clothing rotation all month long
- 3 styles delivered per order with free shipping
- Items are wet and dry cleaned before delivery
- Pre-paid return bags included for easy exchanges
- Access to multiple brands and styles
- Virtual closet to build and manage selections
- Promotes living with less through shared fashion consumption

## Pricing

Under $60/month for unlimited rotations. Subscribers receive 3 items at a time with free shipping and returns.
