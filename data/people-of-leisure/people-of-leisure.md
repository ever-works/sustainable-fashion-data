## Overview

People Of Leisure is a slow fashion brand based in Los Angeles, California, dedicated to promoting environmental and social responsibility. They offer a stunning selection of unique casual and loungewear, including vintage-inspired band wear, hoodies, dresses, bottoms, eco-friendly outfit sets, jumpsuits, and accessories.

## Materials

- 100% organic cotton
- Deadstock fabrics purchased to divert from landfills
- Hemp incorporated into production
- Low-impact sustainable dyes to reduce water use and harmful chemicals

## Product Range

- Vintage-inspired band wear
- Hoodies and sweatshirts (e.g., Awakened Hoodie)
- Joggers (e.g., Awakened Joggers)
- Dresses and tunics (e.g., 1977 Tunic Top)
- Bottoms and jumpsuits
- Eco-friendly outfit sets
- Accessories

## Ethical & Sustainability Practices

- Garments ethically made in Los Angeles, California
- Zero Sweat Policy: living wages, benefits, breaks, and flexible hours for all employees
- Started by purchasing deadstock fabrics destined for landfills
- Low-impact sustainable dyes to minimize environmental impact
- Partners with Tree-Nation to plant trees to compensate for carbon emissions from shipping online orders
