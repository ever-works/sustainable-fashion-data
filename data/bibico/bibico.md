## Overview

Bibico makes laidback fairtrade fashion that is simple yet stylish and very wearable. Every item is crafted from hand-picked natural and organic fabrics in World Fair Trade Organisation-certified cooperatives.

## Features

- All items made from hand-picked natural and organic fabrics
- Produced in WFTO-certified cooperatives
- Only two collections per year (slow fashion approach)
- Small production runs
- Classic, timeless designs
- Size range: UK sizes 8–16

## Pricing

Mid-range (££): approximately £50 – £100
