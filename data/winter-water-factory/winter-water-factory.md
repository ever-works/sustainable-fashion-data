## Overview

Winter Water Factory is a Brooklyn-based children's clothing brand creating playful, colorful garments from 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton. The brand is known for its bold, hand-drawn prints and commitment to local USA manufacturing.

## Features

- 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton
- Made in the USA
- Hand-drawn prints and designs
- Bold, fun patterns for kids
- Brooklyn-designed
- Local manufacturing
- Soft, comfortable fabrics
- Durable construction
- Gender-neutral options

## Sustainability Practices

Winter Water Factory uses exclusively GOTS-certified organic cotton, ensuring the highest environmental and social standards throughout production. All garments are manufactured in the USA, supporting local workers and reducing transportation emissions while maintaining quality control.

## Product Range

Offers dresses, rompers, tops, pants, pajamas, and accessories for babies and children featuring unique, artistic prints.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for organic children's clothing, reflecting USA manufacturing and certified materials.
