## Overview

Etiko is Australia's pioneering Fairtrade fashion brand, established in 2005. The company creates ethically-made sneakers, clothing, and accessories with a strong commitment to fair trade principles and sustainable materials.

## Certifications

- Fairtrade certified (first Australian fashion brand)
- Fair Wear Foundation member
- GOTS certified organic cotton
- Vegan certification on select products

## Materials

- Fairtrade organic cotton
- Natural rubber from sustainable sources
- Recycled materials
- Eco-friendly canvas
- Natural dyes where possible

## Product Range

### Footwear
- Classic sneakers
- High-top sneakers
- Slip-ons
- Sandals

### Clothing
- T-shirts
- Hoodies
- Activewear
- Basics

### Accessories
- Socks
- Bags
- Beanies

## Fair Trade Commitment

- Fair wages for all workers
- Safe working conditions
- No child labor
- Community development premiums
- Support for farmer cooperatives
- Transparent supply chain

## Sustainability Features

- Organic farming practices
- Reduced chemical use
- Water conservation
- Biodegradable packaging
- Carbon offset programs

## Pricing

Affordable fair trade fashion, typically AUD $40-$120 for footwear, $25-$80 for clothing.
