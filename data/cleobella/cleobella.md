## Overview

Cleobella specializes in handmade textiles made by global craftspeople around the world. Artisans in India, Bali, and across Southeast Asia work in unison to integrate traditional techniques like block-printing and weaving into dresses, skirts, and sets.

## Sustainability

- Ethically handmade by global independent artisans
- GOTS-certified organic cotton
- Natural buttons and dyes
- OEKO-TEX certified labels
- Gives back to environmental and educational initiatives
- Plants trees

## Product Range

- Women's dresses, skirts, and sets
- Globally-inspired fits
- Vibrant and rich patterns
- Colorful coordinated sets
- Block-printed and woven textiles
- Sizes: XS – XL
