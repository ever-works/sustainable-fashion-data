## Overview

Swedish Stockings is pioneering sustainable hosiery by using recycled yarn to create stockings and socks. The brand transforms post-consumer waste into high-quality, eco-friendly legwear.

## Features

- Recycled yarn materials
- Sustainable hosiery
- Swedish brand
- Post-consumer waste transformation
- Eco-friendly tights and stockings
- Quality construction
- Stylish designs
- Reduced environmental impact
- Variety of styles and deniers

## Sustainability Practices

Swedish Stockings uses recycled yarn made from post-consumer waste to create all its products, significantly reducing the environmental impact of hosiery production. The brand has pioneered sustainable practices in an industry traditionally dominated by single-use synthetic products.

## Product Range

Offers tights, stockings, socks, and hosiery in various styles, colors, and deniers made from recycled materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable recycled hosiery.
