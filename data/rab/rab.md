## Overview

Rab is a British outdoor brand founded in 1981 on a kitchen table in Sheffield, producing technical equipment worn by professional guides and mountain enthusiasts worldwide. The brand focuses on high-performance mountaineering and hiking apparel with increasing emphasis on sustainable materials and circular design.

## Sustainability

- Uses recycled polyester in many product lines, including the Downpour Eco jacket range
- Mono-polymer construction on select jackets enables full recyclability at end of life
- Fluorocarbon-free (PFC-free) durable water repellent (DWR) treatments
- Pertex Shield recycled polyester membranes for waterproof and breathable protection

## Key Products

- **Downpour Men's/Women's Eco Jacket**: Made from recycled 50-denier polyester with a 2.5-layer Pertex Shield recycled polyester membrane. Features pit zips for ventilation, adjustable hood, and packs into a hand pocket.
- **Downpour Plus**: Enhanced version with increased durability for extended use
- **Latok Jacket**: Premium alpine shell for extreme conditions

## Certifications

- bluesign® approved materials
- PFC-free DWR on most waterproof garments

## Pricing

Mid-range to premium outdoor apparel pricing. Jackets typically range from approximately £80–£200+ depending on the model and technical specifications. Available through retailers including REI and Rab's direct online store.
