## Overview

Plum Diamonds is a jewelry brand with a focus on lab-grown diamonds as a direct alternative to mined stones, highlighting both environmental and ethical advantages.

## Sustainability Features

- Lab-grown stones reduce the need for large-scale land excavation
- Significantly lower water and energy demands compared to conventional mining
- Messaging centers on accessibility and transparency
- Clear distinctions between mined and lab-grown stones
- Fine-jewelry aesthetic for consumers seeking traditional designs with a clearer ethical profile
