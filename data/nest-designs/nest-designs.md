## Overview

Nest Designs creates maternity clothing from bamboo and organic cotton. The brand offers comfortable, sustainable pieces for pregnancy and nursing with soft, natural materials and thoughtful designs.

## Features

- Bamboo materials
- Organic cotton
- Maternity clothing
- Nursing-friendly designs
- Comfortable and soft
- Natural materials
- Sustainable production
- Pregnancy and postpartum wear
- Thoughtful functionality

## Sustainability Practices

Nest Designs uses bamboo and organic cotton, both sustainable natural fibers that are soft and gentle on sensitive skin. The brand creates nursing-friendly designs that extend the life of garments beyond pregnancy.

## Product Range

Offers maternity and nursing clothing including sleepwear, loungewear, and essentials made from bamboo and organic cotton.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for bamboo and organic cotton maternity wear.
