## Overview

On Running is a Swiss performance footwear and apparel company focused on movement — whether running, innovating, or rethinking sustainability. The brand is pushing for a greener future through fossil-free materials, circular design, and subscription-based circularity programs.

## Product Range

- Running shoes
- Everyday shoes
- Trail running shoes
- Apparel and accessories

## Sizing

- Women's 5 – 12
- Men's 7 – 15

## Price Range

- $130 – $330

## Sustainability & Circularity

- **Cyclon Subscription Program**: Subscribe to receive and return running shoes, keeping materials in the loop for recycling
- Fossil-free materials development
- Design for circularity from the outset
- Preloved/second-hand shoe marketplace on their site
- Vegan shoe options available
- Recycled materials used across collections

## Performance

- Exceptional support for joint impact reduction
- Speed-enhancing design
- CloudTec cushioning technology
- Comfortable for daily wear across various activities
- Clean, minimal aesthetic

- Swiss engineering and design
- Lightweight construction
