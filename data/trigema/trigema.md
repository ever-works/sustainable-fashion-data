## Overview

Trigema is a German clothing manufacturer keeping production within Europe to maintain high labor standards. Established in 1919, the brand creates sustainable basics with ethical European manufacturing.

## Features

- Established in 1919
- Production kept in Europe
- High labor standards
- German manufacturer
- Sustainable basics
- Ethical manufacturing
- Quality materials
- European production
- Long-standing heritage

## Sustainability Practices

Trigema maintains all production within Europe, ensuring high labor standards and fair working conditions. The brand's commitment to European manufacturing supports regional workers while maintaining quality control and reducing long-distance transportation.

## Product Range

Offers basic clothing including t-shirts, underwear, and essentials made in Europe.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for European-made basics with high labor standards.
