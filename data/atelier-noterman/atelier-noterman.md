## Overview

Atelier Noterman is a Belgian family business that has been making high-quality men's trousers for over 75 years. Known for their perfectly fitting chinos and jeans, they combine classic craftsmanship with modern comfort and style.

## Features

- Perfectly fitting chinos and jeans for men
- Classic craftsmanship combined with modern comfort
- Family-run company with over 75 years of experience
- Physical store in Kluisebergen, Belgium

## Sustainability

- Garments made in a family-run atelier in Portugal
- Fairer wages for workers
- More ethical production practices
- Transparency in manufacturing
- Focus on quality and durability
