## Overview

Manufactured in Vancouver, LONDRĖ creates minimalist swimwear from recycled plastic bottles with closed-loop production.

## Environmental Impact

- Uses 6 recycled plastic bottles per suit
- Kept over 1 million bottles from beaches
- Closed-loop water recycling system
- Minimizes waste in production and cutting
- Take-back recycling program

## Sustainability Features

- Oeko-Tex certified fabrics
- Closed-loop water recycling
- Recycling and take-back program for old suits
- Minimal waste production
- Made in Canada

## Materials

- Recycled plastic bottles
- Oeko-Tex certified fabrics
- Silky, soft, double-lined compression material

## Product Range

Elegant, minimalist swimwear with focus on fit and quality using sustainable materials.
