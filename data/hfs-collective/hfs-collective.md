## Overview

HFS Collective is a US-based vegan handbag brand that creates high-quality bags designed with organic and upcycled materials. The brand's philosophy centers around the idea of holding less and experiencing more, with a focus on hands-free designs.

## Best For
- Belt bags ideal for traveling, full days out, and everyday errands

## Product Range
- Belt bags
- Wallets
- Crossbody bags
- Wine and water bottle holders

## Ethics & Sustainability
- Vegan and cruelty-free
- PVC-free materials
- Ethically made in the US
- Organic and upcycled materials
