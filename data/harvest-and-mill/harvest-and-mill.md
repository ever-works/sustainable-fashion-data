## Overview

Harvest & Mill supports U.S. organic cotton farmers, American heritage mills, and local sewing communities to rebuild supply chains based on ecological and ethical principles.

## Product Details

- **Occasions**: Casual wear, loungewear for women and men
- **Materials**: 'Colorgrown' organic cotton grown in the USA
- **Natural Dyes**: Sourced from farmers and dye artists in Indiana and California
- **Low-Impact Dyes**: Fiber-reactive dyes made in the USA for black color
- **Clean Finish**: All designs also available in natural undyed unbleached fabric
- **Production Made**: Oakland, Berkeley, San Francisco, CA
- **Investment Level**: $$

## Sustainability Focus

- Supports organic cotton grown in the USA
- Rebuilding domestic supply chains
- Locally sewn in California
- Ecological and ethical production principles
- Low-impact dyeing processes
