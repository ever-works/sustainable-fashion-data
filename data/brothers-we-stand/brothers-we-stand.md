## Overview

Brothers We Stand is a responsible men's clothing retailer based in the UK, curated with style in mind. The brand's vision is to support customers in building a wardrobe of stylish and sustainably-made menswear that is 100% vegan.

## Features

- Curated selection of responsible men's clothing
- 100% vegan clothing range
- Online retail platform
- UK-based

## Sustainability

- All clothing is 100% vegan
- Focus on sustainably-made menswear
- Curated by Brothers We Stand for quality and responsibility
- Independently assessed by Good On You

## Sizing

Sizes available are S-XL.
