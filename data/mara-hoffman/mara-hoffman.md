## Overview

Mara Hoffman has been a leader in sustainable luxury fashion since 2015, creating bold, beautiful pieces with minimal environmental impact.

## Sustainability Features

- Climate-neutral operations
- Uses high proportion of eco-friendly materials
- Full Circle circularity program for garment take-back
- Digital printing technology to reduce water use
- Compostable packaging
- Transparent sustainability practices

## Materials

- ECONYL® (regenerated nylon from waste)
- REPREVE® (polyester from recycled plastic)
- Hemp
- Organic cotton
- Linen
- Ethical alpaca wool
- Modal from Lenzing Group

## Product Range

Swimwear, ready-to-wear clothing, and dresses known for bold prints and vibrant colors.

## Circularity

The Full Circle program accepts worn Mara Hoffman garments for resale or recycling, extending product life and reducing waste.
