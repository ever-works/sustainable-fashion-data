## Overview

Rawganique is a pioneer in the sustainable fashion space, having transitioned completely from cotton to 100% organic hemp across its entire product range including tees, sweaters, and home goods. The brand embodies a raw, authentic ethos with chemical-free, earth-first clothing.

## Key Features

- **100% Organic Hemp**: Complete product line made from organic hemp only, no cotton blends
- **Full Transition from Cotton**: Eliminated cotton entirely in favor of hemp
- **Chemical-Free Products**: No synthetic chemicals used in fiber processing
- **Diverse Range**: Includes tees, sweaters, and home goods
- **Earth-First Philosophy**: Environmental impact drives all production decisions

## Materials

100% certified organic hemp across all product categories — no cotton, no synthetic blends.

## Sustainability

- Eliminates water-guzzling cotton entirely
- Organic hemp cultivation requires no synthetic pesticides
- Chemical-free processing protects workers and ecosystems
