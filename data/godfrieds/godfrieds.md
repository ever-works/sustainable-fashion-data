## Overview

Godfrieds is a Belgian denim brand that specializes in both standard and tailor-made jeans. The brand is known for its exceptional materials and craftsmanship, offering a personal fitting experience at their atelier.

## Features

- Standard jeans and custom tailormade options
- Denim jackets and dungarees available
- 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton sourced from Japan
- Authentic selvedge denim made using traditional methods
- Personal consultation and fitting at the atelier in Dentergem

## Sustainability

- Uses 100% GOTS-labelled organic cotton
- Selvedge denim produced in authentic, genuine ways
- Made-to-order approach reduces overproduction
- Local atelier with expert guidance for perfect fit
