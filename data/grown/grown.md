## Overview

Grown is an Irish company committed to using fully organic, biodegradable and recyclable products in their clothing.

## Materials

- Tencel
- Organic cotton

## Environmental Impact

- Plants a native Irish tree for every t-shirt sold
- Donates 1% of annual sales to environmental protection
- Planted 150 indigenous Irish trees at Ballinlough Castle in collaboration with Body & Soul festival for Earth Day

## Product Range

- T-shirts

## Sizing

- XS to XXL
