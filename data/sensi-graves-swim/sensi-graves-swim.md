## Overview

Sensi Graves Swim is a Climate Neutral Certified company and 1% for the Planet member that makes eco-friendly women's swimwear from recycled fabrics. These eco-friendly surf bikinis are designed for women by professional kiteboarder Sensi Graves.

## Materials

- Recycled fibers from Repreve
- Recycled fibers from Econyl
- Packaged in compostable bags

## Ethical Practices

- Climate Neutral Certified
- 1% for the Planet member
- Factory partners abide by the fair labor practices outlined by the Fair Labor Standards Act
