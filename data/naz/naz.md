## Overview

Naz is a Portuguese brand producing clothing from natural fibres including organic cotton and Lyocell in Portuguese factories committed to fair employee treatment.

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Lyocell (sustainably sourced wood pulp fibers)
- Natural fiber focus

## Production

- Made in Portuguese factories
- Fair employee treatment
- European manufacturing reduces transportation emissions

## Product Range

Women's contemporary clothing made from natural, sustainable materials.
