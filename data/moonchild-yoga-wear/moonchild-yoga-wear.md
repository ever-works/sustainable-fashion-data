## Overview

Moonchild Yoga Wear is a Danish-owned athleisure brand founded in 2015 by Louise Wang Nielsen. The brand produces collections of yoga and loungewear inspired by Scandinavian nature, designed for women, ethically and responsibly made from carefully selected fabrics and sustainable materials.

## Ethics & Sustainability

- Organic cotton
- Q-NOVA regenerated fibers
- Recycled polyester from plastic waste
- STEP BY OEKO-TEX Certification at maximum evaluation level
- Focus on sustainable production practices and social responsibility
- Donates part of proceeds to charities including Action Child Aid and Women for Women International

## Product Range

- Yoga clothing
- Yoga mats (natural rubber with eco-polyurethane top surface)
- Bags
- Straps
- Blocks
- Pillows
- Bolsters
