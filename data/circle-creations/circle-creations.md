## Overview

Circle Creations is an ethical fashion brand that blends hemp with Tencel and organic cotton to create capsule basics. Their focus is on traceable, plant-based fabrics with minimal environmental impact.

## Key Features

- **Hemp-Tencel-Organic Cotton Blends**: Combines three sustainable fibers for optimal comfort and durability
- **Ethical Production**: Garments produced with fair labor and ethical practices
- **Capsule Basics**: Focused collection of wardrobe essentials
- **Traceable Supply Chain**: Full transparency in material sourcing and production
- **Plant-Based Fabrics**: Only plant-derived textiles used in products

## Materials

- Hemp: strongest natural fiber
- Tencel: soft, sustainably-produced lyocell from wood pulp
- Organic cotton: chemical-free cotton cultivation

## Sustainability

- Traceable supply chain ensures ethical and environmental standards
- Plant-based fabrics minimize synthetic textile pollution
- Minimal environmental impact across production lifecycle
