## Overview

Rifó is an Italian clothing company that manufactures garments in Italy from recycled clothing materials. The brand also operates a take-back program, allowing customers in Italy to donate worn-out jeans and wool jumpers via NaturaSi supermarkets for recycling into new garments.

## Sustainability Practices

- Garments made in Italy from recycled clothing
- Take-back program: customers can donate worn-out jeans and wool jumpers through NaturaSi supermarkets
- Local Italian production to reduce transportation emissions
- Recycled material processing

## Sizing

- Women: XS – XL
- Men: S – XL

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Italian-made recycled fashion.
