## Overview

Founded in 2014, Vissla redefines surf culture with focus on environmental consciousness and innovative materials.

## Sustainability Features

- Boardshorts from upcycled coconut fibers
- Wetsuits from limestone-based neoprene
- Eco-friendly materials throughout
- Partnership with Surfrider Foundation
- Creators & Innovators Upcycle Contest
- Community waste reduction initiatives

## Materials

- Upcycled coconut fibers
- Limestone-based neoprene
- Eco-friendly materials
- Sustainable surfwear fabrics

## Product Range

Eco surf wetsuits, boardshorts, and sustainable surfwear designed for warmth, flex, and all-season performance.

## Community Engagement

- Creators & Innovators Upcycle Contest
- Surfrider Foundation partnership
- Promotes creativity and waste reduction
- Beach cleanups and environmental initiatives

## Innovation

Pioneers sustainable materials in surf industry, proving eco-friendly can deliver high performance.
