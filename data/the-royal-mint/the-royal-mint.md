## Overview

The Royal Mint has been making coins for the U.K. for more than a thousand years and has now launched its first-ever luxury jewelry line.

## Sustainability Features

- Jewelry made from gold sourced entirely from upcycled electronic waste
- First jewelry collection made from e-waste-sourced gold (the 886 Collection)
- Named after the Royal Mint's establishment date (886 AD)
- Designed by jewelry designer Dominic Jones

## Craftsmanship

- Pieces crafted from solid gold or silver using ancient techniques from coin and medal-making
- Precious metals are struck, forged, and pulled rather than cast
- This technique increases the density of the metal and makes it 30 percent stronger
