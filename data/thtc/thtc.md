## Overview

THTC (True Hemp Trading Company) is a UK-based pioneer in organic hemp clothing, specializing in t-shirts, hoodies, socks, caps, and other streetwear essentials.

## Features

- Organic hemp t-shirts, hoodies, socks, and caps
- Streetwear collection made from sustainable hemp fabrics
- White-label production ranges for charities, NGOs, and other organisations
- Based in the United Kingdom
- Hemp is a low-impact, fast-growing crop requiring minimal water and no pesticides

## Sustainability

- Uses organic hemp as primary material
- Hemp cultivation has a low environmental footprint
- Produces white-label ranges for charitable and non-profit organisations
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Hemp Benefits

- Requires minimal water compared to cotton
- Grows without synthetic pesticides
- Naturally enriches soil health
- Durable, breathable, and antibacterial fibers
- One of the most sustainable textile crops available

Affordable pricing for organic hemp streetwear.
