## Overview

Bodily is a maternity and nursing intimates brand focused on supporting women through every stage of motherhood — from pregnancy to postpartum recovery and beyond. Beyond products, the brand actively supports women's well-being with initiatives like their Pregnancy Loss Support Hub and gives back to causes that uplift mothers.

## Features

- **OEKO-TEX Certified Fabrics**: Ultra-soft materials free from harmful chemicals
- **Product Range**: Nursing tanks, bras, underwear, multi-packs, and curated boxes
- **Dual-Purpose Design**: Bras are soft enough for sleep and supportive enough for all-day wear
- **Nursing Line**: Postpartum collection of nursing bras suitable for the breastfeeding journey
- **Social Mission**: Commitment to supporting women through pregnancy, postpartum, and beyond; includes Pregnancy Loss Support Hub

## Sizing

- Size Range: 32A – 42G/46C
- Recommendation: Size up in bras to current pregnancy size
