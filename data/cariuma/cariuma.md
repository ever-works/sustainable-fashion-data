## Overview

Cariuma creates sustainable sneakers without compromising on style, comfort, or quality.

## Sustainability Features

- Carbon-neutral operations
- Plants two trees for every pair sold
- Low-impact dyes
- Sustainable material sourcing
- Ethical manufacturing in Brazil
- Transparent production process

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Bamboo
- Recycled PET
- Leather Working Group-certified leather and suede
- Natural rubber
- Cork insoles

## Product Range

Sneakers in various styles including low-tops, high-tops, and slip-ons for men and women.

## Environmental Commitment

Every pair of sneakers contributes to reforestation efforts, with over 2 million trees planted in partnership with environmental organizations.
