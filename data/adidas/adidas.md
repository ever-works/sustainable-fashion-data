## Overview

adidas is a global sportswear leader making significant sustainability commitments and achieving measurable environmental improvements.

## Sustainability Achievements

- 10% absolute emissions reduction by 2026
- 100% recycled polyester across key product lines by 2026
- 99% recycled polyester used in products as of 2024
- Uses 100% third-party certified cotton since 2018
- 100% of strategic raw materials certified by 2026

## Certifications & Partnerships

- Founding member of Leather Working Group
- Founding member of Better Cotton Initiative
- Founding member of Fair Labour Association
- Commitment to ecosystem preservation
- Water resource protection standards

## Materials

- Recycled polyester
- Third-party certified cotton
- Certified sustainable raw materials
- Leather from LWG sources

## Product Range

Sportswear, footwear, and athletic equipment for multiple sports and activities.

## Environmental Goals

By 2026, ensures 100% of strategic raw materials are certified to standards guaranteeing ecosystem and water resource preservation.
