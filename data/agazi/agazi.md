## Agazi Vegan Shoes

Agazi creates vegan shoes that are chic and contemporary, fusing comfort and style with sustainability. The company was founded by former lawyer Agnieszka Ziolek with a desire to revolutionize the shoe industry with an offering that was wearable, versatile, and sustainable.

## Materials

Agazi works with innovative plant-based materials:

**Apple Leather**: Apple skin is made with fiber from apple juice production waste through a process patented by an Italian company that's eco-friendly and uses no toxic substances.

**Plant Leathers**: Made from grapes, apples and cactus, as well as a polyurethane consisting of 69% bio-based materials.

**Hemp Fabric**: Hypoallergenic, breathable, mold-resistant, absorbs moisture effectively, and is Oeko Tex certified and made in Europe.

**Sustainable Linings & Soles**: The linings of the shoes are made with sustainably sourced bamboo or organic cotton, while the soles are made from natural cork.

## Production & Ethics

Agazi makes vegan shoes handcrafted in its own atelier in Poland, with design and production taking place under one roof. The brand is rated "Great" by Good On You.

## Product Range

Offers sneakers, sandals, heels, flats, boots, and dress shoes for both men and women, all using non-animal-derived materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for handcrafted vegan footwear.
