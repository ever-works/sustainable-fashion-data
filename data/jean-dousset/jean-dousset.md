## Overview

Jean Dousset is a fine jewelry brand founded by the great-great-grandson of Cartier, offering modern jewelry designs featuring lab-grown diamonds and upcycled gold.

## Sustainability Features

- Lab-grown diamonds as a sustainable alternative to mined diamonds
- Upcycled gold in jewelry settings
- Commitment to an ethical supply chain
- More affordable than traditional luxury jewelry while maintaining designer quality
- Pieces designed to be handed down for generations
