## Overview

TALI & TASI (formerly Tasi Travels) is an Australian travelwear brand designed for adventures. The brand boasts a stunning collection of handmade garments in lower-impact materials that are made-to-order, allowing customers to feel good knowing they are leaving a light footprint.

## Key Features

- Travelwear designed for adventure
- Handmade garments
- Made-to-order production model
- Lower-impact materials
- Sizes available in XS/S, S/M, and M/L

## Sustainability Practices

- Made-to-order production eliminates overproduction and excess inventory
- Uses lower-impact materials
- Handmade production approach
- Designed to minimize environmental footprint

## Size Range

- XS/S, S/M, and M/L
