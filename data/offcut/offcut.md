## Overview

Offcut is a New Zealand brand making caps from scraps, taking clean textile waste and transforming it into limited-run hats. The brand creates unique accessories while eliminating fabric waste from the fashion industry.

## Features

- Made from textile scraps
- Clean fabric waste use
- Limited-run production
- New Zealand brand
- Caps and hats
- Zero new fabric
- Unique designs
- Waste elimination
- Sustainable accessories

## Sustainability Practices

Offcut gives new life to clean textile waste that would otherwise be discarded, transforming scraps into stylish caps. Each limited-run collection uses different waste materials, creating unique pieces while eliminating fabric waste.

## Product Range

Offers caps and hats made entirely from textile scraps in limited-run collections.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for upcycled textile waste hats.
