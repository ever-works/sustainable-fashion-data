## Overview

Devold is a heritage Norwegian brand established in 1853, with over 165 years of experience in creating high-quality woolen clothing. The brand combines traditional Norwegian craftsmanship with modern sustainability practices.

## Heritage & History

Founded in 1853, Devold has been a trusted name in Norwegian outdoor clothing for over a century and a half. The brand has equipped numerous polar expeditions and represents the pinnacle of wool expertise.
