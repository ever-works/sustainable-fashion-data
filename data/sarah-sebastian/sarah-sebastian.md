## Overview

Sarah & Sebastian are pioneers of sustainable fine jewellery, producing ultra-fine luxury pieces that are entirely made to order in small quantities, eliminating waste in the manufacturing process.

## Sustainability Practices

- **Small-batch production**: All pieces are made to order, resulting in zero to no waste
- **RJC certified**: Works with suppliers certified by the Responsible Jewellery Council, ensuring compliance with ethical, human rights, social and environmental standards
- **Local suppliers**: Partners primarily with local Australian suppliers for maximum transparency
- **Repair program**: Offers repairs to extend the life of each piece
- **Recycling program**: Customers can exchange no-longer-worn pieces for store credit
- **Ocean conservation**: Launched 'The Xanthe Project' in 2023, a philanthropic initiative dedicated to ocean conservation

## Product Range

Ultra-fine luxury jewellery spanning rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets.
