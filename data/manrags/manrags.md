## Overview

ManRags is an Australian brand shaking up the sock and underwear market with a pioneering subscription model and commitment to textile recycling.

## Sustainability Features

- Textile recycling program to prevent socks from ending in landfill
- Saved over 550,000 socks from becoming landfill waste
- Subscription service reduces packaging waste and overconsumption
- Essentials delivered monthly to reduce individual shopping trips

## Products

- Quality socks and underwear available in various sizes
- Subscription packages with chosen length
- Available for adults and smaller feet

## How It Works

- Choose a package of essentials and subscription length
- Products are sent automatically every month
- Used items can be returned for recycling through their textile recycling movement
