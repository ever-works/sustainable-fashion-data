## Overview

Free Label makes all clothing in Canada with 100% woman-owned manufacturers in Toronto and Vancouver. The inclusive sustainable brand offers sizes XS through 5X, creating pieces in small batches using sustainable materials in local factories.

## Features

- 100% woman-owned manufacturers
- Made in Toronto and Vancouver
- Sizes XS through 5X
- Small batch production
- Sustainable materials
- Local Canadian factories
- Zero stock waste
- Inclusive sizing
- Women's empowerment focus

## Sustainability Practices

Free Label produces clothing in small batches to eliminate waste and overproduction. Working exclusively with woman-owned Canadian factories ensures ethical labor practices while supporting female entrepreneurship and local manufacturing.

## Product Range

Offers women's clothing in sizes XS-5X including dresses, tops, bottoms, and outerwear.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Canadian-made, size-inclusive sustainable fashion.
