## Overview

Brøgger is a fashion label rooted in sustainability through design longevity and responsible material selection. The brand actively promotes slow fashion principles to counter industry overconsumption.

## Key Features

- Designs focused explicitly on garment longevity and timeless wearability
- Uses organic, recycled, and deadstock materials to minimise environmental impact
- Actively discourages overconsumption through deliberate design choices
- Embraces slow fashion methodology across its collections
- Minimalist aesthetic suited for long-term wardrobe integration

## Pricing

Contemporary pricing accessible to conscious consumers seeking long-lasting, quality pieces.
