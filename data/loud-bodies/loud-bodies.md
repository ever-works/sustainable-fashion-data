## Overview

Loud Bodies is a revolutionary size-inclusive sustainable brand offering sizing from XXS through 10XL. For customers with atypical body types outside this range, the brand provides free customization, ensuring everyone can access sustainable fashion.

## Features

- Sizing from XXS to 10XL
- Free customization for atypical bodies
- Certified sustainable fabrics
- Organic cotton and linen
- Tencel and Ecovero materials
- Deadstock fabrics from luxury brands
- True size inclusivity
- Custom fit services
- Body-positive values

## Sustainability Practices

Loud Bodies uses certified sustainable fabrics including organic cotton, linen, Tencel, and Ecovero. The brand also incorporates deadstock fabrics from luxury brands, giving new life to excess materials. By offering free customization, Loud Bodies ensures accessibility while minimizing returns and waste.

## Product Range

Offers dresses, tops, bottoms, and essentials designed to fit and flatter bodies of all sizes and proportions.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing reflecting custom services and certified sustainable materials.
