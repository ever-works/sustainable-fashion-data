## Overview

Founded in 1988 by Mark Bloom, Komodo is the original ethical fashion brand inspired by backpacking trips across Asia.

## Sustainability Achievements

- Ethical Accreditation achieved
- Scored 100 on The Good Shopping Guide's GSG Ethical score
- Top of Fashion Retailers Ethical Comparison Table
- All cotton is organic and GOTS certified
- Member of 1% for the Planet

## Materials

- Organic GOTS-certified cotton
- Premium natural and eco fibers
- Innovative green PU coating
- Recycled PET from plastic bottles
- Sustainable fabrics exclusively

## Innovation

- Integrated advanced online sizing technology
- Reduces returns (projected £4.4bn cost by 2026)
- Further sustainability commitment

## Product Range

Ethical fashion for free-spirited individuals including vegan clothing and shoes.

## Heritage

Nearly 40 years as pioneer of ethical fashion, maintaining commitment to sustainability since inception.
