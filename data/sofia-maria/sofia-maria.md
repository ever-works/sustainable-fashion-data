## Overview

Sofia Maria is a sustainable artisan jeweller whose fine jewellery line is inspired by organic lines, natural textures, and imperfect forms, embodying the Japanese philosophy of wabi-sabi.

## Sustainability Practices

- **Locally handmade**: All pieces are handmade by Maria herself in Sydney, Australia
- **Recycled metals**: Uses 100% recycled metals whenever possible
- **Local sourcing**: Materials are locally sourced and recycled in Australia
- **Scrap metal reuse**: Reuses scrap metals from the production process
- **On-shore production**: Entire production is based in Australia

## Design Philosophy

The brand celebrates the imperfect, impermanent, and incomplete through organic, nature-inspired forms.
