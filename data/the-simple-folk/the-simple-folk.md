## Overview

Founded by two mothers who are best friends, The Simple Folk creates organic essentials for families with conscious choices at the heart.

## Sustainability Features

- 100% organic materials
- OEKO-TEX-certified dyes
- Eco-friendly carbon-brushing softening techniques
- Classic pieces that transcend trends
- Sustainable production
- Ethical manufacturing

## Materials

- 100% organic materials
- OEKO-TEX-certified dyes
- Natural, safe fabrics

## Product Range

Organic, ethical essentials for women, babies, and children with minimalist aesthetic and timeless design.

## Philosophy

Believes in power of conscious choices, creating pieces as statements of sustainability, ethical production, and love for the planet.

## Design Approach

Creates classic, minimalist pieces designed to last and transcend seasonal trends, encouraging long-term use and reduced consumption.
