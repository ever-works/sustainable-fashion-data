## Overview

In the Soulshine is an Australian brand that produces vegan t-shirts with a focus on ethical manufacturing practices. Their tees are made with vegan ink and assembled in Bali.

## Features

- Vegan inks used for printing
- Factory in Bali where salaries are three to five times more than the average for a tailor
- Ethical and transparent labor practices
- Sustainable production methods
- Australian brand with international manufacturing
