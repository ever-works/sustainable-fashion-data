## Overview

Vrai is a fine jewelry brand built around lab-grown diamonds produced using renewable energy, positioning itself as one of the more transparent players in the fine jewelry space.

## Sustainability Features

- Diamonds grown by Diamond Foundry (backed by Leonardo DiCaprio), a San Francisco-based company using plasma reactors powered by certified renewable energy
- Avoids environmental and human rights concerns tied to traditional diamond mining
- Supply chain traceability
- Made-to-order production to limit excess inventory and waste
- Settings crafted using recycled gold and platinum
- Publishes clear information about sourcing and production standards
