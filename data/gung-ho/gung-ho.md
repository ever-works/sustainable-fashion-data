## Overview

Gung Ho Design is a London-based brand creating garments from fabric like Tencel, which is substantially biodegradable, and organic cotton. Some garments are made in Fair Wear certified factories in India, while others are made in London.

## Sustainability Practices

- Uses Tencel (substantially biodegradable) and organic cotton
- Some garments made in Fair Wear certified factories in India
- Others made in London, UK
- To minimize waste, offers only two sizes on some garments
- Designed to reduce textile waste

## Sizing

- XS – L

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable London-made fashion.
