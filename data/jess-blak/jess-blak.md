## Overview

Brisbane-based Jess Blak creates jewellery inspired by textures found in ruins and shipwrecks, capturing artefacts that recall stories lost and the mystery of times past, with ethicality at the fore of all operations.

## Sustainability Practices

- **Local manufacturing**: Production based in Australia
- **Responsibly sourced materials**: All gems and metals are recycled, lab-grown, or responsibly sourced
- **Conflict-free sourcing**: Ensures materials do not support or benefit unlawful conflict or contribute to human rights abuses
- **Small business partnerships**: Works with local and small businesses to plate and cast

## Design Inspiration

Textures found in ruins and shipwrecks, inspired by artefacts that recall historical stories and mystery.
