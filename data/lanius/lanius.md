## Overview

Lanius is a German company making ethical sustainable clothing from natural fibres including organic cotton and wool.

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Organic wool
- Some pieces also include non-animal products (vegan options available)

## Production

- Entire production of some garments carries GOTS organic certification
- Factories hold various social standard certificates: GOTS, SA8000, WRAP, BSCI Code of Conduct, Fair Wear Foundation
- Small family businesses are exceptions but are visited regularly to ensure social standards

## Sizing

- Women: 34-44
