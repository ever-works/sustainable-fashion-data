## Overview

Vitamin A is a California-based sustainable swimwear brand that pioneered eco-luxury in the swimwear industry. The brand creates stylish bikinis, one-pieces, and cover-ups using innovative recycled materials while maintaining local manufacturing.

## Features

- EcoLux fabric made from recycled nylon, Lycra, and polyester
- Most swimwear manufactured in California
- Eco-friendly luxury designs
- Flattering, contemporary silhouettes
- Sustainable fabric innovation
- Local production supporting American workers
- High-quality construction
- Resort wear and cover-ups
- Mix-and-match separates

## Sustainability Practices

Vitamin A manufactures most of its swimwear in California, reducing transportation emissions and supporting local workers. The brand's signature EcoLux blend uses recycled nylon, Lycra, and polyester to create luxurious swimwear that performs well while reducing environmental impact. Each piece combines sustainability with sophisticated California style.

## Pricing

Premium sustainable swimwear with bikini tops starting around $78-98 and bottoms around $68-88.
