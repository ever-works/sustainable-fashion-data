## Overview

Prende Pants was founded by TQ Evans, who lost her confidence after delivering three babies and dealing with diastasis recti. She designed leggings to help women feel like themselves again during post-pregnancy recovery. The brand claims to be the first post-pregnancy, high-waist, eco-friendly leggings for women.

## Features

- Adjustable four-stage compression system that can be fitted to your waist for extra core support
- High-waist design for a smoother midsection
- Designed specifically for post-pregnancy recovery and the transition back to pre-pregnancy clothes
- Made in the United States
- Shipping boxes made from 95% post-consumer content and fully recyclable

## Materials

- 76% recycled polyester
- 24% spandex

The use of recycled polyester ensures these materials get a second use instead of winding up in a landfill.
