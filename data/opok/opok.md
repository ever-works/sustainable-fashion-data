## Overview

Opok is a sustainable men's basics brand that produces essential wardrobe staples from certified organic cotton. The brand focuses on the fundamentals of a quality menswear wardrobe — t-shirts, underwear, socks, and other everyday pieces — made with responsible materials and ethical manufacturing.

## Features

- Organic cotton menswear basics
- Essential wardrobe staples including t-shirts and underwear
- Certified sustainable materials
- Ethical production practices
- Focus on comfort and durability
- Minimalist designs for versatility

## Materials

- GOTS-certified organic cotton
- Low-impact dyes
- Eco-friendly packaging
