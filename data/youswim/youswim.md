## Overview

Youswim challenges traditional swimwear sizing by offering just three one-piece styles (Aplomb, Eva, Poise) and six separates styles — each designed to fit an incredibly wide range of body types across seven sizes (US 2–24). The concept eliminates the need for precise sizing while maintaining a flattering, form-fitting look.

## Features

- **Adaptive sizing**: Each style fits a broad size range (US 2–24) without compromising on fit or appearance
- **European-sourced materials**: OEKO-TEX certified nylon and elastane woven ethically in England
- **Plastic Negative certified**: The brand offsets more plastic than it produces
- **Vibrant color options**: Available in a wide palette of bold, eye-catching colors
- **Single pricing**: One price across all pieces regardless of style
- **Versatile one-piece styles**: Three core silhouettes (Aplomb, Eva, Poise) designed for different preferences
- **FSC certified**: Packaging and materials follow responsible sourcing standards
- **Eco-friendly packaging**: Shipped in sustainable, minimal packaging

## Sustainability
