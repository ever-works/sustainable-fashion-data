## Overview

Studio Sol (formerly Fabrik) is a Brisbane-based fashion label created by designer Sarah Hardie with a clear set of priorities: ethical, sustainable, and slow. The brand creates clothing designed with intention and made to last.

## Features

- Elevated basics, sundresses, cotton shirts, and everyday staples
- Brisbane-based design
- Designed for long-term wear, year after year

## Sustainability Practices

- 100% natural fabrics used
- Clothing designed with intention and made to last
- Ethical and sustainable production
- Slow fashion philosophy

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing reflecting quality natural fabrics and Australian design.
