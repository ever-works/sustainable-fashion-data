## Overview

ESSĒN was founded in 2016 by Marre Muijs as a response to a fashion cycle that overproduces more than it carefully crafts, chases trends more than it determines classics, and wastes more than it sustains. The Australian shoes and accessories brand operates on a slow fashion model.

## Key Features

- Shoes and accessories
- Single, permanent collection (eschews the fashion calendar)
- Limited production runs
- Low-waste cutting techniques
- Focus on carefully crafted classics over trend-chasing

## Sustainability Practices

- Limits production runs to avoid overproduction
- Uses low-waste cutting techniques
- Single permanent collection approach eliminates seasonal waste
- Rejects the traditional fashion calendar
- Focuses on quality craftsmanship over quantity
