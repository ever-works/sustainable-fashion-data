## Overview

OSOM Dreams is a sustainable fashion brand with a mission to reduce waste and pollution through their innovative clothing recycling program. They offer recycled apparel through a unique subscription model.

## Recycling Program

- Yearly subscription program for recycled attire
- Supported by Clothes Cycle
- Customers fill 3 boxes with old clothes and use a pre-paid label to return them
- Returned garments are recycled into new clothing items

## Sustainability Practices

- Focus on recycled materials and reducing textile waste
- Pollition reduction through circular processes
- Subscription model designed to minimize overproduction
- Partnership with Clothes Cycle for garment collection and processing

## Pricing

Yearly subscription model for recycled clothing; pricing available on their website.
