## Overview

Founded by Maggie Hewitt, Maggie Marilyn is a New Zealand brand pioneering slow, conscious fashion. The B-Corp certified label is one of the most prominent brands upholding continuous sustainability standards.

## Features

- B-Corp certified
- Founded by Maggie Hewitt
- New Zealand brand
- Slow fashion pioneer
- Continuous sustainability standards
- Ethical production
- Transparent practices
- Contemporary women's fashion
- Quality craftsmanship

## Sustainability Practices

Maggie Marilyn maintains B-Corp certification while pioneering slow fashion principles in New Zealand. The brand upholds rigorous, continuous sustainability standards throughout production and maintains complete transparency in its supply chain.

## Product Range

Offers contemporary women's fashion including dresses, separates, knitwear, and tailoring with sustainable practices.
