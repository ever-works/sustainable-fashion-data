## Overview

pinqponq is a German brand that makes stylish and functional bags out of recycled plastic. The brand sources materials under the Fair Wear Foundation Code of Conduct and traces all of its supply chain. It is also 100% vegan.

## Features

- Stylish and functional bags from recycled plastic
- 100% vegan
- Fair Wear Foundation Code of Conduct sourcing
- Full supply chain traceability
- Based in Germany
