## Overview

Ccilu uses innovative technology to create eco-friendly shoes by recycling commonly discarded materials. The brand is known for introducing novel sustainable materials into footwear production.

## Features

- XpreSole: first shoe made using recycled spent coffee grounds
- Five cups' worth of coffee grounds recycled per pair of shoes
- Recycles plastic bottles into footwear
- Low-emission manufacturing processes
- Focus on reducing waste through innovative material use
