## Overview

Pixie Mood has been creating vegan accessories since 2010, establishing itself as a leader in cruelty-free fashion. The Canadian brand focuses on functional, stylish bags made from sustainable vegan leather alternatives.

## Features

- Recycled vegan leather materials
- Solvent-free production processes
- Completely animal-free
- Functional, practical designs
- Contemporary aesthetic
- Wide range of styles
- Affordable luxury
- Quality construction
- Established in 2010

## Sustainability Practices

Pixie Mood uses recycled materials and solvent-free vegan leather in their production, reducing both animal harm and environmental pollution. The brand has maintained its commitment to cruelty-free fashion since its founding in 2010, continuously improving its sustainable material sourcing and production methods.

## Product Range

Offers an extensive collection including backpacks, totes, crossbody bags, wallets, clutches, and travel accessories in various colors and styles.

## Pricing

Affordable to mid-range pricing, making vegan accessories accessible to a wider audience.
