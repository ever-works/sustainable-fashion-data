## Overview

Established in 2012, Rothy's creates sustainable, comfortable footwear from recycled plastic bottles, diverting waste from landfills and oceans.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses recycled plastic bottles (11 per pair)
- Diverts plastic from landfills and oceans
- 3D knit technology reduces waste
- Machine washable for longevity
- Vegan leather options
- Uses bio-based components

## Innovation

- Proprietary 3D knitting technology
- Spins recycled plastic bottles into signature thread
- Zero-waste knitting process
- Comfortable, breathable construction

## Materials

- Recycled water bottles
- Ocean plastic
- Vegan leather
- Wool
- Bio-based components

## Product Range

Flats, sneakers, loafers, and bags known for classic styles and comfort. Machine washable for easy care and extended life.
