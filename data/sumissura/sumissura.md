## Overview

Sumissura is a sustainable clothing brand focused on custom, made-to-measure garments and shoes produced only after purchase.

## Sustainability Practices

- Produces custom clothing and shoes to order, eliminating clothing stock and reducing waste
- Known tailors and artisans with fair working conditions throughout the supply chain
- AI-generated lookbook imagery combined with real-life photography, reducing emissions by 91%
- No size restrictions due to made-to-measure approach, accommodating plus-size and petite customers

## Products

- Custom shoes and clothing
- Business attire
- Custom jeans
- Self-designed garments

## Key Features

- Made-to-measure tailoring to individual body measurements
- Customer design capability for garments
- Fusion of traditional tailoring with modern technology
- Emphasis on inclusivity and self-expression through self-designed clothing
