## Overview

Poplinen is a sustainable fashion brand creating versatile, comfortable clothing with a focus on eco-friendly materials and zero-waste practices. The brand offers inclusive sizing and timeless designs.

## Materials

- Organic cotton (GOTS certified)
- Recycled cotton
- Tencel (lyocell)
- Linen
- Hemp
- Natural fiber blends

## Sustainability Practices

- Low-waste cutting techniques
- Fabric scraps upcycled into baby onesies
- Minimal fabric waste
- Eco-friendly dyes
- Sustainable packaging
- Made-to-order options available

## Size Inclusivity

Offering sizes XS through 3X, Poplinen is committed to making sustainable fashion accessible to more body types.

## Product Range

- Dresses and jumpsuits
- Tops and blouses
- Pants and shorts
- Loungewear
- Baby onesies (made from fabric scraps)

Timeless, versatile pieces designed for everyday wear.

## Production

- Ethical manufacturing
- Fair labor practices
- Small batch production
- Quality over quantity focus

## Features

- Easy-care fabrics
- Comfortable, flattering fits
- Versatile, mix-and-match designs
- Travel-friendly materials

## Pricing

Mid-range sustainable fashion, typically $60-$150 per item.
