## Overview

Originally created for surfers, Finisterre now offers durable outdoor fashion designed for performance and longevity. As a Certified B Corporation, the British brand meets the highest environmental and ethical production standards.

## Features

- Certified B Corporation
- Ethical supply chain
- Organic materials throughout
- Designed for durability
- Performance outdoor wear
- Surf-inspired designs
- British heritage brand
- Multiple UK locations
- Sustainable innovation

## Sustainability Practices

Finisterre maintains B Corp certification, demonstrating exceptional commitment to environmental and social responsibility. The brand uses organic materials, maintains transparent and ethical supply chains, and designs products for long-term durability rather than seasonal trends.

## Product Range

Offers jackets, coats, base layers, surf wear, swimwear, and outdoor essentials for men and women, all designed for coastal and outdoor activities.

## Locations

Stores in London, Bristol, Edinburgh, and other UK locations.

## Pricing

Premium pricing reflecting sustainable materials and ethical production.
