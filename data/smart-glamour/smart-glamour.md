## Overview

Smart Glamour creates handmade-to-order clothing in an incredible size range from XXS to 15XL, all produced in the United States. The brand combines radical size inclusivity with zero waste practices.

## Size Inclusivity

- **XXS-15XL**: Industry-leading size range
- **Made-to-Order**: Custom fit for every body
- **No Size Limits**: Truly inclusive sizing
- **Educational Focus**: Teaching inclusive design practices

## Zero Waste Production

- **Minimal Waste**: Thoughtful pattern cutting reduces scrap
- **FabScrap Partnership**: Donates unusable scraps to textile recycling nonprofit
- **Locally-Sourced Fabrics**: Cotton-based materials from local suppliers
- **US Production**: Made in America with fair labor practices

## Made-to-Order Model

- **Custom Creation**: Each piece made specifically for customer
- **No Overstock**: Produces only what's ordered
- **Perfect Fit**: Tailored to individual measurements
- **Quality Focus**: Handcrafted with care

## Social Mission

Smart Glamour is dedicated to changing the fashion industry through education about inclusive sizing and sustainable production methods.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for custom-made, size-inclusive American clothing.
