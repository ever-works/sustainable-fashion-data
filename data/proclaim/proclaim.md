## Overview

Founded by Shobha Philips to expand the definition of 'nude' in lingerie, Proclaim offers inclusive intimates for all skin tones.

## Inclusivity Mission

- Expands definition of 'nude'
- Matches brown skin tones
- Size-inclusive range
- Represents diversity
- Founded to address market gap

## Sustainability Features

- Sustainable materials
- Ethical production
- Quality construction
- Focus on longevity

## Product Range

Inclusive lingerie and intimates in diverse nude shades representing all skin tones, not just fair skin.

## Founder Story

Created by Shobha Philips after being tired of never finding 'nude' that matched her brown skin tone.

## Impact

Addressing systemic exclusion in lingerie industry by creating truly inclusive nude options for women of color.
