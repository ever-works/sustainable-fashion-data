## Overview

Kathmandu is a New Zealand brand with stockists in the UK that produces outdoor and lifestyle clothing.

## Sustainability Practices

- Buys carbon credits to offset air travel
- Recycles 100% of polybags in New Zealand
- Works with small cooperatives providing employment to disadvantaged individuals
- Working towards Zero Waste goal
- Some garments made from recycled fibres and organic cotton
- Earthcolour range dyed with plant-based dyes instead of petrochemical dyes

## Notes

Some products are blended fabrics, making them unrecyclable. Few garments made from recycled fibres and organic cotton.

## Sizing

UK 6 – 18, XS – XXL
