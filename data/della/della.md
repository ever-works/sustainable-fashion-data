# DELLA

DELLA is a Ghanaian fashion brand partnering directly with artisan communities to preserve traditional African textile craftsmanship while creating contemporary, globally-inspired designs. By employing West African artisans at fair wages and centering indigenous techniques like hand-weaving and block-printing, the brand keeps cultural heritage economically viable. Where global fashion appropriates African aesthetics from overseas factories with no connection to the communities that originated them, DELLA ensures authentic cultural expression benefits the artisans who carry these traditions forward.
