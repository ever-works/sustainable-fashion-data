## Overview

LØCI is a London-based brand producing 100% vegan sneakers that have gained recognition from top luxury fashion brands and creatives worldwide. The brand is committed to sustainability, using innovative materials and donating a portion of profits to biodiversity charities.

## Materials

- Recycled ocean plastic
- Bio-leather
- Cotton canvas and jersey linings made of recycled plastic
- 100% vegan construction

## Product Range

- Sneakers (ECHO, LUNA, CLOUD, and other styles)
- Apparel
- Colorways include classic, limited edition, and seasonal collections

## Sizing

- Women's 5 – 14
- Men's 4 – 13

## Price Range

- $120 – $150

## Sustainability & Ethics

- Donates 10% of profits to biodiversity charities like Re:Wild
- Uses recycled ocean plastic in upper materials
- Bio-leather construction
- Eco-conscious processes from design to production

## Comfort

- Designed for minimal break-in time
- Comfortable for all-day wear
- Suitable for everyday styling with dresses, jeans, and shorts
