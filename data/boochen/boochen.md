## Overview

Boochen is an eco-conscious brand based in Hanover, Germany, founded by surfers who wanted to create bikinis better for surfing and the ocean. The company focuses on reducing waste and respecting nature through sustainable materials and production.

## Ethics & Sustainability

- Fair trade certified
- Fibres reclaimed from sea-waste via Econyl partnership
- Uses Lycra Extra Life for activewear durability
- REPREVE regenerated fibre from pre- and post-consumer waste (Global Recycling Standard 4.0)
- Partnership with UNIFI for sustainable fibres
- Compostable ziplock bags made from PBAT and plant-based PLA
- Acid-free paper packaging
