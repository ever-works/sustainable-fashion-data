## Overview

Rodebjer is a Swedish fashion brand known for its clean, Scandi-cool aesthetic and serious commitment to sustainability. The brand uses natural fibers including organic cotton and Tencel to create contemporary women's collections.

## Features

- Swedish design brand
- Scandi-cool aesthetic
- Natural fibers focus
- Organic cotton
- Tencel materials
- Contemporary women's fashion
- Sustainable commitment
- Clean, minimalist designs
- Quality construction

## Sustainability Practices

Rodebjer prioritizes natural, sustainable fibers including organic cotton and Tencel (lyocell) throughout its collections. The brand combines its signature Scandinavian aesthetic with environmental responsibility and quality craftsmanship.

## Product Range

Offers contemporary women's clothing including dresses, tops, knitwear, trousers, and outerwear with clean Scandinavian design.

## Pricing

Premium pricing for Scandinavian sustainable contemporary fashion.
