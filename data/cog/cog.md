# COG

COG is a sustainable footwear brand crafting ethically-made sneakers designed for conscious consumers who refuse to compromise on comfort or environmental responsibility. Independently rated 'Good' by Good On You, the brand proves that everyday footwear can be produced with verified ethical standards and responsible materials. Where mainstream sneaker brands rely on toxic synthetic materials and anonymous factory labor, COG delivers clean, comfortable footwear with independently verified sustainability credentials.
