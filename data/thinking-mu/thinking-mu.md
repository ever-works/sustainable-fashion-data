# Thinking Mu

Thinking Mu is a Spanish Fairtrade-certified brand crafting clothing for men and women from 100% organic cotton and organic merino wool, ensuring ethical worker treatment at every stage of the supply chain. By using only organically farmed natural fibers and Fairtrade-verified factories, the brand eliminates pesticides, supports farming communities, and guarantees living wages. Where fast fashion exploits workers while flooding clothes with toxic chemicals, Thinking Mu delivers comfortable, nature-derived garments with independent verification of both people and planet.
