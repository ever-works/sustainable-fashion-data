## Overview

Lovia is a Finnish brand making bags from waste materials that would otherwise end up in landfills. Much of the waste comes from the Finnish furniture industry, which the brand transforms into sustainable accessories.

## Features

- Finnish brand
- Waste material use
- Furniture industry waste
- Landfill diversion
- Upcycled bags
- Sustainable accessories
- Waste transformation
- Environmental focus
- Circular economy

## Sustainability Practices

Lovia rescues waste materials from the Finnish furniture industry, transforming them into stylish bags and accessories. This approach diverts waste from landfills while creating unique, sustainable products from locally-sourced materials.

## Product Range

Offers bags and accessories made from upcycled Finnish furniture industry waste.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Finnish upcycled accessories.
