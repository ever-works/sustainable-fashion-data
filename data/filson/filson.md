## Overview

Filson has been creating exceptionally durable outdoor clothing and gear since 1897. The brand's commitment to quality and longevity means many Filson products last for decades, with some pieces passed down through generations.

## Features

- Established in 1897
- Exceptionally durable construction
- Built to last generations
- Repair services available
- American heritage brand
- High-quality materials
- Outdoor and workwear focus
- Timeless designs
- Many Made in USA products

## Sustainability Practices

Filson's approach to sustainability centers on creating products so durable they rarely need replacement. The brand offers repair services to extend product life even further, and many Filson items become family heirlooms, representing the ultimate in sustainable consumption through extreme longevity.

## Product Range

Offers jackets, coats, vests, shirts, pants, bags, luggage, and outdoor accessories built for rugged use and long-term durability.

## Pricing

Premium to luxury pricing justified by exceptional durability and lifetime value, with jackets typically $300-600.
