## Overview

Beyond Retro was founded in 2002 in East London with a mission to reclaim, reduce, and reuse on-trend and timeless vintage styles without contributing to a carbon footprint. The company has grown to operate 16 brick-and-mortar stores across the UK, Finland, and Sweden, in addition to a global online store.

## Sustainability

- Circular innovation through vintage and pre-loved clothing
- Upcycled options to extend garment lifecycles
- Keeps rare vintage items out of landfills
- Reduces carbon footprint by extending existing garment life

## Product Range

- Vintage clothing from the 1960s through Y2K era
- Accessories
- Rare and unique vintage pieces
- Items available at every price point
