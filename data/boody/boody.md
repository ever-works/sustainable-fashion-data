## Overview

Boody creates comfortable everyday essentials from organically grown bamboo with multiple sustainability certifications.

## Sustainability Features

- OCIA certified organic bamboo
- Closed-loop production system
- Recycles water in production process
- Uses non-toxic solvents
- Lower-waste cutting techniques
- Multiple certifications

## Certifications

- ECOCERT certified
- Oeko-Tex certified
- PETA-approved
- SGS certified
- OCIA (Organic Crop Improvement Association)

## Materials

- Certified organic bamboo viscose
- Sustainably farmed bamboo
- Natural, breathable fibers

## Product Range

Underwear, basics, activewear, and essentials for men, women, and babies made from soft, sustainable bamboo.

## Environmental Process

Closed-loop production and water recycling minimize environmental impact while creating hypoallergenic, breathable clothing.
