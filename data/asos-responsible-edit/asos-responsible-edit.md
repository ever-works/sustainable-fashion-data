## Overview

The Responsible Edit is ASOS's dedicated sustainable fashion line, offering a curated selection of clothing, accessories, and footwear that meet specific environmental and ethical criteria.

## Key Features

- **Recycled Fabrics**: Includes garments made from recycled polyester, organic cotton, and other eco-friendly materials
- **Ethical Sourcing**: Products sourced from suppliers meeting responsible manufacturing standards
- **Wide Range**: Spans multiple categories including womenswear, menswear, activewear, and accessories
- **Affordable Sustainability**: Makes sustainable fashion accessible at mid-range price points
- **Clear Labeling**: Items in the Responsible Edit are clearly identified for easy consumer identification

## Sustainability Criteria

Products in the Responsible Edit typically meet one or more of the following standards:

- Made with at least 50% recycled materials
- Organic cotton certified by recognized standards
- Responsibly sourced wool or other natural fibers
- Ethical production verified through supply chain audits
- Reduced environmental impact in manufacturing processes

## Pricing

Affordable to mid-range pricing, making sustainable fashion accessible to a broad consumer base.
