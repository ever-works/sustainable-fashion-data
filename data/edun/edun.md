## Overview

EDUN is a New York-based women's fashion label founded in 2005 by Ali Hewson, rooted in the mission of promoting ethical production and trade across Africa.

## Mission & Approach

- Promotes production and trade across the African continent
- Works closely with manufacturers, artists, and local communities
- Strengthens trade and creates lasting business ties with African creators in fashion
- Multi-pronged approach to ethical clothing production

## Business Development

- Received investment from LVMH in recent years
- Aims to spread sustainable fashion practices across the wider high-fashion world

## Products

- Women's fashion collections
- Pieces produced in partnership with African artisans and communities
