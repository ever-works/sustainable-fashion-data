## Overview

REER3 stands for slow fashion streetwear in a reduced design, produced more sustainably. Founded by a Brazilian-born fashion designer and artist based in Germany, the brand creates contemporary streetwear that prioritises quality and environmental responsibility.

## Features

- Slow fashion streetwear collection
- Reduced, minimalist design aesthetic
- GOTS certified organic cotton
- Recycled polyester materials
- Lower-impact dyes used throughout
- 100% vegan product line
- Available in sizes XS-2XL

## Sustainability

- 100% vegan fashion
- GOTS certified organically grown cotton
- Uses recycled polyester
- Lower-impact dyes reduce chemical pollution
- Produces more responsibly with a focus on quality over quantity
- Based in Germany
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable streetwear.
