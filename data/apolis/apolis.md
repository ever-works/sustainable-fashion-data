## Overview

Apolis is a sustainable clothing brand from America founded by brothers Raan and Shea Parton in 2004, after traveling around the globe and recognizing the importance of local, community production.

## Philosophy

- Brand name rooted in "advocacy through industry" (apolis meaning "citizens without a city" in Greek)
- Committed to local, community-based production models
- Supports developing communities worldwide

## Sustainability Practices

- Works closely with manufacturers around the world
- Provides fair, living wages to workers
- Provides structure for further worker development
- Creates ethical clothing that empowers communities

## Products

- Wide range of apparel for men and women
- Accessories
- Products designed to be long-lasting classics
