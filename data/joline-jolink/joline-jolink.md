## Overview

Joline Jolink is a Dutch fashion brand creating garments primarily from deadstock and leftover fabric, with sustainable delivery across the Netherlands.

## Sustainability Features

- Primary materials sourced from deadstock and leftover fabric
- Pre-loved resale program extends garment lifecycle
- Bicycle delivery available across 30 cities in the Netherlands
- Zero-emission last-mile logistics
- Reduces textile waste from surplus materials

## Delivery

- Free bicycle delivery across 30+ Dutch cities
- Zero carbon emissions for last-mile delivery
- Local distribution network

## Pre-Loved Program

Resale marketplace for previously owned Joline Jolink garments, extending product lifespans and reducing demand for new production.

## Materials

- Deadstock fabrics diverted from landfill
- Leftover materials from textile production
- Surplus fabric repurposing

## Product Range

Contemporary women's fashion including dresses, tops, and separates made from rescued textile materials.
