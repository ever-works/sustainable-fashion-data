## Overview

milo+nicki is a sustainable clothing brand based in Austin, Texas, founded by Nicki Patel. The brand celebrates the power and strength of women, culture, and diversity while pioneering the use of innovative sustainable fabrics like banana fabric.

## Sustainability Approach

- One of the first brands to use banana fabric, a zero-carbon emission material
- Practices intersectional environmentalism, connecting racial justice with environmentalism
- Designed in-house by founder Nicki Patel
- Creates open dialogue around manufacturing ethics and consumer mindset
- Advocates for "seasonless fashion" - building versatile wardrobes usable across different seasons

## Products

- The Fiesta Dress
- Zero Waste Headbands
- Various clothing items celebrating culture and diversity

## Brand Philosophy

Founder Nicki Patel started the brand after discovering that her stressful corporate job was physically and mentally affecting her. She turned to fashion with a focus on answering the question: "Who is the human behind your brand?" The brand is named as a testament to her pup Milo and promotes intersectional environmentalism. The company's website features blogs and a "Be You" campaign showcasing interviews with people that have inspired Patel. Patel recommends manifesting "seasonless fashion" to build a more sustainable closet.
