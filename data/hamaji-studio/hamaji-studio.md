## Overview

Hamaji Studio was founded in 2017 by Louise Sommerlatte with a principle of preserving ancient textile traditions and nomadic handcrafts. The brand's name means 'nomad' in local Swahili.

## Sustainability Practices

- All fabrics made by hand
- Uses natural fibers in textiles
- Uses natural ingredients for dyes
- Empowers artisans in the East African region
- Preserves ancient textile traditions and nomadic handcrafts

## Design Philosophy

The brand draws inspiration from the everyday charm, natural beauty, and vibrant culture of East Africa, creating designs that reflect the region's heritage and environment.

## Craftsmanship

- Handcrafted fabrics using traditional techniques
- Natural dyeing processes
- Nomadic handcraft preservation
