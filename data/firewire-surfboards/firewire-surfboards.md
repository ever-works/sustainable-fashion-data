## Overview

Firewire produces 100% ECOBOARD certified surfboards verified by Sustainable Surf. To earn this certification, Firewire utilizes less-toxic materials and minimizes resin, foam, and wood waste. They also up-cycle waste materials to manufacture surfboards with a measurably reduced carbon footprint compared to conventional surfboards.

## Innovative Products

Beyond surfboards, Firewire manufactures:
- Surfboard leashes using recycled plastic bottles
- Paving stones from upcycled EPS surfboard foam
- Traction pads made from algae biomass
