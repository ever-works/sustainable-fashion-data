## Overview

Andie Swim creates premium women's swimwear from recycled nylon sourced from ocean waste and fishing nets. The brand combines style, fit, and environmental responsibility.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Recycled Nylon**: Made from reclaimed fishing nets
- **Ocean Waste**: Transforms ocean plastic into swimwear
- **ECONYL® Fabric**: Regenerated nylon from waste materials
- **Durable Quality**: Long-lasting swimwear reduces need for replacement

## Ethical Manufacturing

- **Fair Labor Practices**: Ensures workers receive fair wages
- **Safe Working Conditions**: Ethical factory standards
- **Transparency**: Dedicated sustainability page details practices
- **Supply Chain Visibility**: Clear communication about production

## Design Philosophy

Andie Swim focuses on fit, comfort, and style. The brand offers sizes XS-3X with designs that flatter various body types, proving that sustainable swimwear doesn't sacrifice fashion.

## Environmental Impact

By using recycled ocean waste, Andie Swim helps clean our oceans while creating beautiful, functional swimwear that reduces demand for virgin materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range to premium pricing for sustainable recycled swimwear.
