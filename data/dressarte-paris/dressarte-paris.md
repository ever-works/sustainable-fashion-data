## Overview

Dressarte Paris makes custom clothes using luxurious, lower-impact materials that often come from surplus sources. Each garment is made-to-order, offering customers the option to choose from existing sizes or enter their own measurements.

## Features

- Custom-made to order
- Choice of existing sizes or personal measurements
- Luxurious lower-impact materials
- Surplus source materials reduce waste

## Sustainability

- Uses surplus materials to reduce waste
- Made-to-order production eliminates overproduction
- Lower-impact material selection
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Pricing

Mid-to-premium custom womenswear pricing.
