## Overview

Frisson Knits is a New Zealand brand creating locally sourced, ethical wool creations. The brand specializes in sustainable knitwear using New Zealand wool with responsible production practices.

## Features

- Locally sourced New Zealand wool
- Ethical wool production
- Sustainable knitwear
- New Zealand brand
- Responsible practices
- Quality wool creations
- Local sourcing
- Artisan knits
- Natural fibers

## Sustainability Practices

Frisson Knits uses locally sourced New Zealand wool, supporting domestic farmers and reducing transportation emissions. The brand's commitment to ethical wool production ensures animal welfare and environmental responsibility.

## Product Range

Offers knitwear including sweaters, cardigans, and accessories made from locally sourced New Zealand wool.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for locally sourced New Zealand wool knitwear.
