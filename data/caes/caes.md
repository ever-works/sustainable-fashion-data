## Overview

CAES (pronounced like case) is a Dutch fashion brand built on the philosophy that fashion does not have to be fast or seasonal. The brand creates timeless, minimalist clothes using innovative lower-impact materials.

## Features

- Timeless, minimalist clothing designs
- Lower-impact materials including Desserto cactus leather, recycled cotton, and TENCEL Lyocell
- No seasonal collections
- Partly traces its supply chain
- Code of conduct covering ILO Four Fundamental Freedoms principles

## Sustainability

- Uses Desserto cactus leather as a sustainable material alternative
- Recycled cotton reduces virgin fibre demand
- TENCEL Lyocell from responsibly sourced wood pulp
- Supply chain traceability
- Code of conduct aligned with ILO principles
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Dutch contemporary fashion.
