## Overview

Sassy Spud is an ethical unisex clothing brand committed to 100% sweatshop-free manufacturing.

## Key Features

- All clothing made in Fair Wear Foundation-approved factories, ensuring workers receive safe, dignified, and properly paid employment
- 10% of profits from every order donated to UK mental health charities, including The Mix, Mind, and Shout
- Produces unisex clothing
