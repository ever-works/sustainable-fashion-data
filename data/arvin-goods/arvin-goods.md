## Overview

Arvin Goods produces sustainable everyday essentials including t-shirts, underwear, socks, and loungewear. The brand focuses on reducing environmental impact through the use of organic and recycled materials.

## Key Features

- T-shirts made from organic cotton and recycled cotton blends
- Underwear and socks crafted from organic and recycled fibers
- Zero virgin plastic packaging
- Low-impact dyes and water-efficient production processes
- Carbon-neutral shipping
- Products designed for durability and longevity

## Sustainability

- Eliminates virgin plastics from packaging and products
- Uses GOTS-certified organic cotton
- Incorporates recycled cotton to reduce water consumption
- Transparent about manufacturing partners and practices

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing accessible for sustainable basics. T-shirts typically range from $30–$50, underwear from $15–$25 per pair.
