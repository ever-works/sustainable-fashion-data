## Overview

SAMARA is a Toronto, Canada-based brand founded by two sisters Salima and Samara, focusing on elegant, cruelty-free, minimalist bags and accessories made from sustainable materials.

## Product Range
- Bags
- Accessories

## Materials & Practices
- Premium eco-friendly materials
- Limits PVC use, uses water-based PU and microfibres (less toxic)
- Recycled ocean plastics
- Recycled plastic bottles
- Bamboo
- Apple-leather totes made from apple skins (by‑products of juicing industry)
