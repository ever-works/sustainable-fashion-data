## Overview

Omnes is a sustainable fashion brand that partners with Earthly, a climate organisation that helps measure carbon footprint. Since 2020, the brand has worked to offset 125 per cent of its calculated environmental impact.

## Key Features

- Offsets 125% of its calculated carbon footprint via Earthly partnership
- Prioritises natural, deadstock and recycled materials
- Offers wedding and wedding guest-appropriate gowns
- Proves sustainable fashion can be accessible and affordable
- Focuses on transparency in its environmental impact measurement

## Pricing

Positioned as an affordable sustainable fashion brand, proving that eco-conscious clothing does not require a high price tag.
