## Overview

Blanche is a Danish fashion brand designing clothing as an ode to the modern female. The brand uses organic and recycled materials while embracing Scandinavian minimalism and contemporary aesthetics.

## Features

- Danish contemporary fashion
- Organic materials
- Recycled fabrics
- Scandinavian minimalism
- Modern female focus
- Clean aesthetics
- Sustainable materials
- Contemporary designs
- Quality construction

## Sustainability Practices

Blanche uses organic and recycled materials throughout its collections, maintaining sustainable practices while creating contemporary fashion with clean, minimalist Scandinavian design principles.

## Product Range

Offers women's contemporary fashion including dresses, tops, knitwear, trousers, and outerwear with minimalist aesthetic.

## Pricing

Premium pricing for Scandinavian sustainable contemporary fashion.
