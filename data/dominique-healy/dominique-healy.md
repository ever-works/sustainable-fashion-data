## Overview

Dominique Healy is a Melbourne-based slow fashion designer known for creating trans-seasonal pieces in small, limited runs. The label has built a reputation for using sustainable deadstock fabrics to produce distinctive, eye-catching designs.

## Features

- Trans-seasonal collections
- Small, limited production runs
- Luxe fabrics and flowing silhouettes
- Gorgeous prints and flowing sleeves
- Melbourne-based design and production

## Sustainability Practices

- Uses sustainable deadstock fabrics
- Small production runs to minimise waste
- Each piece made in Melbourne
- Produced either in the designer's studio or through an Ethical Clothing Australia accredited factory

## Pricing

Premium pricing reflecting limited-run production and luxury materials.
