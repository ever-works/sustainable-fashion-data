## Overview

Due South is an Irish eco clothing brand spotted at Bloom 2018, making casual wear from sustainable materials.

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Recycled cotton
- Recycled post-consumer PET blended with organic cotton offcuts

## Notes

- Blending fibres currently makes the final fabric un-recyclable
- T-shirts are printed by hand in Ireland

## Product Range

- T-shirts
- Hoodies
- Sweaters

## Sizing

- XS to XXL
