## Overview

BAGGU creates simple, playful, and functional bags designed to replace single-use plastic bags. The brand has expanded from its signature reusable shopping bag to include a full range of bags and accessories, all emphasizing sustainability and practicality.

## Features

- Recycled nylon from pre-consumer waste
- Saves nylon scraps from landfills
- Reusable bag alternatives
- Colorful, fun designs
- Lightweight and packable
- Machine washable
- Durable construction
- Wide variety of sizes and styles
- Affordable pricing

## Sustainability Practices

BAGGU makes wallets and many bags from recycled nylon material sourced from pre-consumer waste, preventing nylon scraps from being landfilled. The brand's core mission is to replace single-use plastic bags with durable, reusable alternatives that can be used thousands of times, significantly reducing waste.

## Product Range

Offers reusable shopping bags, backpacks, totes, pouches, wallets, travel bags, and more in a rainbow of colors and patterns.

## Pricing

Affordable pricing with signature reusable bags starting around $12-14 and larger bags ranging $20-60.
