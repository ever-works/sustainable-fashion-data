## Overview

KitX was founded by Kit Willow in Australia, sourcing organic materials and upcycled marine waste while supporting artisans. The brand creates contemporary fashion with strong environmental and social impact.

## Features

- Founded by Kit Willow
- Organic materials
- Upcycled marine waste
- Artisan support
- Australian brand
- Contemporary fashion
- Environmental impact
- Social responsibility
- Sustainable sourcing

## Sustainability Practices

KitX sources organic materials and transforms upcycled marine waste into fashion, contributing to ocean cleanup. The brand supports artisan communities while creating contemporary designs with minimal environmental impact.

## Product Range

Offers contemporary women's fashion made from organic and upcycled materials.
