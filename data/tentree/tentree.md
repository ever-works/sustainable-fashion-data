## Overview

tentree is a sustainable lifestyle apparel brand with a mission to plant trees and inspire conscious consumption.

## Sustainability Features

- Plants 10 trees for every item purchased
- Climate Neutral Certified
- Uses sustainable fabrics and blends
- Part of 1% for the Planet
- Transparent about environmental impact
- Over 150 million trees planted to date
- Customer tree tracking system

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Tencel
- Recycled polyester
- Sustainable fabric blends
- Cork
- Hemp

## Product Range

Casual wear, basics, and outdoor apparel including t-shirts, hoodies, pants, and accessories for men and women.

## Impact

Each purchase contributes to global reforestation efforts, with customers able to track where their trees are planted through the tentree impact app.
