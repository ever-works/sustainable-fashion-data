## Overview

The Travelling Kimono is a sustainable fashion label based in Noosa on Queensland's Sunshine Coast, founded by Janelle Rawlins and Nicki Edmiston. The brand creates coastal, beach-inspired clothing focused on limited-edition handmade pieces that minimise waste through the use of recycled linen and fabric remnants.

## Features

- Limited-edition handmade kimonos in recycled linen
- Headscarves and accessories made from fabric remnants to avoid wastage
- Beach-inspired coastal clothing designs
- Creative workshops hosted in-store
- Founded by Janelle Rawlins and Nicki Edmiston

## Sustainability Practices

- Uses recycled linen for garment production
- Repurposes fabric remnants into accessories to eliminate textile waste
- Limited-edition production runs to prevent overproduction
- Handmade manufacturing process
