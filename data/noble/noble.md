## Overview

Noble is built on a deep commitment to ethical production and environmental stewardship. 100% of their garments are crafted from GOTS-certified organic Pima cotton—grown, harvested by hand, milled, and sewn locally to minimize carbon emissions and ensure fair treatment at every step.

## Sustainability

- Certified B Corp
- 100% GOTS-certified organic Pima cotton
- Regenerative agriculture practices
- Local production to minimize carbon emissions
- Fair treatment throughout the supply chain
- Upcycled, chemical-free denim collection
- Compostable packaging
- Mail-in credit program for circularity
- USDA organic certified

## Product Range

- Cotton canvas dresses and jumpsuits
- Full garment collection
- Upcycled chemical-free denim
- Kids sizes (12M – 12Y)
