## Overview

Natascha von Hirschhausen is a Berlin-based fashion brand focused on sustainable and mindful design as an alternative to mass production. The brand emphasizes endurance across every facet of its operations.

## Features

- Uniquely designed unisex denim trousers
- Local production in Berlin
- Quality materials selected for durability
- Available exclusively through the brand's own channels

## Sustainability

- Zero-waste pattern designs that reduce the roughly 20% of material lost in conventional fashion cutting to less than 1%
- Locally produced entire collection in Berlin, reducing transportation emissions
- Focus on endurance quality and timeless design over trends
- Alternative to mass-production fashion model
