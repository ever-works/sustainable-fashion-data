## Overview

Warp + Weft is a veteran denim brand collaborating with eco-friendly mills for 30 years to produce high-quality, sustainable denim at accessible prices.

## Sustainability Features

- Proprietary dyeing process reduces water usage by 76%
- Recycles 98% of water used in production
- Vertically integrated dyeing and washing processes
- Offsets carbon emissions through reduction projects
- Uses lower-impact materials
- All denim under $100

## Materials

- Recycled cotton
- TENCEL Lyocell
- Lower-impact denim materials

## Product Range

Denim jeans in various fits and washes for men and women, sizes up to 3X.

## Inclusivity & Affordability

Committed to making sustainable denim accessible through competitive pricing and size-inclusive range up to 3X.

## Water Conservation

Uses 1,400 fewer gallons of water per pair compared to conventional denim through innovative recycling and dyeing processes.
