## Overview

Liandra Swim is an Australian swimwear brand featuring Aboriginal culture prints. The brand uses fabrics made from regenerated plastics and recycled elastane, celebrating Indigenous art while supporting environmental sustainability.

## Features

- Aboriginal culture prints
- Regenerated plastic fabrics
- Recycled elastane
- Australian brand
- Indigenous art celebration
- Sustainable swimwear
- Cultural respect
- Eco-friendly materials
- Unique designs

## Sustainability Practices

Liandra Swim creates swimwear from regenerated plastics and recycled elastane, transforming waste into beautiful, functional products. The brand respectfully celebrates Aboriginal culture through its prints, supporting Indigenous artists and cultural preservation.

## Product Range

Offers bikinis and one-piece swimsuits featuring Aboriginal-inspired prints made from recycled materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Australian sustainable swimwear with cultural significance.
