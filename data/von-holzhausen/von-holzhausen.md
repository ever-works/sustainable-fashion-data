## Overview

von Holzhausen is a California-based luxury brand revolutionizing leather goods with innovative, sustainable materials. The brand creates high-end accessories and lifestyle products without animal products.

## Innovative Materials

### Technik-Leather
- Proprietary material made from recycled plastic bottles
- Durable and water-resistant
- Soft, supple texture
- Easy to clean and maintain

### Plant-Based Leather
- Made from renewable plant sources
- Lower environmental impact
- Biodegradable options

## Product Range

- Handbags and totes
- Wallets and cardholders
- Phone cases
- Laptop sleeves
- Home accessories
- Pet accessories
- Travel goods

## Sustainability Features

- 100% vegan and cruelty-free
- Made from recycled and renewable materials
- Reduced carbon footprint
- Water-resistant without chemical treatments
- Durable for long-term use
- Recyclable at end of life

## Design

- Minimalist, modern aesthetic
- Timeless designs
- Functional and practical
- Available in multiple colors
- Clean lines and quality craftsmanship

## Production

- Made in California
- Direct-to-consumer model
- Small batch production
- Quality control and craftsmanship

## Innovation

von Holzhausen collaborates with automotive and design industries, including partnerships with Tesla, showcasing the versatility and quality of their sustainable materials.
