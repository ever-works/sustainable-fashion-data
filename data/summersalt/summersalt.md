## Overview

Summersalt is a travelwear brand offering sustainable swimwear, athleisure, and loungewear. The brand gathered over 1.5 million measurements from 10,000 women to inform its fit, creating versatile garments from color-blocking one-pieces to light and breezy sweaters.

## Best For

Swimwear and activewear, size range XS–2X; up to US 24.

## Features

- Fit informed by 10,000+ women's measurements
- Color-blocking one-piece swimsuits
- Light and breezy sweaters
- Versatile travel-ready garments
- Postpartum-friendly length options

## Sustainability

- Recycled fabrics including recycled polyamide
- Recycled packaging
- WRAP-accredited factories
- Sustainably certified production
- Regenerative cupro materials
- Cruelty-free wool
