## Overview

Creating eco-conscious clothing in West LA since 2014, Whimsy + Row makes limited-run batches of handcrafted clothing.

## Sustainability Features

- Locally sourced deadstock materials
- Eco-friendly fabrics
- Limited-run production
- Handcrafted clothing
- Made in downtown LA
- Reduces waste through deadstock use

## Materials

- Deadstock materials
- Eco-friendly fabrics
- Locally sourced textiles

## Product Range

Sustainable women's clothing and accessories with contemporary, minimalist aesthetic.

## Local Manufacturing

Made in downtown Los Angeles supporting local artisans and reducing transportation emissions.

## Production Model

Limited-run batches reduce overproduction and waste, creating exclusive, mindfully-made pieces.
