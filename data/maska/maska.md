## Overview

Maska is a Swedish brand specializing in long-lasting knitwear made from organic cotton, hemp, wool, cashmere, silk, and Tencel. Their garments require fewer washes and lower washing temperatures, contributing to sustainability.

## Sustainability Practices

- Materials: organic cotton, hemp, wool, cashmere, silk, and Tencel
- All knitwear yarn spun in Europe (except cashmere and silk blend)
- Production in the EU following European labour laws
- One style produced in Nepal at a Swedish-owned, Fair Wear Foundation compliant factory
- Knitwear designed to seldom need laundering and require lower washing temperatures

## Sizing

- XS – XL

## Pricing

Mid-to-premium pricing for European-made knitwear.
