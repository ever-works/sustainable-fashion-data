## Overview

The Spark Company sells ethically made, 100% vegan-friendly feminist and LGBTQ+ apparel with a strong commitment to sustainability and social activism.

## Key Features

- Guarantees no sweatshops or child labour ever
- 100% vegan-friendly products
- Zero-waste policy — prints all apparel to order
- Uses vegan dyes
- Plastic-free packaging
- Donates part of profits to charities including Bloody Good Period (menstrual equality) and akt (LGBTQ+ youth homelessness), among others close to the brand's mission

Budget-friendly pricing (affordable range).
