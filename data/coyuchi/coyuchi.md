# Coyuchi

Coyuchi creates GOTS and MADE SAFE certified organic cotton sleepwear and home textiles in factories that recycle 98% of their wastewater. By using only certified organic fibers and maintaining rigorous water reuse protocols, the brand sets an industry benchmark for sustainable home goods production. Where conventional bedding and sleepwear rely on pesticide-treated cotton and chemical-laden manufacturing, Coyuchi's dual certification approach and exceptional water stewardship prove that the most intimate products can be the most environmentally responsible.
