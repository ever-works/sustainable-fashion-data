## Overview

Coyuchi offers breathable organic cotton pajamas that are both GOTS and MADE SAFE certified. The brand's products are sewn in a factory that recycles 98% of its wastewater, demonstrating exceptional environmental responsibility.

- MADE SAFE certified
- Breathable pajamas
- 98% wastewater recycling
- Sustainable sleepwear
- Home textiles

- Quality organic materials
- Responsible manufacturing

## Sustainability Practices

Coyuchi maintains both GOTS and MADE SAFE certifications, ensuring organic standards and product safety. The brand's manufacturing partner recycles 98% of wastewater, demonstrating extraordinary commitment to water conservation.
