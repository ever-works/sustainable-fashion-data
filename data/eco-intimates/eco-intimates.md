## Overview

Eco Intimates is a basics brand featuring organic cotton lingerie, intimates, and sleepwear for women who care about their bodies and the Earth.

## Features

- Organic cotton lingerie and intimates
- Sustainable sleepwear range
- Loungewear available in sizes XS to 2XL
- Some lingerie available up to size 4XL
- Focus on eco-friendly materials
