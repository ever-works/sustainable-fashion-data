## Overview

BEEN London is a pioneering sustainable accessories brand that transforms waste into beautiful, functional products. Based in London, the brand specializes in creating timeless accessories from innovative recycled materials.

## Innovative Materials

### Recycled Textiles
- Discarded clothing
- Fabric waste
- Textile off-cuts
- Upcycled materials

### Apple Leather
- Made from apple industry waste
- Vegan leather alternative
- Biodegradable components

### Recycled Plastics
- Single-use plastic waste
- Post-consumer plastic

### Leather Off-Cuts
- Industrial leather waste
- Repurposed materials
- Zero waste approach

## Product Range

- Handbags and totes
- Backpacks
- Crossbody bags
- Wallets and cardholders
- Phone cases
- Travel accessories
