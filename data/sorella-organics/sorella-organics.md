## Overview

Sorella Organics is an Australian brand offering a certified organic sleepwear range that includes tops, pants, wraps, nighties, and maternity wear. The label's founder Anna McGregor has a background in international human rights work, which is reflected in the brand's ethical practices.

## Features

- Certified organic sleepwear and maternity wear
- Uses Fairtrade certified cotton
- Strict social standards met in production and trading
- Founder with international human rights background
- Products available in sizes S-XL
- Accessible pricing

## Pricing

Purchasing the clothing is described as affordable and won't impact your bank balance significantly.
