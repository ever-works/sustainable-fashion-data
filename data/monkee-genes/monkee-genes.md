## Overview

UK-based Monkee Genes offers organic, ethically produced jeans from multiple origins - organic from Indonesia, ethically produced from Turkey, and grassroots manufacturing in England.

## Sustainability Practices

- Organic denim from Indonesia
- Ethically produced jeans from Turkey
- Grassroots jeans made in England
- Multiple sourcing locations with ethical oversight

## Sizing

- Women: M – L, 24 – 32 (some items available in XS & S)
- Men: 28 – 38 (some items available in XS & S)

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for ethical denim.
