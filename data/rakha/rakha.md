## Overview

Rakha is a UK-based brand whose approach to design and garment making is sustainability, through products made from eco-friendly or repurposed materials which can be bio-degraded or recycled, contributing to the development of sustainable communities throughout the supply chain.

## Sustainability Practices

- Works only with green manufacturers with established environmental management systems
- Aims to use recycled or bio-degradable materials for garments
- All organic or sustainable materials are certified
- Fabrics include: Woolmark certified Merino Wool, GOTS certified Organic Cotton, Bamboo, Lenzing certified cellulose fibers, RPET recycled fabrics
- Limits carbon impact of operations
