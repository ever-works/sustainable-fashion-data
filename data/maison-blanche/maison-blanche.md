## Overview

maison blanche is a Swiss fashion label that produces vegan, conceptual fashion and raises awareness of socio-political issues. The brand combines artistic expression with ethical fashion values.

## Features

- Vegan fashion with no animal-derived materials
- Conceptual, artistic design approach
- Socio-political messaging through garments
- Based in Switzerland

## Sustainability

- 100% vegan product line
- Ethical production practices
- Raises awareness of social and political issues
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Pricing

Mid-to-premium pricing for Swiss conceptual fashion.
