## Overview

Girlfriend Collective creates inclusive, sustainable activewear from recycled materials with a commitment to transparency and fair labor practices.

## Sustainability Features

- Made from recycled plastic bottles
- Sustainable fabric sourcing
- Transparent supply chain
- Size-inclusive sizing
- Gender-inclusive designs
- Ethical manufacturing practices

## Materials

- Recycled polyester from plastic bottles
- Recycled nylon
- Sustainable fabrics
- Recycled materials

## Product Range

Activewear for all genders including leggings, sports bras, tops, shorts, sweatshirts, puffers, hoodies, and vests. Wide size range to accommodate all bodies.

## Inclusivity

Offers extended sizing and gender-neutral options, making sustainable activewear accessible to everyone regardless of size, gender, or body type.
