## Overview

Bagatiba is a Los Angeles-based jewelry brand founded by Jessie Andrews, specializing in timeless and handcrafted sustainable pieces.

## Sustainability Features

- Closed-loop upcycling and sustainability initiatives
- Energy consumption management
- Uses recycled metals
- Eliminates single-use plastics
- Recyclable and biodegradable packaging

## Values

- Transparency, authenticity, sustainability, and ethics
- Collaborative approach to environmental change
