## Overview
Ascension Clothing offers well-priced certified organic cotton t-shirts manufactured solely using renewable green energy from wind and solar power in fairly traded factories. T-shirts are printed with environmentally friendly processes.

## Sustainability Features
- Certified organic cotton
- Manufactured 100% with renewable green energy (wind and solar)
- Fairly traded factories
- Environmentally friendly printing
- Donates 10% of gross profits to charity

## Sizing
- One size

## Pricing
Very affordable pricing for sustainable basics.
