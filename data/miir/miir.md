## Overview

MiiR is a Seattle-based brand that designs sustainable, functional accessories including insulated drinkware, bags, and outdoor gear products. The company operates a unique giving model where every product sold helps fund community development projects around the world.

## Features

- Insulated bottles, tumblers, and food containers
- Sustainable backpacks, duffels, and totes
- Outdoor and travel gear
- Each product includes a Give Code to track the project it supports
- B Corp certified
- Climate neutral certified

## Giving Model

- A percentage of every sale funds global giving projects
- Projects span clean water, health, and economic development
- Products include trackable Give Codes for transparency
- Over 400 community projects funded worldwide
