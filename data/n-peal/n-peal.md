## Overview

N.Peal is a British luxury cashmere brand that has been spinning high-quality cashmere since the 1930s. The brand is renowned for combining timeless design with long-lasting quality, and has been worn by iconic figures, including James Bond in the film Skyfall.

## Sourcing & Materials

- Cashmere sourced from traceable Mongolian origins
- High-grade cashmere fibers selected for durability and softness
- Focus on responsible sourcing practices

## Product Range

- Cashmere sweaters, scarves, and knitwear
- Timeless, classic designs that transcend seasonal trends
- Pieces designed for longevity and repeated wear

## Heritage

With a history dating back to the 1930s, N.Peal has built a reputation as a staple of British luxury knitwear, combining heritage craftsmanship with modern responsible sourcing commitments.
