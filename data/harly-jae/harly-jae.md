## Overview

Harly Jae is a Canadian slow fashion house creating feminine, vintage-inspired womenswear. The brand uses all-natural and deadstock materials, producing garments in small batches of 40-200 units in Vancouver factories.

## Features

- Slow fashion approach
- Feminine, vintage-inspired designs
- All-natural materials
- Deadstock fabric use
- Made in Vancouver
- Small batch production (40-200 units)
- Local manufacturing
- Sustainable materials
- Romantic aesthetic

## Sustainability Practices

Harly Jae embraces slow fashion principles by producing small batches in Vancouver, using all-natural fabrics and deadstock materials that would otherwise go to waste. This approach minimizes environmental impact while supporting local manufacturing.

## Product Range

Offers feminine dresses, tops, skirts, and separates with vintage-inspired silhouettes and romantic details.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for small-batch, locally-made vintage-inspired fashion.
