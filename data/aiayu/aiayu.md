## Overview

Established in 2005 by Maria Høgh Heilmann, Aiayu is a Danish luxury brand whose name means "soul" in Aymara, the language of Bolivia's indigenous people. The brand focuses on craftsmanship, sustainability, and longevity.

## Sustainability Milestones

- **Certifications**: Sources from GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) or RWS (Responsible Wool Standard) accredited suppliers wherever possible. Requires manufacturing partners to hold leading certifications such as SA8000.
- **Fibre-to-fibre recycling**: Most materials are managed as pure mono materials to optimise recycling options at end of product life.
- **Repair service**: Offers paid repair services to help prolong the life of products, a practice uncommon among luxury brands that typically focus less on after-sales care.
