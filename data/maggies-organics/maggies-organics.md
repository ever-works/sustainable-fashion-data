## Overview

Maggie's Organics has been a pioneer in organic fair trade clothing for over 30 years, specializing in socks and basics. The brand goes beyond standard fair trade requirements with its Real Fair Trade Pledge, demonstrating exceptional commitment to workers and environmental stewardship.

## Features

- Over 30 years of organic fair trade production
- Real Fair Trade Pledge
- Exceeds standard fair trade requirements
- Organic cotton materials
- Specializes in socks and hosiery
- Basics and everyday essentials
- Worker-focused practices
- Environmental commitment
- Long-lasting quality

## Sustainability Practices

Maggie's Organics has maintained its commitment to organic fair trade practices for over three decades. The brand's Real Fair Trade Pledge goes beyond basic certification, ensuring exceptional working conditions, fair compensation, and environmental responsibility throughout the supply chain.

## Product Range

Offers organic cotton socks in various styles, leggings, tights, apparel basics, and accessories for men, women, and children.

## Pricing

Accessible pricing for organic fair trade basics, with socks typically $10-18 per pair.
