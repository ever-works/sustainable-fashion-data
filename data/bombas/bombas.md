## Overview

Bombas is a certified B Corp brand specializing in high-quality, comfort-focused socks with the widest collection of styles available, including options for the whole family from babies to adults.

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Supima cotton
- Recycled cotton
- Merino wool

## Key Features

- Built-in blister tabs to prevent rubbing
- Honeycomb arch support for a secure fit
- Airflow venting for breathability
- Zone cushioning for targeted comfort
- Responsible and recycled material options
- Circular fashion partnership with For Days — customers can send back used socks to keep them out of landfills

## Social Impact

- Donates one item for every item purchased to people experiencing homelessness
- Partners with 3,500+ giving partners

- Toddler 5T–7.5T
- Junior 8T–11T
