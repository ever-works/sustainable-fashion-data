## Overview

Miik is a Canadian sustainable clothing brand making women's fashion in Canada. The brand offers versatile, comfortable pieces with eco-friendly materials and ethical local production.

## Features

- Made in Canada
- Sustainable materials
- Women's fashion
- Versatile designs
- Comfortable pieces
- Eco-friendly production
- Ethical manufacturing
- Canadian brand
- Local production

## Sustainability Practices

Miik maintains Canadian production using sustainable, eco-friendly materials. The brand creates versatile pieces designed for multiple occasions, promoting a minimalist wardrobe approach and reducing the need for excessive clothing.

## Product Range

Offers versatile women's clothing made in Canada from sustainable materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Canadian-made sustainable fashion.
