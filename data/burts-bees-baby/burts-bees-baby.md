## Overview

Burt's Bees Baby offers a full collection of toddler and kids apparel made from 100 percent GOTS Certified organic cotton. Readily available online and through third-party retailers, the brand is best known for its boldly printed matching pajamas. Sizes range from newborn to 12 years old.

## Products

- **Matching Pajama Sets** — Boldly printed sleepwear made for comfort and durability through repeated wears and washes
- **Toddler and Kids Collection** — Apparel for sizes NB–12Y
- **Family Collections** — Select matching pieces for the entire family

## Sustainability & Certifications

- GOTS Certified organic cotton
- Made with recycled materials
- Budget-friendly pricing ($18–$32 range)

Affordable range of $18–$32 per piece.
