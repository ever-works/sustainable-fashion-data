## Overview

babaà is a Spanish knitwear label known for producing high-quality knits using wool and cotton sourced from local Spanish farmers. The brand has gained significant visibility on social media, with its oversized cardigans frequently appearing on fashion influencers.

## Materials & Sourcing

- Wool sourced from local Spanish farmers
- Cotton sourced from local Spanish farmers
- Supports local agriculture and supply chains in Spain

## Signature Products

- Oversized cardigans (widely recognized on social media)
- Quality knitwear garments
