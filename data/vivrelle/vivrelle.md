## Overview

Vivrelle is a luxury rental service specializing in designer handbags and fine jewelry. The platform offers coveted pieces from luxury brands including Cartier, Van Cleef & Arpels, Khaite, The Row, Prada, and Saint Laurent, allowing members to rotate their accessories without the commitment of purchase.

## How It Works

Members choose from two main membership tiers, each available in Classique and Couture variants:

### Single Item Tiers
- **Classique Plan**: $139/month — one item at a time from jewelry and handbags collections
- **Couture Plan**: $239/month — one item at a time from jewelry, handbags, and limited edition pieces

### Multiple Item Tiers
- **Classique Plan**: $249/month — two items at a time from jewelry and handbags collections
- **Couture Plan**: $339/month — two items at a time from jewelry, handbags, and limited edition pieces

## Key Features

- No required return dates — keep items for as long or as short as desired with an active membership
- Access to limited edition and hard-to-find pieces on Couture tiers

## Pricing

Starting at $139/month for the single-item Classique plan, up to $339/month for the multi-item Couture plan. All prices include shipping in both directions.
