## Overview

Ingrid+Isabel is a maternity brand with a new athleisure line designed for comfort and style during pregnancy and beyond. The brand focuses on soft, flattering pieces suitable for travel, lounging, and casual outings.

## Features

- **Sustainable Materials**: Uses low-impact materials including TENCEL Modal and organic cotton
- **Reduced Virgin Polyester**: Has removed nearly all virgin polyester from the collection
- **Eco-Packaging**: Packaging is either recycled or compostable
- **Resale Program**: Helps extend the lifecycle of garments by keeping clothes in use longer
- **Product Range**: Maternity joggers, tanks, and nursing pullovers

## Sizing

- Size Range: XS – XL
- Recommendation: Use pre-pregnancy size for an accurate fit
