## Overview

Founded in 2018 by Beninese designer Kassim Lassissi, Allëdjo is a menswear clothing line designed and produced by artisans in Dakar, Senegal.

## Sustainability Practices

- Designed and produced by artisans in Senegal
- Supports local African craftsmanship
- Ethical production practices

## Design Philosophy

The brand is a merger of the designer's love of travel and exquisite apparel. The print-heavy color palette and free-flowing materials celebrate the renaissance man on the move, drawing inspiration from travel and cultural exchange.
