## Overview

Nobody Denim is a Melbourne denim brand with operations concentrated within a 6km radius. Accredited with Ethical Clothing Australia, the brand has reduced water consumption by 50% since 2017.

## Features

- Melbourne-based operations
- 6km radius production
- Ethical Clothing Australia accredited
- 50% water reduction since 2017
- Sustainable denim
- Local manufacturing
- Australian brand
- Environmental focus
- Quality jeans

## Sustainability Practices

Nobody Denim's compact 6km production radius minimizes transportation emissions while maintaining Ethical Clothing Australia accreditation. The brand's 50% reduction in water consumption demonstrates ongoing commitment to environmental improvement.

## Product Range

Offers sustainable denim including jeans, jackets, and denim pieces made in Melbourne.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Australian ethical denim.
