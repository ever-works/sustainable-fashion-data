## Overview

Pact is the leading brand in sustainable and ethical clothing, specializing in everyday essentials made from organic cotton.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses almost exclusively GOTS-certified organic cotton
- Saves hundreds of millions of gallons of water through organic farming
- Partners with Fair Trade Certified factories
- Measures and offsets carbon footprint of every product
- Ensures safe working conditions and fair labor practices
- Transparent supply chain

## Materials

- GOTS-certified organic cotton
- Small percentage of elastane for stretch

## Product Range

Everyday essentials including underwear, basics, activewear, loungewear, and sleepwear for men, women, and children.

## Pricing

Affordable pricing with bras starting at $35 and underwear at $16.
