## Overview

Earth and Elle is based in Vancouver, Canada, and was founded with the mission to create sustainable and comfortable organic bras and underwear. Their signature pieces use a soft hemp and organic cotton blend fabric in a high-rise, full-coverage "pin-up booty short" cut.

## Features

- Hemp and organic cotton blend fabric
- Low-impact dyes for reduced environmental harm
- High-rise, full-coverage "pin-up booty short" design
- Made locally in Vancouver, Canada
- Body-inclusive models and sizing
