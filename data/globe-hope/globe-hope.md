## Overview

Globe Hope is a Finnish brand that creates clothing from locally sourced deadstock and recycled textiles manufactured locally in the Baltic region, as well as in factories in Bangladesh and India with GRS (Global Recycled Standard) certification.

## Sustainability Practices

- Clothing from locally sourced deadstock and recycled textiles
- Manufacturing in the Baltic region and GRS-certified factories in Bangladesh and India
- Repair service offered
- Take-back program: customers receive credit when returning used garments and bags
- Global Recycled Standard certification

## Sizing

- Women: S – XL
- Men: S – XXL

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for upcycled and recycled fashion.
