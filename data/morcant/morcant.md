## Overview

Morcant offers long-lasting, gender-neutral clothing made in small batches from organic cotton and recycled polyester in Fair Wear certified factories in Bangladesh. All packaging is certified recyclable and plastic-free.

## Sustainability Practices

- Small batch production to minimize waste
- Organic cotton and recycled polyester materials
- Fair Wear certified factories in Bangladesh
- Certified recyclable, plastic-free packaging
- Gender-neutral, long-lasting designs

## Sizing

- Gender-neutral sizing

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for gender-neutral sustainable basics.
