# Depop

## Overview
Depop is a mobile app and website that combines social media features with a marketplace for secondhand fashion. Founded in 2011, it has become popular among Gen Z shoppers seeking unique and sustainable clothing options.

## Features
- Buy and sell vintage, secondhand, and upcycled fashion
- Social feed allows users to follow sellers and discover items
- In-app payments with buyer and seller protection
- Global community with users in over 150 countries
- Depop Studio offers tools for sellers to enhance listings

## Pricing
Depop charges a 10% transaction fee on the total item price (including shipping) for sellers. Buyers pay the listed price plus shipping; there are no fees for buyers.

## Sustainability Impact
By facilitating the resale of clothing, Depop extends the lifecycle of garments, reduces textile waste, and lowers the demand for new production, contributing to a circular fashion economy.
