## Overview

Wildfang is about challenging gender norms with suits that appeal to the masculine, feminine and everything in between. Starting in Portland, this genderfluid brand works hard to shift gender norms and embrace inclusive practices at all levels.

## Sustainability

- Climate Neutral certified
- Ethical and eco-friendly production practices
- Recyclable packaging
- Gives back to causes like Planned Parenthood
- Gender inclusive design

## Size Range

XS – 3X; up to US 26W

## Key Products

- Gender inclusive blazers and suits
- Bold colors and stunning cuts
- Pattern suits for fashion statements
