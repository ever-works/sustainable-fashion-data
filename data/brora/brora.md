## Overview

Brora is an independent slow fashion brand committed to making long-lasting, quality clothing in natural fibres. For over 30 years, Brora has been leading the way in British-made clothing, taking great pride in its slow fashion ethos and British manufacturing.

## Features

- British manufacturing and British-made clothing
- Commitment to renewable fabrics including organic cotton, wool, and linen
- Heritage-inspired knitwear as a speciality (cardigans, jumpers)
- Range also includes dresses, skirts, trousers, tops, and blouses
- Timeless styles designed for years of wear
- Size range: UK sizes 6–18
