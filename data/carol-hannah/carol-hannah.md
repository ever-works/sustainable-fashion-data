## Overview

- Exceptional craftsmanship
- Local manufacturing
- Personalized service

## Sustainability Practices

By creating all gowns made-to-order in their NYC studio, Carol Hannah eliminates waste from overproduction and ensures each dress is crafted specifically for its wearer. Local production supports skilled artisans and reduces transportation emissions.
