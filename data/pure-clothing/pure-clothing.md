## Overview

Pure Clothing is an Irish brand offering certified vegan unisex clothing made sustainably with attention to every detail of the supply chain.

## Features

- Certified vegan unisex sweatshirts, hoodies, shorts and joggers
- Materials: certified organic cotton and recycled polyester
- Produced in Fairwear and Wrap certified factories
- Embroidered using wind power in Mayo, Ireland
- Recycled polyester labels stitched with organic thread
- Sustainable packaging: recycled tissue paper with soy-based ink, recycled card boxes with water-based ink, product information on seed paper

## Sizing

- XS to XL
