# Completedworks

Completedworks is a British jewelry brand crafting pieces from recycled gold and sterling silver, using recycled and sustainably sourced materials to minimize the environmental impact of fine jewelry production. Each sculptural piece combines artistic expression with verified ethical sourcing, creating heirloom-quality accessories without supporting destructive mining practices. Where conventional jewelry production relies on environmentally devastating new mining operations, Completedworks transforms existing precious metals into contemporary art — proving luxury can be beautiful without extraction.
