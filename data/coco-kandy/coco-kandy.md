# Coco & Kandy

Coco & Kandy is a Bulgarian brand crafting uncomplicated, warm-weather clothing from lower-impact materials with a fully transparent and regularly-visited supply chain. By manufacturing locally and personally auditing every supplier visit, the brand ensures ethical conditions and verifiable environmental responsibility. Where fast fashion's offshore supply chains hide exploitation and pollution behind layers of contractors, Coco & Kandy's transparent, locally-based production model proves that traceable, ethical fashion can deliver effortless summer style.
