## Overview

Ecksand Jewelry considers itself a pioneer in sustainable jewelry, based in Montreal.

## Sustainability Features

- All jewelry made using recycled gold
- Lab-grown diamonds
- Recycled diamonds of the highest quality as an eco-conscious option
- Recycled diamonds are reclaimed and not mined or produced, leaving virtually zero environmental and social impact

## Services

- Ready-made jewelry collections
- Custom piece building service
- Addresses the issue of 180 million tons of waste dumped into oceans annually by mining companies
