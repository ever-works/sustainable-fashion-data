# Reet Aus

Reet Aus is an Estonian fashion designer specializing in garments upcycled from deadstock fabric sourced directly from factories in Bangladesh and Poland, pioneering an industrial-scale approach to upcycling. The brand's innovative production model intercepts textile waste at the source — factory floors — and transforms surplus material that would otherwise be incinerated or landfilled into contemporary fashion pieces. Where fast fashion brands treat deadstock as worthless waste and continually produce new fabrics, Reet Aus proves that factory-level upcycling can achieve commercial scale while making a genuine dent in the industry's staggering material waste problem.
