## Overview

Alohas is a sustainable on-demand fashion brand producing made-to-order pieces in externally audited Spanish factories, eliminating overstock waste through a pre-order model.

## Sustainability Features

- Made-to-order production eliminates overstock
- Externally audited factories in Spain
- Pre-order model prevents waste
- Reduced carbon footprint through local production
- No excess inventory

## Product Range

Fashion-forward footwear, clothing, and accessories produced on demand through the pre-order system.

## Pricing

Pre-order pricing with discounts for early orders, making sustainable fashion more accessible.
