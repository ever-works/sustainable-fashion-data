## Overview

Leanne Marshall creates wedding dresses made in the USA using only repurposed and recycled materials. The designer employs zero-waste cutting techniques to create sustainable luxury bridal wear.

## Sustainability Practices

Leanne Marshall uses exclusively repurposed and recycled materials, requiring no new fabric production. Zero-waste cutting techniques ensure minimal material waste, creating luxury bridal wear that is completely sustainable.

Offers wedding dresses and bridal gowns made from repurposed and recycled materials with zero-waste techniques.
