## Overview

Gobi Cashmere is one of Mongolia's largest vertically integrated cashmere brands, based in the country where the world's finest cashmere originates. From sourcing raw fibers to spinning yarn and sewing finished garments, every stage of production happens in-house.

## Vertical Integration

- Complete control over the supply chain from raw fiber to finished product
- In-house sourcing, spinning, and sewing operations
- Enhanced traceability at every production stage
- Support for local craftsmanship and Mongolian textile heritage

## Quality & Craftsmanship

- Premium Mongolian cashmere fibers
- Quality control maintained throughout the entire production process
- Combination of traditional techniques with modern manufacturing

## Product Range

- Cashmere sweaters, cardigans, and knitwear
- Scarves, wraps, and accessories
- Full wardrobe pieces for men and women
- Outerwear and coats crafted from cashmere
