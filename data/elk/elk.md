## Overview

Elk was founded in Melbourne in 2004, creating bi-annual collections where simplicity and sustainability meet innovation. The Australian brand offers contemporary fashion with ethical production practices.

## Features

- Founded in 2004
- Melbourne-based brand
- Bi-annual collections
- Simplicity meets sustainability
- Innovation focus
- Contemporary fashion
- Ethical production
- Australian brand
- Quality materials

## Sustainability Practices

Elk combines simplicity with sustainability through innovative approaches to design and production. The brand's bi-annual collection schedule reduces overproduction while maintaining contemporary, ethical fashion.

## Product Range

Offers contemporary women's fashion with timeless, simple designs.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Australian sustainable contemporary fashion.
