## Overview

GOHEMP is a Japanese brand specializing in hemp clothing along with other eco-friendly and organic materials. The brand creates contemporary casual wear that minimizes environmental impact through sustainable fiber choices.

## Features

- Hemp as primary fiber
- Eco-friendly materials
- Organic fabrics
- Japanese brand
- Contemporary casual wear
- Sustainable production
- Minimal environmental impact
- Natural fibers focus
- Quality construction

## Sustainability Practices

GOHEMP uses hemp and other eco-friendly, organic materials throughout its collections. Hemp is one of the most sustainable fibers available, requiring minimal water and no pesticides while improving soil health. The brand combines these materials with Japanese quality craftsmanship.

## Product Range

Offers contemporary casual clothing including shirts, pants, jackets, and accessories made from hemp and organic materials.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Japanese hemp and organic clothing.
