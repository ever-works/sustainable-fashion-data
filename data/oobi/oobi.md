## Overview

Oobi is an Australian girl's fashion, swimwear, homewares, and lifestyle brand that creates stylish clothes for children while prioritizing environmental responsibility.

## Features

- Uses low-impact non-toxic dyes throughout its entire range
- No animal products used in any products
- Uses sea freight to transport products to reduce climate impact
- Product range includes girls' fashion, swimwear, and homewares
- Also available pre-loved through Retykle
