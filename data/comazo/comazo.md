# Comazo

Comazo is a sustainable intimates brand crafting comfortable non-wired bras and underwear from GOTS-certified organic cotton with a minimal elastane blend for optimal fit. By using certified organic cotton, the brand eliminates the toxic pesticides that dominate conventional cotton production while delivering all-day comfort without underwire constraints. Where mainstream lingerie relies on synthetic blends treated with harsh chemicals, Comazo proves that GOTS-certified organic basics provide healthier contact materials for everyday wear.
