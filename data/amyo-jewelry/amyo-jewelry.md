## Overview

AMYO Jewelry is an NYC-based women-made jewelry brand creating minimalist pieces with a focus on sustainability and ethical production. All pieces are handmade in their New York studio.

## Materials & Sourcing

- **Precious Metals**: 14K gold, sterling silver, and gold vermeil
- **US Sourcing**: Materials sourced from US businesses
- **Recycled Metals**: Recycles all metal scraps from production
- **Quality Focus**: Premium materials for lasting quality

## Ethical Production

- **Handmade in NYC**: All jewelry crafted in their New York City studio
- **Women-Made**: Created by women artisans
- **Fair Wages**: Guaranteed fair wages for all makers
- **Small Batch**: Thoughtful, small-batch production

## Design Philosophy

AMYO specializes in minimalist, timeless jewelry designed to be worn every day. The pieces are versatile, elegant, and built to last a lifetime.

## Environmental Commitment

By recycling metal scraps and sourcing locally, AMYO minimizes environmental impact while supporting the US jewelry supply chain.

## Pricing

Accessible to mid-range pricing for handmade fine jewelry made in the USA.
