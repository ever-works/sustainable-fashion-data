## Overview

Yogi Bare brings accessible and ethically-made yoga mats and tools to the world. The name represents stripping back and reflecting the brand's eco credentials. Founded by Kat, the brand emphasizes sharing, connecting, and learning through yoga.

## Ethics & Sustainability

- Ethically made production
- Close factory partnership in China
- Regular third-party audits for welfare, safety, and product safety
- Fair pay for team and factory workers
- Quality control and standards maintained year-round
- High ethical, safe, and happy working environment

## Product Range

- Yoga mats
- Acupressure mats
- Blocks
- Straps
- Wheels
