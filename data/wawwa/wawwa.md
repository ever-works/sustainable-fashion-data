## Overview

WAWWA is a Manchester-based social enterprise creating sustainable streetwear fashion. The brand combines urban aesthetics with eco-friendly materials and ethical manufacturing practices.

## Sustainability Features

- Organic cotton clothing
- Recycled polyester and materials
- Renewable energy in production
- Ethical manufacturing
- Social enterprise model
- Carbon-conscious operations
- Sustainable packaging

## Materials

- GOTS certified organic cotton
- GRS certified recycled polyester
- Recycled cotton
- Eco-friendly dyes and prints
- Water-based inks

## Product Range

- T-shirts and tops
- Hoodies and sweatshirts
- Jackets
- Trousers and joggers
- Accessories

Unisex and gender-neutral designs with bold graphics and contemporary streetwear style.

## Social Impact

As a social enterprise, WAWWA is committed to creating positive change in the fashion industry, supporting fair labor practices and environmental sustainability.

## Production

- Made in Portugal and UK
- Fair Trade certified factories
- Transparent supply chain
- Small batch production

## Pricing

Affordable sustainable streetwear, typically £25-£80 per item.
