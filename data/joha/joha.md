## Overview

Joha is a trusted Scandinavian brand that has been going for over 50 years, specialising in cosy ethical baselayers. All products are made exclusively from wool and/or organic cotton with no synthetic fibres.

## Features

- 100% natural fibres only (wool and organic cotton) — no synthetics
- Oeko-Tex Standard 100 certification — every component including thread tested for harmful substances
- Scandinavian independent certification for responsible wool sourcing
- Established brand with over 50 years of experience
- Size range: XS–XXL
