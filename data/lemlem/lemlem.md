## Overview

Ethiopian supermodel Liya Kebede established the resortwear and swimwear brand, lemlem, driven by her discovery of traditional weavers losing jobs in her country due to diminishing demand. The brand is an artisan-driven, Africa-made brand that celebrates women and nature.

## Sustainability Practices

- Artisan-driven production supporting traditional Ethiopian weavers
- Africa-made manufacturing
- Handwoven textiles preserving traditional craftsmanship
- Employs local artisans and provides stable employment

## Design Philosophy

The brand is characterized by vibrant colors and stripes, offering casual and chic pieces easy to wear on all occasions. Designs celebrate women and nature while preserving traditional weaving techniques.
