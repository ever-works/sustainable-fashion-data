## Overview

Mon Coeur creates vibrant, durable children's clothing for ages 0-10 in Portugal using 100% recycled materials. The brand combines bold designs with environmental responsibility.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Reclaimed Cotton**: 100% recycled cotton from textile waste
- **Recycled Fishing Nets**: Transforms ocean waste into durable fabric
- **Zero Virgin Materials**: Completely circular material sourcing

## Renewable Energy Production

The Portuguese factory operates exclusively on renewable energy, resulting in:
- Zero fossil fuel consumption
- Minimal carbon footprint
- Clean energy manufacturing

## Design Philosophy

Mon Coeur creates bold, colorful clothing designed for active children. The styles are:
- Comfortable and flexible
- Durable for play
- Gender-neutral options
- Easy to care for

## Environmental Impact

By using only recycled materials and renewable energy, Mon Coeur represents a truly circular approach to children's fashion, diverting textile waste and ocean plastics from landfills.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable, European-made children's clothing.
