## Overview

Zou Xou is an Argentinian footwear brand working with local artisans to create timeless, handcrafted shoes in small batches using traditional techniques and quality natural materials. The brand offers made-to-order options to reduce waste and ensure perfect fit.

## Product Range

- Mary Janes
- Mules
- Ballet flats and glove flats
- Slingbacks
- Loafers
- Lace-ups
- Flat and heeled sandals

## Sizing

- Women's 4.5 – 11.5 (see sizing details)

## Price Range

- $195 – $360

## Sustainability & Ethics

- Made-to-order production reduces waste
- Small batch manufacturing
- Works with Argentinian artisans
- Uses quality natural materials
- Traditional handcrafting techniques

## Key Features

- Inspired by classic shoe styles like pumps, slip-ons, and mules
- Custom made-to-order option available for non-standard sizes
- Designed for both comfort and timeless style
- BIPOC owned and woman owned
- Shoes built to last, reducing need for frequent replacements
