## Overview

Owl is a Spanish brand creating sustainable, urban clothing and underwear for both men and women. Their commitment to natural materials extends to unique undyed cotton pieces.

## Sustainability

- Underwear made from organic cotton
- Collection features 100% undyed organic cotton items that prevent allergic reactions
- Natural coloring derives directly from the cotton plant itself
- No chemical dyes used on undyed pieces, reducing water pollution and chemical exposure
- Natural variation in color between items is celebrated as part of the natural process

## Products

- Men's underwear and urban clothing
- Women's underwear and urban clothing
- Undyed organic cotton collection with natural color variations

## Unique Approach

- The color in undyed pieces comes directly from the cotton plant
- Natural variation means each item can have a slightly different shade
- Philosophy that nature never creates two of the same things

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for organic cotton products.
