## Overview

Local Eclectic is a curated jewelry marketplace launched in 2013 by Alexis Nido-Russo, spotlighting unique, accessible designs from independent artists worldwide. The woman-led team handles everything from photography to fulfillment in-house, supporting small-scale craftsmanship across the globe.

## Sustainability & Ethics

- 94% of carried brands are women-founded/owned
- Recycled materials used across product offerings
- Supports independent designers and small businesses
- Most brands have teams of five or fewer people
- In-house fulfillment ensures thoughtful handling
- Custom sizing and design options available

## Product Range

- Rings, charms, necklaces, earrings, bracelets, anklets
- Jewelry sets
- Both fine and fashion jewelry
- Exclusive collections not available elsewhere
- Custom sizing and design options

## Pricing

- $20 (anklet) – $6,500 (vintage diamond ring)
- Wide range to suit various budgets
