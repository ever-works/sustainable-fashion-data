## Overview

My Bump creates USA-made maternity clothing that combines style, comfort, and sustainability. Every piece is crafted domestically, supporting American manufacturing and reducing carbon footprint from shipping.

## Made in USA

- **Domestic Production**: All clothing manufactured in the United States
- **Local Jobs**: Supports American garment workers
- **Reduced Shipping**: Lower carbon emissions from domestic production
- **Quality Control**: Oversight of ethical production standards

## Eco-Conscious Materials

- **Sustainable Fabrics**: Environmentally responsible material choices
- **Quality Focus**: Durable materials that last through pregnancy
- **Comfort Priority**: Soft, breathable fabrics for expecting mothers

## Ethical Production

- **Fair Labor**: American workers paid fair wages
- **Safe Conditions**: Ethical working environments
- **Transparency**: Clear visibility into production process

## Design Philosophy

My Bump creates maternity wear that allows women to feel confident and comfortable throughout pregnancy. The versatile pieces work for various occasions from casual to professional.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for USA-made sustainable maternity clothing.
