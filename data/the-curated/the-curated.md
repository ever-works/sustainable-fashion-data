## Overview

The Curated is a direct-to-consumer luxury brand that specializes in high-quality cashmere sweaters. By eliminating traditional retail middlemen and markups, the brand offers premium cashmere knitwear at more accessible price points while maintaining luxury quality standards.

## Business Model

- Direct-to-consumer approach
- Eliminates traditional retail markups
- Focus on premium cashmere products

## Products

- Cashmere sweaters
- Luxury knitwear garments
