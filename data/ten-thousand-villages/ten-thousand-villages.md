## Overview

Ten Thousand Villages pioneered the global fair trade movement, connecting artisans from developing countries with consumers worldwide.

## Fair Trade Impact

- Pioneer of maker-to-market fair trade
- Creates opportunities for artisans in developing countries
- Prioritizes workers and environment
- Helps consumers shop with intention
- Long-term partnerships with artisans
- Community development focus

## Sustainability Features

- Fair trade practices
- Ethical sourcing
- Supports traditional craftsmanship
- Environmental responsibility
- Social impact focus

## Product Range

Handcrafted ethical jewelry and home goods made by artisans worldwide using traditional techniques.

## Heritage

Decades of fair trade leadership, building sustainable livelihoods for artisan communities globally.

## Mission

Provides vital fair income to artisans in developing countries by marketing their products in a just and direct manner.
