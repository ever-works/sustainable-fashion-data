## Overview

Kente Gentleman is an Ivorian brand that designs clothes hand-in-hand with local artisans, producing with a fashion philosophy that is as stylish as it is ethical.

## Sustainability Practices

- Collaborates directly with local Ivorian artisans
- Ethical production philosophy
- Supports local craftsmanship and community
- Combines traditional techniques with contemporary design

## Design Philosophy

The brand focuses on creating garments that are both fashionable and ethically produced, working closely with artisans to maintain quality and cultural authenticity.
