## Overview

Apiece Apart was founded by two creatives who designed collections that are easy to mix and match, solving the problem of having a full closet but nothing to wear. The brand consistently works toward improving sustainability practices and offers a preloved clothing archive for buying and selling used pieces.

## Features

- Collections designed for effortless mixing and matching
- Versatile pieces that coordinate across collections
- Size-inclusive range from XXS to 2X (up to US 16)
- Preloved clothing archive for buying and selling
- Eco-friendly packaging with compostable mailers
- FSC-certified materials
- GOTS-certified materials

## Sustainability

- Donates 1% of profits to environmental causes
- Eliminated 133,825 plastic polybags from landfills
- Compostable mailers for all shipping
- Eco-friendly materials throughout collections
- Regenerative cotton partnerships
- Plant trees initiatives
- Second-hand/resale program through brand archive

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for contemporary wardrobe staples.
