## Overview

Just Black Denim produces All-American contemporary women's jeans in every style imaginable. Each pair is handmade in Los Angeles, then dyed using sustainable liquid indigo ink that cuts back on water usage and prevents quick fading compared to regular ink.

## Sustainability

- Sustainable waterless indigo ink dyeing process
- Reduced water usage and chemical pollution
- Handmade in Los Angeles
- Pre-loved denim recycling program
- 1% for the Planet member
- Extended garment longevity through fade-resistant dyeing

## Product Range

- Flare, straight, skinny jeans
- Jean skirts
- Contemporary women's denim in every style
- Sizes: X-Small – Large (up to US size 31)
- Extended sizing available in select styles
