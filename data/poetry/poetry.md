## Overview

Poetry is a London-based clothing brand rooted in a love for natural fabrics, timeless design, and careful craftsmanship. The brand creates understated, luxurious pieces using premium natural fibers, working closely with expert mills and long-standing suppliers.

## Sustainability

- 99.29% natural materials used across collections
- HRDD (Human Rights Due Diligence) supply chains
- Audited factories
- Eco-friendly packaging
- Partnership with FSC-certified suppliers
- Focus on enduring, long-lasting garments

## Product Range

- Elevated everyday garments
- Cashmere, linen, and alpaca pieces
- Premium natural fiber collections
- Timeless wardrobe staples
- Sizes: XS – L
