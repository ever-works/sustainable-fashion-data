## Overview

Loomy Jewels is a Sydney-based Instagram-run small business dedicated to reselling gorgeous vintage engagement rings, offering rare gems and one-of-a-kind pieces while giving jewellery a second life.

## Sustainability Practices

- All pieces are resurrected second-hand vintage jewellery
- One-off unique pieces with no new production needed
- Operates with minimal overhead and environmental footprint
- Extends the lifecycle of existing jewellery rather than creating new pieces

## Product Focus

Rare vintage engagement rings, unique gemstones, and one-of-a-kind jewellery pieces.

## Pricing

Prices vary piece-to-piece.
