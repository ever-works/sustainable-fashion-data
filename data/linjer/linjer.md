## Overview

Linjer is a family-owned jewelry brand known for its birthstone collections and ethically made fine jewelry. The brand combines sustainability with vibrant, personalized design, offering pieces for both everyday wear and special occasions.

## Sustainability & Ethics

- Recycled precious metals (sterling silver, solid gold)
- Ethically sourced conflict-free gemstones and diamonds
- Carbon-neutral shipping
- FSC-certified recycled packaging
- High-quality materials built for longevity

## Product Range

- Birthstone necklaces, earrings, and bracelets
- Stones including opal, citrine, tanzanite, and garnet
- Rings, earrings, necklaces, bracelets
- Jewelry sets
- Gold, silver, diamond, and pearl pieces for daily and special occasions
