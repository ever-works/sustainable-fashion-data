## Overview

ndrwr by Woody is a sustainable underwear line created by the Belgian brand Woody, offering comfortable everyday basics at accessible prices.

## Materials

- Made from lyocell, a natural cellulosic fiber produced from wood pulp
- Lyocell is soft, supple, moisture-absorbent, and moisture-repellent
- Ideal fabric for everyday wear against the skin

## Products

- Timeless underwear basics
- Suitable for all shapes and sizes
- Designed for everyday comfort

## Pricing

Affordable pricing tier, making sustainable underwear accessible to a broader audience.

## Availability

Available at select sustainable retail stores such as Supergoods in Antwerp and Mechelen.
