## Overview

Saturday Swimwear is a unique small-batch brand where each suit is handmade by the owner as she travels across the United States. The brand uses ECONYL regenerated nylon sourced from waste materials like discarded fishing nets.

## Features

- Handmade by brand owner
- ECONYL regenerated nylon
- Made from discarded fishing nets
- Small-batch production
- Travel-inspired designs
- Waste reduction focus
- Unique, artisan quality
- Ocean waste recovery
- Made in USA

## Sustainability Practices

Saturday Swimwear uses ECONYL, a regenerated nylon made from waste materials including discarded fishing nets recovered from oceans. Each handmade suit contributes to ocean cleanup efforts while providing unique, small-batch swimwear made with care in the United States.

## Product Range

Offers handmade bikinis and one-piece swimsuits in limited quantities with unique designs.

## Pricing

Premium pricing reflecting handmade construction and sustainable materials.
