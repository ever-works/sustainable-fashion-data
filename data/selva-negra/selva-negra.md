## Overview

Selva Negra is a sustainable fashion and home decor brand founded by Mexican Filipino American designer Kristen Gonzalez. Materials are sustainably sourced locally in LA, with additional imports from Japan and Turkey.

## Sustainability Practices

- Uses compostable packaging materials
- Fabrics sourced from deadstock wholesalers to reduce new fabric production
- Materials sustainably sourced locally in LA
- Includes upcycled section with hair accessories, underwear, and unique blouses

## Product Range

- Sustainable clothing including upcycled pieces
- Funky home decor such as multicolored rugs and artistic vases
- Accessories and household items
