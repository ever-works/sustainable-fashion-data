## Overview

The Elder Statesman is a Los Angeles-based luxury knitwear brand that brings a bold, colorful aesthetic to ethical cashmere. Known for playful luxury, the brand takes high-grade cashmere and transforms it into statement pieces with hand-dyed finishes.

## Production

- All production done locally in Los Angeles
- Hand-dyed finishes for unique, one-of-a-kind colorations
- High-grade cashmere sourced responsibly
- Small-batch, local manufacturing ensuring oversight and quality

## Design Philosophy

- Bold, vibrant color palettes and playful patterns
- Statement knitwear with personality
- Luxury pieces that blend ethical responsibility with expressive design

## Product Range

- Cashmere sweaters and cardigans
- Scarves, accessories, and lounge pieces
- Seasonal collections featuring innovative dye techniques
