## Overview

Rave Review is a Swedish brand that transforms textiles of all kinds—from clothes to bedding to home goods—into fun, elevated designs. The brand gives new life to pre-existing materials through creative upcycling.

## Features

- Swedish upcycling brand
- Transforms various textiles
- Uses clothes, bedding, home goods
- Fun, elevated designs
- Pre-existing materials only
- Creative reconstruction
- Unique pieces
- Zero new textile production
- Contemporary aesthetic

## Sustainability Practices

Rave Review exclusively uses pre-existing textiles, upcycling materials from various sources including clothing, bedding, and home goods. This approach eliminates textile waste and requires no new fabric production while creating unique, one-of-a-kind fashion pieces.

## Product Range

Offers upcycled clothing and accessories, with each piece being unique due to the nature of upcycled materials.

## Pricing

Premium pricing for unique upcycled designer fashion.
