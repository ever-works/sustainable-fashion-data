## Overview

The Social Outfit is a sustainable fashion brand in Australia where ethical clothing is made by refugees in Sydney. The social enterprise provides employment, training, and empowerment while creating contemporary sustainable fashion.

## Features

- Made by refugees in Sydney
- Social enterprise model
- Employment opportunities
- Training programs
- Empowerment focus
- Australian brand
- Ethical clothing
- Contemporary fashion
- Community impact

## Sustainability Practices

The Social Outfit combines environmental sustainability with social impact, employing refugees in Sydney and providing training and empowerment opportunities. The brand creates contemporary fashion while addressing both environmental and social challenges.

## Product Range

Offers contemporary women's clothing made by refugees in Sydney.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Australian social enterprise fashion.
