## Overview

Gap is a well-known apparel retailer that has expanded its organic cotton offerings in recent years. While not a fully sustainable brand, it provides organic cotton basics at budget-friendly prices, making healthier fabric choices more accessible.

## Materials Used

- Organic cotton

## Sustainability Initiatives

- Washwell program uses 20% less water than traditional methods
- Recycled packaging for mailers
- Supplier sustainability programs monitoring environmental and labor standards in factories

## Size Range

XXS to XXL

## Notable Products

- 100% Organic Cotton Chambray Shirt
- Organic Cotton Vintage Soft T-Shirt
- Organic Cotton Vintage Soft Long Sleeved T-Shirt

## Pricing

Budget-friendly, with frequent sales making organic cotton pieces affordable. Widely available both online and in physical stores.
