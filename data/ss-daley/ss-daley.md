## Overview

SS Daley is a British fashion label that emerged in 2020, quickly gaining attention for its distinctive take on the English dandy aesthetic. The brand combines traditional tailoring with a modern, sustainable approach. Harry Styles is a notable supporter and investor in the brand.

## Key Features

- Reimagines the traditional English dandy aesthetic with a contemporary, sustainable lens
- Prioritises responsibly-sourced materials across all collections
- Features an Orange Label line made exclusively from upcycled textiles
- Creates one-of-a-kind pieces through upcycling techniques
- Backed by high-profile investor and patron Harry Styles
- Has grown significantly since its 2020 debut

## Pricing

Contemporary designer pricing, with upcycled Orange Label pieces at a premium for their unique, one-of-a-kind nature.
