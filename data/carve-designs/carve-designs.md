## Overview

Born from a love of surfing, adventure, and the great outdoors, Carve Designs creates clothing for real women with real bodies and bold spirits. What began as a search for better boardshorts on a surf trip to San Pancho has evolved into a full beach lifestyle brand committed to effortless style and sustainability.

## Sustainability

- 95% of collection crafted from recycled, organic, and eco-friendly materials
- Swimsuits spun from plastic bottles
- Organic denim
- Eco-friendly packaging
- Members of Ocean Conservancy nonprofit and Sustainable Apparel Coalition
- OEKO-TEX certified materials
- Upcycled and recycled materials throughout

## Product Range

- Beach lifestyle wear
- Swimwear
- Organic denim
- Casual everyday clothing
- Sizes: XXS – XL (up to US 16)
