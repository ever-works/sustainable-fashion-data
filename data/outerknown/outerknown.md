## Overview

Co-founded by surfing legend Kelly Slater, Outerknown blends style with sustainability in surf fashion.

## Sustainability Features

- Fair Trade Certified clothing
- Uses ECONYL® regenerated nylon
- Eco-friendly materials throughout
- Transparent and ethical sourcing
- Full supply chain transparency
- Environmental consciousness focus

## Ocean Protection

- Partnerships with Ocean Conservancy
- Funds beach cleanups
- Environmental initiatives
- Ocean protection advocacy
- Community engagement

## Materials

- ECONYL® regenerated nylon from ocean waste
- Eco-friendly materials
- Sustainable fabrics
- Recycled materials

## Product Range

Surf wear, casual clothing, and outdoor apparel combining performance with environmental responsibility.

## Mission

Transforming surf industry through sustainable practices while protecting oceans that inspire the brand.
