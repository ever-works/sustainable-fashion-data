## Overview

LALAM Morocco is a sustainable fashion brand deeply rooted in Morocco's rich cultural identity. Influenced by post-colonial ideologies, the brand collaborates with traditional Souk vendors to keep independent stores thriving.

## Key Practices

- Partners with traditional Souk vendors to support independent stores
- Shares a fair amount of profit back with shop owners
- Creates exclusive pieces that carry rich cultural stories
- Supports the vibrant culture and artisan community of Morocco
- Each piece expresses unique taste while preserving Moroccan cultural identity

## Sustainability

- Supports local artisan communities and traditional retail ecosystems
- Promotes fair profit distribution to shop owners
- Reduces reliance on mass-produced fashion through culturally significant, handcrafted pieces
- Preserves Moroccan cultural heritage and traditional retail practices
