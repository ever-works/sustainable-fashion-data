## Overview

ReCreate is a New Zealand brand making boutique clothing that transforms lives through fair employment, training, and empowerment. The social enterprise uses lower-impact materials including organic cotton.

## Features

- Social enterprise model
- Fair employment focus
- Training and empowerment programs
- New Zealand brand
- Organic cotton materials
- Lower-impact fabrics
- Boutique clothing
- Life transformation mission
- Ethical production

## Sustainability Practices

ReCreate combines environmental sustainability with social impact, using organic cotton and lower-impact materials while providing fair employment, training, and empowerment opportunities. The social enterprise model ensures positive community impact.

## Product Range

Offers boutique women's clothing made from organic cotton and sustainable materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for New Zealand social enterprise fashion.
