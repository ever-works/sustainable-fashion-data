## Overview

Unspun creates custom-fit jeans and denim shorts using 3D body scanning technology, eliminating waste through made-to-order production.

## Sustainability Features

- Made-to-measure reduces overproduction
- Custom sizing eliminates fit issues and returns
- On-demand manufacturing
- Reduces environmental footprint significantly
- Zero inventory waste
- Minimizes water and energy use

## Custom Fit Technology

- 3D body scanning for perfect fit
- Custom measurements for every customer
- Crafted according to individual body shape
- Comfortable and flattering fit guaranteed
- Reduces returns and waste

## Materials

- Sustainable denim
- Eco-friendly fabrics

## Product Range

Custom-fit jeans and denim shorts for men, made-to-order based on individual measurements.

## Innovation

Combines technology with traditional craftsmanship to create perfectly fitting jeans while minimizing environmental impact through demand-based production.
