# The RealReal

## Overview
The RealReal is an online marketplace for authenticated luxury consignment, founded in 2011. It allows users to buy and sell pre-owned luxury fashion, including clothing, accessories, fine jewelry, watches, and home decor.

## Features
- Authentication of luxury items by experts
- Wide range of designer brands
- Free shipping and returns for buyers
- Consignment service where sellers receive a percentage of the sale price
- Physical retail locations in select cities

## Pricing
The RealReal takes a commission on each sale, which varies based on the item's price and category. Sellers receive the remainder. Buyers pay the listed price plus applicable taxes.

## Sustainability Impact
By facilitating the resale of luxury fashion, The RealReal extends product lifecycles, reduces waste, and lowers the environmental impact associated with producing new goods.
