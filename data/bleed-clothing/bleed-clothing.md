## Overview

Bleed Clothing is a German sustainable streetwear brand that transitioned from cotton basics to hemp-rich collections, maintaining 100% vegan standards across all products.

## Features

- Hemp-rich collections replacing conventional cotton
- 100% vegan across all products
- German sustainable fashion with high manufacturing standards
- Completely transparent supply chains
- Ethical production certified by Fairtrade and Fair Wear Foundation
- Streetwear and outdoor apparel focus

## Materials

- Hemp: core fiber, replacing conventional cotton
- Organic cotton
- Recycled polyester
- Natural plant-based materials

## Sustainability

- Fairtrade certified
- Fair Wear Foundation partner
- Transparent supply chain allowing consumers to verify environmental and ethical claims
- Hemp cultivation using minimal water
- Zero animal products in any garment
- Vegan certified

- Fair Wear Foundation
