## Overview

Moissanite & Co. was founded to offer fine jewelry that is both affordable and ethically responsible, specializing in premium moissanite earrings and other elegant pieces.

## Sustainability Features

- Moissanite as a sustainable alternative to traditional mined stones
- Conflict-free sourcing
- Environmentally conscious practices
- High brilliance at more accessible price points
- Trusted destination for shoppers seeking stunning yet ethical jewelry choices
