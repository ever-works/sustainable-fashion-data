## Overview

Rewilder creates unique hand-crafted purses from salvaged and upcycled industrial materials, transforming waste products into stylish accessories.

## Features

- Purse fabrics made from salvaged filter cloth from beer manufacturing plants
- Handles made from salvaged climbing rope from local gyms
- Hand-crafted production
- Upcycled and salvaged materials
- Unique, one-of-a-kind designs
