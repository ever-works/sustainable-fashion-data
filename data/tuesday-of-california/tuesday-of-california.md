## Overview

Tuesday of California creates inclusive fashion with adjustable components designed to accommodate more body shapes. The brand uses deadstock fabrics and sustainable materials for versatile, inclusive designs.

## Features

- Adjustable design components
- Accommodates various body shapes
- Deadstock fabric use
- Sustainable materials
- Size-inclusive approach
- California brand
- Versatile designs
- Waste reduction
- Innovative fit technology

## Sustainability Practices

Tuesday of California uses deadstock fabrics, giving new life to surplus materials. The brand's innovative adjustable designs accommodate various body shapes, reducing returns and waste while making sustainable fashion more inclusive.

## Product Range

Offers women's clothing with adjustable features made from deadstock and sustainable materials.
