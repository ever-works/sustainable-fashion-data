## Overview

Thought (formerly Braintree) is a UK-based clothing brand that ensures fabrics, garment design, manufacturing, and delivery are carefully considered to minimize environmental impact. Founding member of the Ethical Fashion Forum.

## Sustainability Practices

- Materials: wool, organic cotton, bamboo, hemp, recycled polyester, rayon, Tencel, and modal
- Azo-free dyes (free from harmful carcinogens)
- Environmentally friendly finishes
- Wool sourced from farms compliant with Animal Welfare policy
- Each piece made in the same country, avoiding multiple shipments
- Slow shipping options with environmental consideration
- Founding member of the Ethical Fashion Forum

## Sizing

- 6 – 18

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for ethical everyday clothing.
