## Overview

AUGUSTE is a Byron Bay-based vintage-inspired fashion brand known for effortless, versatile pieces suitable for beach and bar settings. The brand also offers children's and teen styles with matching family sets.

## Features

- Vintage-inspired aesthetic
- Easy, breezy designs for versatile styling
- Matching styles available for women and children
- Byron Bay-based brand

## Sustainability Practices

- Transparent supply chain reporting
- Uses eco-friendly fabrics
- Actively working to reduce environmental footprint
- Shares sustainability journey openly with customers

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing reflecting sustainable production and Australian design.
