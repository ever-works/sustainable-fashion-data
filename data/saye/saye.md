## Overview

SAYE is a Spain-based sustainable fashion brand focused on reducing the planet's carbon footprint through the use of ecological yarns and natural dyes.

## Product Range
- Sneakers
- Apparel
- Accessories

## Materials & Practices
- Locally-acquired ecological yarns
- Natural dyes
- Upcycled sneakers made from recycled mango fruit, organic cotton, recycled Polyurethane (PU) leftovers, environmentally-certified leather
- Testing new recycled materials for future introduction
