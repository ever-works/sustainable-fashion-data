## Overview

Organic Basics is a Copenhagen-based sustainable basics brand known for creating high-quality essentials with minimal environmental impact.

## Sustainability Features

- Certified B Corporation
- Part of 1% for the Planet
- Transparent impact reporting
- Uses low-impact materials
- Focuses on durability and longevity
- Sustainable supply chain

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Recycled nylon and polyamide
- TENCEL™ Lyocell
- LENZING™ ECOVERO™
- Recycled plastic from oceans and landfills (swimwear)

## Product Range

Basics including underwear, activewear, loungewear, t-shirts, and swimwear for men and women.

## Philosophy

Creates timeless, minimalist basics designed to last, reducing the need for constant replacement and minimizing fashion waste.
