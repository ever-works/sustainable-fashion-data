## Overview

Míe is a Nigerian fashion brand that creates elegant linen dresses and matching sets with romantic details like puff sleeves, playful frills, and feminine colors. All products are made-to-order by local seamstresses in Nigeria, with worldwide shipping available.

## Features

- Elegant linen dresses and matching sets
- Romantic design details including puff sleeves and frills
- Femine color palettes
- Made-to-order production model
- Handmade by local seamstresses in Nigeria
- Worldwide shipping available
- Size-inclusive range from XS to 3X

## Sustainability

- Natural fabric materials
- Made-to-order production reduces waste from overproduction
- Handmade by local artisans in Nigeria
- Brand actively seeking ways to improve production processes to meet client needs without harming the environment

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for handmade linen garments.
