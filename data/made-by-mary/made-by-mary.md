## Overview

Made by Mary is a woman-founded jewelry brand known for thoughtful, beautiful artisan-made pieces that are lightweight and durable enough for continuous everyday wear. The brand's Live-In Collection features pieces designed to withstand sleep, play, swim, and daily activities.

## Sustainability & Ethics

- Ethically sourced materials
- Fair wages for artisans
- Woman-founded company
- Some pieces made to order, reducing waste
- Recycled materials used in production

## Product Range

- Necklaces, earrings, rings, bracelets, anklets
- Bridal jewelry
- Jewelry sets
- Live-In Collection: durable everyday pieces
- Ready-to-ship bolder statement pieces

## Pricing

- $24 (mini hoop earrings) – $300 (solid 14K gold chain necklace)
- Accessible pricing for everyday fine jewelry
