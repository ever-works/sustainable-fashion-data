## Overview

Dick Moby is an ethical eyewear brand based in Amsterdam, set out to make sunglasses and eyewear environmentally friendly.

## Sustainability Practices

- 97% recycled acetate used for all frames (the remaining 3% is black ink)
- Microfiber lens cloths made from recycled PET
- Glasses cases made from recycled leather

## Products

- Sunglasses with recycled acetate frames
- Eyewear accessories including lens cloths and cases

## Philosophy

The brand believes that a pair of sunglasses can be made from sustainable materials like wood, glass, and metal, and has set out to prove that eyewear can be both stylish and environmentally responsible.
