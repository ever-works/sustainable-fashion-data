## Overview

All Things Fiona Lily sells locally screen-printed apparel made from faulty stock garments that would otherwise be dumped.

## Sustainable Practice

- Uses faulty stock garments that would otherwise go to landfill
- Prevents textile waste through upcycling

## Production

- Locally screen-printed in Ireland

## Product Range

- Sweatshirts
- Crewnecks
- T-shirts

## Sizing

- S to XL
