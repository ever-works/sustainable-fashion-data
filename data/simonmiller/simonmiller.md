## Overview

SIMONMILLER is a lifestyle brand based in Los Angeles that inspires vibrant and lively living. CEO and Creative Director Chelsea Hansford ensures each piece strikes a balance between boldness and timelessness.

## Features

- Playful luxury aesthetic combining boldness with timeless design
- Contemporary fashion pieces with unique flair
- Signature items include the Loch Poplin Button Up and versatile wardrobe staples
- Versatile pieces suitable for office or vacation wear
- Strong emphasis on quality materials and craftsmanship

## Ethics

- Uses natural materials across collections
- Made in the USA (Los Angeles)
- Woman-owned and led by CEO/Creative Director Chelsea Hansford
