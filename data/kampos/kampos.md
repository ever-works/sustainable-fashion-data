## Overview

Kampos is a luxurious Italian brand making unique pieces from recycled plastic bottles, fishing nets, and organic fabrics. The brand transforms ocean waste into high-end sustainable fashion with exceptional quality.

## Features

- Recycled plastic bottles
- Recycled fishing nets
- Organic fabrics
- Italian luxury brand
- Ocean waste transformation
- Unique designs
- High-end sustainable fashion
- Premium quality
- Environmental cleanup

## Sustainability Practices

Kampos contributes to ocean cleanup by using recycled plastic bottles and fishing nets recovered from marine environments. Combined with organic fabrics, the brand creates luxury fashion that benefits both environmental health and style.

## Product Range

Offers luxury clothing and accessories made from recycled ocean waste and organic materials.

Luxury pricing for Italian sustainable fashion from recycled materials.
