## Overview

Born in Byron Bay, Afends leads the way in hemp fashion. The Australian brand draws inspiration from the environment, streetwear, and surf culture to create sustainable casual wear.

## Features

- Byron Bay origins
- Hemp fashion leader
- Eco-friendly materials
- Surf culture inspired
- Streetwear aesthetic
- Environmental inspiration
- Australian brand
- Sustainable casual wear
- Contemporary designs

## Sustainability Practices

Afends champions hemp as a sustainable fiber choice, utilizing this eco-friendly material throughout its collections. Hemp requires minimal water and no pesticides while improving soil health, making it one of the most sustainable fibers available.

## Product Range

Offers casual clothing including t-shirts, shirts, pants, dresses, and accessories inspired by surf and streetwear culture.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Australian sustainable hemp fashion.
