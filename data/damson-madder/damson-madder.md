## Overview

British brand Damson Madder was founded in 2020 to challenge the waste and harm associated with the fast fashion industry. Damson Madder focuses on slower production, thoughtful design, and transparency.

## Features

- Clothes designed with quality and longevity in mind
- Sustainable fabrics used: organic cotton, responsibly sourced fibres, and lower-impact fabrics
- Reduced use of water, energy, and harmful chemicals
- Traceable supply chains
- Audited factories
- Clear code of conduct protecting workers' rights
- Supports women-led environmental charities
- Size range: UK sizes 6–20
