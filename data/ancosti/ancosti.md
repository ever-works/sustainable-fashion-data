## Overview

Inspired by the natural elements and Ancosti Island in the Northern Côte-Nord region of Canada, Ancosti features sweaters, dresses, outerwear, and more. Most pieces come in contemporary neutrals, with some featuring abstract patterns and unique silhouettes designed to be super wearable for many occasions.

## Sustainability

- Uses eco-friendly materials including organic cotton, recycled polyester, and wool
- Eco-friendly packaging
- In-house manufacturing for products, exercising control from sketches to final products
- Working to find even more sustainable materials
- Budget friendly

## Products

- Sweaters (starting at $105)
- Dresses
- Outerwear

Notable pieces include the One-Shoulder Asymmetrical Top (wearable as a light sweater in spring or layered in colder months) and the Front Split Hem Cardigan (simple, chic, and versatile for many outfit combinations).
