## Overview

Soft Revolt is an Amsterdam-based brand focused on innovative, comfortable bras without underwire that still offer substantial support across sizes B to G.

## Sustainability

- Bras are knitted in one piece as much as possible, minimizing waste fabric after production
- Uses biodegradable elastane for stretch components
- All products manufactured in the EU under ethical working conditions
- Focuses on reducing textile waste through seamless knit technology

## Products

- Non-wired bras with seamless construction
- Full size range: cups B through G
- Innovative knit technology for support without underwire

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing, direct-to-consumer with online webshop.
