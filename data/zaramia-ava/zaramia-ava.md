## Overview

Zaramia Ava is against fast fashion and creates timeless, durable garments designed to be worn any time of year. Most garments are handmade to order in the UK.

## Sustainability Practices

- Timeless, seasonless designs meant to last
- Most garments handmade to order in the UK
- Uses end-of-roll and organic fabrics
- Pattern cutting and fabric cutting done to eliminate waste
- Recycles all fabric off-cuts, samples, pattern paper, and card

## Sizing

- Made to order sizes

## Pricing

Mid-to-premium pricing for custom-made sustainable fashion.
