## Overview

Founded in 1996 by Kirsten Weihe-Keidel, Sense Organics brings durable, sustainable baby clothes to all families.

## Sustainability Features

- 100% organic cotton
- GOTS-certified materials
- Vegan clothing
- Renewable energy in supply chain
- Wind Energy Project support
- Fair labor practices
- 'Good' sustainability rating

## Certifications

- GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard)
- Rated 'Good' by Good On You

## Materials

- 100% organic cotton
- GOTS-certified materials
- Vegan fabrics

## Product Range

Sustainable clothing for babies and toddlers with focus on durability and comfort.

## Environmental Initiatives

Incorporates renewable energy through Wind Energy Project and other sustainability efforts in supply chains.

## Heritage

Nearly 30 years of commitment to organic, sustainable baby clothing with comprehensive approach to ethics and environmental stewardship.
