## Overview

Lovetrigger is an Amsterdam urban brand creating contemporary streetwear from organic cotton with a strong social mission. The brand combines style with positive social impact.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Organic Cotton**: GOTS certified organic cotton
- **Chemical-Free**: No harmful substances in production
- **Quality Fabrics**: Soft, durable materials

## Social Impact Production

Produced by **Makers Unite**, a B Corp certified social enterprise in the Netherlands:
- **Employment for Youth**: Provides jobs for young people from vulnerable backgrounds
- **Skills Training**: Teaches garment production skills
- **Fair Wages**: Ensures living wages for workers
- **B Corp Certified**: Meets highest standards for social and environmental performance

## Product Range

- Hoodies
- T-shirts
- Jackets
- Streetwear essentials

All pieces feature contemporary urban designs with a focus on comfort and versatility.

## Dutch Production

Made in the Netherlands, supporting local social enterprises and reducing carbon footprint from shipping.

Mid-range pricing for organic cotton streetwear with social impact.
