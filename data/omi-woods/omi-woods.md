## Overview

Omi Woods offers stunning contemporary heirloom jewelry inspired by Africa and its diaspora with ethical gold sourcing.

## Sustainability Features

- Fair trade gold from small-scale artisanal mines
- Sourced from African mines
- Conflict-free fine metals
- Ethical sourcing practices
- Supports artisanal mining communities

## Cultural Heritage

- Name 'Omi' means 'water' in Yoruba
- Inspired by founder's Jamaican-Ashanti-Maroon heritage
- African diaspora influence
- Contemporary heirloom designs

## Materials

- Fair trade gold from Africa
- Conflict-free metals
- Ethically sourced materials

## Product Range

Contemporary heirloom jewelry designed to be passed down through generations.

## Mission

Connects African heritage with ethical sourcing, supporting small-scale artisanal miners while creating beautiful, meaningful jewelry.
