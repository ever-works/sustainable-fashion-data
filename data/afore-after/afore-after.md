## Overview

Afore After is an Irish brand making timeless, long-lasting clothing in Poland from sustainable materials with minimal environmental impact.

## Materials

- Closed-loop rayon
- Fabric sourced in Europe close to factory to minimise emissions

## Production

- Made in Poland
- Printed using water-based inks in a low-energy, closed-loop system
- Company purchases own fabric and components reducing financial burden on factory partner

## Sustainable Components

- Biodegradable buttons
- Recycled polyester labels
- Recycled polyester thread and lining
- Swing tags made from recycled paper

## Shipping

- Climate neutral shipping
- Plastic-free packaging

## Sizing

- XS to XL
