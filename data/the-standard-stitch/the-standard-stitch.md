## Overview

The Standard Stitch creates clothes "as gentle on the earth as they are on your body" with inclusive sizing up to 5X. The brand focuses on comfortable basics made from sustainable materials.

## Features

- Inclusive sizing up to 5X
- Recycled cotton
- Organic cotton
- Gentle on earth and body
- Comfortable basics
- Tees and tanks
- Loungewear essentials
- Soft, sustainable fabrics
- Size-inclusive values

## Sustainability Practices

The Standard Stitch uses recycled and organic cotton to create basics that are gentle on both the environment and the wearer. The brand's commitment to size inclusivity ensures that sustainable fashion is accessible to people of all body types.

## Product Range

Offers t-shirts, tanks, and loungewear in sizes up to 5X, focusing on everyday essentials.
