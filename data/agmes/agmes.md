## Overview

AGMES is an NYC-based jewellery brand that takes a hands-on, considered approach to every stage from sampling to production to shipping, rooted in the ethos that less — waste, emissions, and consumption — is more.

## Sustainability Practices

- **Recycled metals**: Uses recycled metals in almost all pieces
- **Zero-waste approach**: Returns scrap metals to suppliers for reuse
- **Local manufacturing**: Production based in New York City
- **Local business support**: Supports local NYC businesses for prototyping, production, and packaging
- **Considered production**: Hands-on approach minimises excess at every stage

## Design Philosophy

Minimalist, considered approach to jewellery creation with emphasis on reducing waste, emissions, and consumption.
