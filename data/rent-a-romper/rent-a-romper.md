## Overview

Rent A Romper operates a completely circular business model designed specifically for children's clothing. Parents can rent wardrobes that are worn, returned, and exchanged for the next size up, dramatically reducing waste from children's rapidly outgrown clothing.

## Features

- Completely circular model
- Rental-based service
- Exchange for next size up
- Eliminates clothing waste
- Cost-effective for parents
- Curated wardrobe options
- Quality children's clothing
- Convenient returns
- Sustainable alternative to buying

## Sustainability Practices

Rent A Romper's circular model addresses one of fashion's most wasteful segments: children's clothing that is quickly outgrown. By enabling families to rent, wear, and return clothing for the next size, the service maximizes garment utilization and eliminates the waste associated with disposable fast fashion for kids.

## How It Works

Parents select a wardrobe, receive the clothes, wear them during that size phase, return them, and receive the next size up, creating a continuous cycle of use without ownership.

## Pricing

Subscription-based rental model offering cost savings compared to purchasing new children's clothing for each growth stage.
