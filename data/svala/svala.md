## Overview

Svala creates handcrafted vegan handbags in Los Angeles using high-quality European materials and fair labor practices.

## Sustainability Features

- Made in Los Angeles
- Fair treatment of workers
- PVC-free materials
- Uses cotton and recycled polyester for linings
- European fabric sourcing
- Diverse vegan leather alternatives
- Eco-friendly materials

## Materials

- European fabrics
- PVC-free vegan leather
- Cotton linings
- Recycled polyester linings
- High-quality cruelty-free materials

## Product Range

Handcrafted vegan handbags in various styles offering cruelty-free alternatives to traditional leather.

Made in Los Angeles ensures fair wages, safe working conditions, and supports local economy while reducing transportation emissions.
