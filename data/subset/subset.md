## Overview

Founded in New York by Cayla O'Connell Davis and Lauren Sagadore in 2018, Subset (formerly Knickey) creates sustainable intimates from organic materials.

## Sustainability Features

- GOTS-certified organic cotton
- 100% carbon-neutral operations
- Invests in carbon offsets through Climate Neutral
- Recycling program accepts underwear from any brand
- Oeko-Tex certification
- Fair Trade certification
- Affordable at $17 per pair

## Recycling Program

- Takes back old underwear from any brand
- Items shredded and turned into insulation, carpet padding, or furniture stuffing
- Extends product lifecycle
- Reduces textile waste

## Materials

- GOTS-certified organic cotton
- TENCEL™
- Recycled polyester
- Regenerated and recycled nylon

## Product Range

Organic cotton underwear, bras, and loungewear for women and men with expanded sizing options.
