## Overview

Up-fuse is a sustainable accessories brand founded in Egypt that upcycles discarded plastic bags into fashionable, functional bags and lifestyle products. The brand's mission is to demonstrate the beauty and value of upcycled materials while reducing plastic pollution.

## Features

- Bags and accessories made from upcycled plastic waste
- Handcrafted by local artisans
- Each product diverts hundreds of single-use plastic bags from waste streams
- Backpacks, totes, laptop bags, and travel accessories
- Unique designs that showcase the upcycled material aesthetic
- Supports local artisan communities

## Impact

- Prevents plastic waste from entering landfills and oceans
- Creates fair-wage employment for local craftspeople
- Raises awareness about upcycling and circular fashion
