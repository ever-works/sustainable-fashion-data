## Overview

FRSKÉ is a sustainable fashion brand that specialises in timeless outerwear and versatile wardrobe staples. The brand is known for its duster coats, such as the Oko Duster, designed to be layered and styled for various occasions.

## Key Products

- Oko Duster Coat — a versatile outerwear piece in the brand's signature style

## Sustainability

FRSKÉ has been independently rated as "Good" or "Great" by Good On You's sustainability assessment methodology, reflecting the brand's commitment to positive impact on people, the planet, and animals.
