## Overview

Nudie Jeans is a Swedish denim brand committed to circular fashion and making jeans that last.

## Sustainability Features

- Free repair service at all stores and repair shops
- Recycling program for pre-used denim
- Focus on circularity and longevity
- Transparent sustainability reporting
- Reduces harmful chemicals during production
- Uses organic cotton

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Recycled denim
- Low-impact materials

## Product Range

Denim jeans, jackets, and shorts for men and women in various fits and styles.

## Repair Culture

Offers free repairs for life, encouraging customers to wear jeans longer and develop a relationship with their denim rather than replacing it.
