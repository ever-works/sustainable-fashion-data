# Citizen Wolf

Citizen Wolf is an Australian brand using revolutionary custom-fit technology to produce made-to-measure t-shirts from certified lower-impact fabrics including organic cotton, hemp, and Australian Merino wool — all handmade in Sydney. Each garment is produced only after receiving the customer's measurements, virtually eliminating the overproduction and sizing waste endemic to conventional apparel manufacturing. Where fast fashion brands waste 30-40% of production on unsold inventory, Citizen Wolf's made-to-order model ensures zero excess stock, perfect fit, and garments built to last.
