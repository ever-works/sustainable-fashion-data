## Overview

Loulette Bride makes all wedding gowns to order in their Brooklyn studio using recycled poly lining and natural fabrics including cotton, silk, hemp, linen, and wool. For every dress sold, a tree is planted in that bride's name with One Tree Planted.

## Features

- Made to order in Brooklyn
- Recycled poly lining
- Natural fabrics: cotton, silk, hemp, linen, wool
- One tree planted per dress
- Brooklyn studio production
- Partnership with One Tree Planted
- Sustainable bridal wear
- Local manufacturing
- Custom production

## Sustainability Practices

Loulette Bride creates all gowns made-to-order, eliminating waste from overproduction. The brand uses recycled polyester lining and natural fibers throughout, and plants one tree for every dress sold through their partnership with One Tree Planted.

## Product Range

Offers wedding dresses made from natural and recycled materials in their Brooklyn studio.

## Pricing

Mid to premium bridal pricing for made-to-order sustainable gowns.
