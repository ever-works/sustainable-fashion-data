## Overview

Nina Rein is a German brand creating sustainable business attire in Europe under fair conditions. The brand offers professional workwear with ethical production and quality materials designed for modern professionals.

## Features

- Sustainable business attire
- Made in Europe
- Fair production conditions
- German brand
- Professional workwear
- Quality materials
- Ethical manufacturing
- Modern professional style
- European production

## Sustainability Practices

Nina Rein maintains fair production conditions throughout European manufacturing, ensuring ethical labor practices. The brand creates durable, professional clothing designed for longevity in the workplace.

## Product Range

Offers business attire and professional workwear for women including blazers, trousers, dresses, and shirts.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for European-made sustainable business wear.
