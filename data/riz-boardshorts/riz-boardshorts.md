## Overview

Riz Boardshorts combines British craftsmanship with a passion for sustainability to create stylish swimwear for men. The brand is known for its commitment to using recycled materials without compromising on design or quality.

## Product Range

- Men's boardshorts and swimwear
- Ocean and coastal-inspired prints

## Sustainability Practices

- Boardshorts made from recycled plastic bottles
- Focus on reducing ocean plastic waste
- Sustainable manufacturing processes

## Design Features

- Unique prints inspired by marine life and coastal landscapes
- British craftsmanship and attention to detail
- Functional and stylish designs
