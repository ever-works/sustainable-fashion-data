## Overview

MANDALA is Germany's first yoga label and a pioneer in eco-friendly fashion. The brand's collections combine trends, innovation and sustainability to create high-quality yoga clothing.

## Ethics & Sustainability

- Fair trade practices
- GOTS certified organic cotton
- Recycled fabrics
- Vegan clothing

## Product Range

- Leggings (with practical pockets)
- Tops
- Bras
- T-shirts
- Pants
- Shorts
- Long sleeves
