## Overview

GLDN is a woman-owned jewelry brand focused on personalized pieces that celebrate meaningful symbols and moments. The brand combines affordability with sustainability, offering nearly all pieces in recycled silver, gold fill, rose gold fill, or solid gold options.

## Sustainability & Ethics

- 90% recycled materials used across product line
- 85% of pieces are made to order, reducing waste
- Donates 10% of profits to charitable causes
- Handmade production process

## Product Range

- Necklaces, rings, bracelets, earrings, anklets
- Jewelry sets
- Personalized pieces with birth flowers, zodiac constellations, and monograms
- Options in recycled silver, gold fill, rose gold fill, or solid gold

## Pricing

- $22 (heart charm) – $520 (snake chain necklace)
- Multiple metal options at various price points for accessibility
