## Overview

Icebreaker is a trusted sustainable outdoor adventure brand well known for its merino wool products and long-lasting apparel designed for practical, adventure-ready performance.

## Sustainability Features

- Ethically made apparel
- Merino wool as a natural, renewable, and biodegradable fibre
- Long-lasting products designed to endure a lifetime of use
- Purpose-built materials selected for activity-specific performance

## Products

- Merino wool socks designed for various outdoor activities
- Socks for hiking, skiing, and warm-weather adventures
- Range of blends depending on the intended purpose
- Wide selection of merino wool apparel beyond socks
