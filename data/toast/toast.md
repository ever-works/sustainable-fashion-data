## Overview

Toast is a slow fashion brand championing beautifully designed, durable women's clothing since 1997. The brand is Welsh-rooted but draws inspiration from cultures and craft traditions around the world.

## Sustainability Initiatives

- Circular initiatives including free repairs
- Clothes swap events for customers
- Archive collection of creatively repaired pieces
- Collaborations with global artisans, weavers, and mills
- Slow production, thoughtful design, traceable supply chains
- Women-led environmental charity support

## Product Range

- Women's clothing including tops, dresses, knitwear, and outerwear
- Accessories

## Pricing

Mid-range to premium.

## Sizing

Range includes extended sizes.
