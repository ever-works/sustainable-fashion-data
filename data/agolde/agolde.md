## Overview

Agolde is a denim-focused sustainable fashion brand dedicated to reducing the environmental impact of jean production. The brand specializes in crafting denim using either organic or regenerative cotton, offering a conscious alternative in an industry traditionally known for high water usage and chemical-intensive processes.

## Sustainability Highlights

- Uses organic cotton in denim production
- Incorporates regenerative cotton sourcing to improve soil health and reduce emissions
- Minimizes environmental cost associated with conventional denim production

## Product Range

- Skinny jeans
- Straight-leg jeans
- Various denim styles addressing current fashion trends

## Design Philosophy

Agolde aims to make popular denim trends — such as the revival of skinny jeans and straight-leg styles — more environmentally responsible by using sustainable cotton sourcing without compromising on style or fit.
