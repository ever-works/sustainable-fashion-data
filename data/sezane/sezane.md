## Overview

Sézane is one of the first French fashion brands to achieve B Corp certification, combining Parisian style with sustainability commitments.

## B Corp Achievement

- B Corp certified since 2021
- Maintained certification through 2026
- Annual factory audits under B Corp standards
- Strong social and environmental performance

## Sustainability Features

- 80-85% of collection uses lower-impact materials
- GOTS-certified organic cotton
- RWS-certified wool
- Products certified by 5 industry certifications
- Tier 1 factories audited annually

## Certifications

- B Corporation
- GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard)
- Oeko-Tex
- FSC (Forest Stewardship Council)
- RWS (Responsible Wool Standard)
- RMS (Responsible Mohair Standard)

## Philanthropy

- DEMAIN philanthropic program since 2018
- Raised over 4.5 million euros for charitable causes

## Product Range

Ready-to-wear, leather goods, accessories, jewelry, and fragrance with sustainable Parisian style.
