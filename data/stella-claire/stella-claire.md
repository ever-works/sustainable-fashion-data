## Overview

Stella Claire is a New Zealand fashion label committed to ethical and sustainable practices. The brand creates timeless, quality clothing designed to last, supporting slow fashion principles and environmental responsibility.

## Sustainability Practices

- Transparent supply chain
- Ethical manufacturing
- Local production where possible
- Environmentally responsible materials
- Minimal waste production
- Quality over quantity
- Fair labor practices

## Materials

- Sustainable fabrics
- Natural fibers
- Organic cotton
- Low-impact materials
- Eco-friendly blends
- Responsibly sourced textiles

## Design Philosophy

- Timeless, classic styles
- Quality construction
- Designed to last
- Versatile pieces
- Seasonless collections
- Minimal, elegant aesthetic

## Product Range

- Dresses and skirts
- Tops and blouses
- Knitwear
- Pants and trousers
- Outerwear
- Coordinating separates

## Made in New Zealand

- Local production support

## Slow Fashion Values

- Investment pieces

- Durable quality

## Target Customer

Women seeking elegant, sustainable clothing that transcends trends and seasons, valuing quality, ethics, and environmental responsibility.

## Pricing

Mid to premium range, reflecting ethical production and quality materials, typically NZD $120-$350 per item.
