## Overview

Tripulse is a Swedish activewear brand on a mission to create high-performing workout clothing that's kind to the planet.

## Sustainability Features

- TENCEL™ Lyocell from sustainably sourced eucalyptus
- Closed-loop, non-toxic manufacturing
- Free from harmful chemicals
- Plastic-based fiber-free
- Cradle-to-Cradle certified stretch
- Compostable Roica® V550 elastane

## Materials

- TENCEL™ Lyocell
- Sustainably sourced eucalyptus and wood pulp
- Cradle-to-Cradle certified Roica® V550
- Compostable elastane

## Product Range

High-performance activewear including leggings, sports bras, t-shirts, tops, and shorts for everyday athletes.

## Production

- Ethically made in Europe
- Closed-loop manufacturing
- Non-toxic processes

## Innovation

Silky-soft, breathable fabric with exceptional performance without plastic or harmful chemicals, proving sustainable can be high-performance.
