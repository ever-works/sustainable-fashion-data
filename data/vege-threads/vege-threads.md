## Overview

Vege Threads is a Melbourne label that makes all clothes entirely in Australia using organic and eco-friendly materials and dyes. The brand supports local manufacturing with transparent, ethical production practices.

## Features

- Made entirely in Australia
- Organic materials
- Eco-friendly dyes
- Melbourne-based brand
- Local manufacturing
- Transparent production
- Ethical practices
- Sustainable materials
- Australian-made fashion

## Sustainability Practices

Vege Threads maintains complete Australian production, using organic and eco-friendly materials throughout. The brand's use of eco-friendly dyes minimizes chemical pollution, while local manufacturing ensures ethical working conditions and reduces transportation emissions.

## Product Range

Offers casual clothing and basics made in Australia from organic materials with eco-friendly dyes.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Australian-made sustainable clothing.
