## Overview

ISTO. is a Portuguese fashion brand with a simple mandate: create clothes that transcend the seasons and work as staple items in your wardrobe all year round. Rather than releasing seasonal collections, ISTO. maintains one permanent collection and pursues quality over quantity to make its items permanently available.

## Features

- Single permanent collection rather than seasonal releases
- Timeless wardrobe staple designs
- Organic materials used throughout the range
- Full pricing transparency showing the true cost breakdown of each garment
- Made in Portugal
- Available in sizes XS-XL

## Products

- Everyday wardrobe essentials
- T-shirts, shirts, knitwear, and trousers
- Accessories

## Sustainability

- Uses organic materials
- Transparent pricing model showing true cost breakdown
- Permanent collection approach reduces overproduction
- Local Portuguese manufacturing
- Quality-over-quantity philosophy to extend product lifespan
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing with full transparency on cost breakdown.
