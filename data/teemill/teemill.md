## Overview

Teemill is a sustainable clothing brand dedicated to creating a circular economy. Their products are made using 100% organic cotton and their processing facilities are powered by renewable energy, including solar power.

## Remill Program

- Customers can donate old Teemill t-shirts to be recycled into new ones
- The program is powered by Rapanui
- Participants receive credits for future custom purchases
- Supports a closed-loop circular fashion system

## Sustainability Practices

- All products created using 100% organic cotton
- Processing facilities use renewable energy including solar power
- Circular economy model with recycling infrastructure
- Custom print-on-demand reduces excess inventory waste

## Pricing

Competitive pricing on organic cotton t-shirts; credits offered for recycled garments.
