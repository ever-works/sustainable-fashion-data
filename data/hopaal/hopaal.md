## Overview

Hopaal is an ethical French brand creating sustainable clothing from recycled materials. The brand specializes in collections made from recycled cotton and polyester with transparent, local European production.

## Features

- French sustainable brand
- Recycled cotton
- Recycled polyester
- European production
- Transparent supply chain
- Local manufacturing
- Casual sustainable wear
- Waste reduction
- Circular materials

## Sustainability Practices

Hopaal uses recycled cotton and polyester throughout its collections, diverting textile waste from landfills. Local European production ensures transparency, reduces transportation emissions, and supports regional manufacturing.

## Product Range

Offers casual clothing including t-shirts, sweatshirts, and basics made from recycled materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for French recycled sustainable fashion.
