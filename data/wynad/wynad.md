## Overview

Wynad clothing creates garments from natural fibres including vegan silk from lotus flower stalks, printed and manufactured in GOTS organic and Fairtrade certified factories. A percentage of the sale of some garments goes towards cooperatives and charities.

## Sustainability Practices

- Natural fibre materials including vegan silk from lotus flower stalks
- Manufactured in GOTS organic and Fairtrade certified factories
- A percentage of sales from some garments goes to cooperatives and charities
- Natural fibre printing techniques
