## Overview

Mila.Vert is a Slovenia-based brand making chic, minimalist clothes with a distinctly modern feel, produced in small batches based on a pre-order system to minimise waste.

## Features

- Pre-order production system to minimise waste
- Small-batch manufacturing
- Chic, minimalist design aesthetic
- Sizes XS-XL
- Avoids overproduction and its ethical/environmental issues
- Slovenia-based production
