# Facettes Studio

Facettes Studio is a Parisian brand crafting versatile wardrobe essentials centred around suits using exclusively upcycled materials with a complete ban on polyester and polyamide. Each collection is supported by in-house repairs and alterations services that extend garment lifespans far beyond the fast fashion norm. Where mainstream womenswear floods markets with virgin polyester, Facettes Studio's upcycling-first approach and repair-friendly design prove that Parisian elegance and circular fashion can coexist.
