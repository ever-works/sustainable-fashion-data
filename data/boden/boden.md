## Overview

Boden has spent nearly 30 years taking the UK by storm and has expanded worldwide. The brand excels in women's dresses for work, the beach, and everything in between, featuring stunning silhouettes and whimsical patterns.

## Sustainability Practices

- Fair trade production across factories in 15 countries
- BCI certified and sustainable cotton
- Ethical supply chain practices
- Eco-friendly options at every part of the supply chain
- Recycled materials incorporated into collections
- Eco-friendly packaging

## Product Range

- Women's dresses (work, casual, formal, beach)
- Men's chinos and apparel
- Children's clothing
- Knitwear and separates

## Size Range

US XS – XL; up to US 22

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for quality, responsibly made everyday wear.
