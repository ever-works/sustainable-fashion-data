# Fair Indigo

Fair Indigo creates timeless apparel from organic Pima cotton in Peru, ensuring workers receive fair wages and ethical treatment through long-term supply chain partnerships. By sourcing premium organic Peruvian Pima — the world's finest cotton — the brand delivers exceptionally soft basics without the pesticide contamination of conventional cotton farming. Where fast fashion exploits Peruvian artisans for bargain-basement prices, Fair Indigo's decades-long partnerships and organic Pima sourcing prove that classic, enduring design and genuine farmer welfare are inseparable.
