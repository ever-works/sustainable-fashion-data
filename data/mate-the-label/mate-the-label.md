## Overview

MATE the Label creates clean essentials with a mission to reduce toxic chemical exposure in fashion.

## Sustainability Features

- GOTS-certified organic fabrics
- Low-toxicity dyes and processes
- Designed to reduce chemical exposure for wearers and workers
- Non-toxic materials throughout
- Transparent about fabric sourcing
- Made with natural, organic materials

## Materials

- GOTS-certified organic cotton
- Organic fabrics
- Low-impact, non-toxic dyes
- Natural materials

## Product Range

Essentials including sweats, leggings, sports bras, t-shirts, loungewear, and full-coverage underwear. Known for clean, minimalist aesthetic.

## Health Focus

Prioritizes health and wellness by eliminating harmful chemicals, making clothing safe for sensitive skin and better for the environment.
