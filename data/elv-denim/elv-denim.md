## Overview

E.L.V. Denim is a benchmark for sustainable luxury, creating sophisticated denim from entirely upcycled materials.

## Sustainability Features

- Every garment made from 100% upcycled materials
- Uses discarded vintage denim from UK warehouses
- Cut by hand for zero waste
- Makers earn fair wages in London
- No new raw materials used
- Made-to-measure options available

## Materials

- 100% upcycled denim
- Discarded vintage denim
- Reclaimed materials

## Product Range

Modern, sophisticated denim including jeans, jean jackets, and custom pieces. Each item is unique due to the upcycled nature of materials.

## Craftsmanship

Every piece is handcrafted in London, ensuring quality while providing fair employment and minimizing environmental impact through complete material reuse.
