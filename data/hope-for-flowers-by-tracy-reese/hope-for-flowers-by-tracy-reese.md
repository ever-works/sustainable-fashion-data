## Overview

Founded by designer Tracy Reese in 2019, Hope for Flowers creates sustainable, feminine, and minimal designs.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses sustainable textiles exclusively
- Ethical production practices
- Focus on longevity and quality
- Minimal, timeless designs
- Environmentally conscious

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Linen
- Tencel™
- Cupro
- Sustainable textiles

## Product Range

Feminine, minimal ready-to-wear including dresses, tops, and separates with elegant, timeless aesthetic.

## Designer Heritage

Created by renowned Black designer Tracy Reese, bringing decades of fashion expertise to sustainable design.

## Philosophy

Proves sustainable fashion can be feminine, beautiful, and sophisticated while maintaining environmental and ethical standards.
