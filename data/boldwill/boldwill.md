## Overview

Founded in 2018, Boldwill pioneered plastic-free sportswear, creating high-performance activewear from natural plant-based materials.

## Sustainability Features

- 100% plastic-free sportswear
- Uses plant-based fabrics exclusively
- Local production in Europe
- Ethical labor practices
- Circular fashion commitment
- Take-back program for worn garments
- Healthy production standards

## Materials

- Hemp
- Eucalyptus
- Beechwood
- Organic cotton
- Bio-based materials
- No polyester or nylon

## Product Range

High-performance activewear and sportswear for training, yoga, and outdoor activities.

## Circular Model

Allows customers to return worn-out garments for recycling, ensuring materials stay in use and nothing goes to waste.

## Innovation

Proves that high-performance athletic wear doesn't require synthetic plastics, using natural materials with equivalent or better performance.
