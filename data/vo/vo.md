## Overview

VO (formerly known as Vegan Outfitters) is an entirely vegan clothing brand describing itself as the next generation of vegan apparel, aiming to be 100% vegan, ethical, and sustainable without compromising on style.

## Key Features

- 100% vegan brand
- Casual clothing including slogan print t-shirts, sweatshirts, jogging bottoms, and hoodies
- All garments made from vegan materials
- Made in ethical conditions
- Printed with eco-friendly water-based inks
- Plastic-free packaging
- 10% of profits donated to UK sanctuaries dedicated to rescuing farm animals from abuse and slaughter
