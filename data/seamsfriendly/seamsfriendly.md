## Overview

SeamsFriendly creates beautiful tailored pieces for all body types, focusing on function and utility in its designs. The brand uses lower-impact materials and offers custom sizing to ensure every customer finds the perfect fit.

## Features

- Reversible and convertible clothing
- Multiple looks from one garment
- Handcrafted in natural fabrics
- Custom sizing available
- Made-to-order production model

## Customization

Shoppers can customize designs to their exact preferences, including:
- Neck style and depth
- Sleeve length
- Garment length
- Fabric selection
- And more

## Sizing

Customisable sizing across all categories:
- Womenswear: Bust 26"-70", waist 19"-63", including petite, regular, and tall heights
- Menswear: Chest 34"-76" and above with free custom sizing
- Kids range: Bust 18"-28", waist 19"-26"
- Standard off-the-rack: XS-XL with custom sizing also available

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Linen
- Lower-impact natural fabrics

## Sustainability

- Made-to-order to eliminate waste
- Lower-impact materials
- Handcrafted by artisans
- Custom sizing reduces returns
- "The most sustainable wardrobe is a smaller one"
- Independently assessed by Good On You
