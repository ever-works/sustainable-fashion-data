## Overview

Palace Skateboards is a British skateboarding and streetwear brand that has become one of the most recognizable names in the streetwear scene. Founded by Lev Tanju in 2009, the brand has grown from a skateboarding label into a cultural phenomenon while incorporating sustainable practices into its product development.

## Sustainable Initiatives

- **Eco-friendly trims and badges**: Palace has implemented sustainable alternatives for product details and embellishments
- **Vegan footwear collaborations**: Partnered with Adidas on the Ultra Boost 21 vegan trainer, minimizing environmental impact while blending sportswear and streetwear aesthetics
- **Reduced animal-derived materials**: The brand has incorporated vegan materials into select product lines

## Product Focus

- Skateboarding apparel and accessories
- Streetwear collections with iconic branding and graphics
- Collaborative limited-edition releases with major sportswear brands
- Footwear designed with sustainability considerations

## Pricing

Premium streetwear pricing, with typical product ranges across apparel, accessories, and footwear at streetwear market rates.
