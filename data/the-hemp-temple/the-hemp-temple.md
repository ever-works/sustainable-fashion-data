## Overview

The Hemp Temple is an independent maker focused on handmade plant-dyed clothing from hemp and other natural fibers. The brand prioritizes minimizing its environmental footprint across all stages of production.

## Product Details

- **Occasions**: Casual wear, loungewear for women and men
- **Materials**: Hemp grown in the Himalayan soils of Bihar, India and Northern Nepal
- **Dyeing**: Natural/plant dyes from food waste and native botanicals, applied in India and Nepal
- **Production**: Handmade by an independent maker
- **Investment Level**: $$$

## Sustainability Focus

- Minimizes resource usage including plastic, dyes, electricity, land, and water
- Production capped by the development of own capabilities, resources, and timelines
- Clean production philosophy with continuous improvement goals
