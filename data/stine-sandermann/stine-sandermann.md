## Overview

Stine Sandermann is a Danish brand making everything by hand with leftover wool collected from sheep farms in rural Denmark. The brand creates unique knitwear through waste reduction and traditional craftsmanship.

## Features

- Handmade production
- Leftover wool from Danish farms
- Rural Denmark sourcing
- Unique knitwear
- Waste reduction
- Traditional craftsmanship
- Danish brand
- Locally sourced
- Artisan quality

## Sustainability Practices

Stine Sandermann collects leftover wool from sheep farms in rural Denmark, giving new life to materials that would otherwise go to waste. Each piece is handmade using traditional techniques, creating unique, sustainable knitwear.

Offers handmade knitwear made from leftover Danish wool.
