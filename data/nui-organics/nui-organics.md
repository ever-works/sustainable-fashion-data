## Overview

Nui Organics creates sustainable children's clothing using certified organic materials and ethical production. The brand prioritizes comfort, durability, and environmental responsibility for children's sensitive skin.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Organic Cotton**: GOTS and OEKO-TEX certified organic cotton
- **Merino Wool**: Ethically sourced, natural temperature-regulating fiber
- **Chemical-Free**: No harmful substances in fabrics

## Ethical Production

- **European & Indian Factories**: Produces in Lithuania, Bulgaria, and India
- **Green Energy**: Lithuanian factory operates on renewable energy
- **Fair Labor**: Ethical working conditions throughout supply chain
- **Quality Focus**: Durable clothing designed to last

## Certifications

- **GOTS Certified**: Global Organic Textile Standard
- **OEKO-TEX**: Tested for harmful substances

## Design Philosophy

Nui Organics creates simple, comfortable clothing that allows children to play freely while being gentle on their skin and the environment. The minimalist designs prioritize functionality and durability.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for certified organic children's clothing.
