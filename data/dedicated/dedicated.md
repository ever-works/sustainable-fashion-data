## Overview

Dedicated is a Swedish responsible streetwear brand committed to creating sustainable fashion for the entire family. The brand offers clothing for men, women, and children, all made with 100% organic cotton and mostly Fairtrade certified.

## Features

- 100% organic cotton clothing
- Mostly Fairtrade certified production
- Streetwear-inspired designs
- Collections for men, women, and children
- GOTS certification (Global Organic Textile Standard)
- Eco-friendly fabrics throughout entire range
- Urban and contemporary styles
- Ethical manufacturing practices
- Transparent supply chain

## Sustainability Practices

Dedicated uses eco-friendly fabrics including 100% organic cotton certified by Fairtrade and the Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS). The brand is committed to responsible production methods and maintaining ethical standards throughout its supply chain while offering accessible, stylish streetwear.
