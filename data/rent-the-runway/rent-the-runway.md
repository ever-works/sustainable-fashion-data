## Overview

Rent the Runway is a designer clothing rental platform offering monthly membership plans and one-time rentals. The service provides access to a wide array of clothing and formal dresses from hundreds of designer brands.

## How It Works

- Browse items with photos from previous renters to see how they fit on different body types
- Select desired size and receive a backup size included
- Option to purchase the clothing for permanent ownership
- Return rented items using provided shipping

## Sustainability Practices

- Offsets 100% of carbon emissions from shipments
- Diverts 90% of waste from landfill
- Displaces need for new clothing production
- Extends the lifecycle of designer garments
- Recycled materials used in packaging

## Pricing

- 5-item monthly subscription at $94/month
- Various subscription tiers available
