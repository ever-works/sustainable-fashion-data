## Overview

Tolu Coker is a British-Nigerian fashion designer whose work has been recognised with features on the British Vogue January 2024 cover. Her collections utilise deadstock and recycled materials to create retro-inspired tailored pieces.

## Key Features

- Creates retro-inspired designs using deadstock and recycled materials
- Tailored pieces that reflect both Nigerian and British heritage
- Designed with longevity in mind to resist fast fashion cycles
- Featured as British Vogue's January 2024 cover star
- Commitment to purposeful, heritage-inspired design

## Pricing

Contemporary designer pricing, reflecting the unique, heritage-inspired designs and sustainable material sourcing.
