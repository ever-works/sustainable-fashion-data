## Overview

FLEKS is a sustainable footwear brand specializing in slip-on shoes designed for everyday comfort and style. The brand focuses on using eco-friendly materials and construction methods to create shoes that are good for both the wearer and the planet.

## Features

- Slip-on design for convenience
- Flexible, lightweight construction
- Recycled and sustainable materials
- Ergonomic insoles for all-day comfort
- Minimalist, versatile aesthetics
- Easy to clean and maintain

## Materials

- Recycled fabrics and components
- Natural rubber outsoles
- Eco-friendly adhesives
- Breathable, sustainably sourced uppers

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing ($$), competitive for sustainable comfort footwear.
