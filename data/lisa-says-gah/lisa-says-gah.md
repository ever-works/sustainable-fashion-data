## Overview

Lisa Says Gah uses deadstock fabric and upcycles it into fresh and eclectic vintage-inspired pieces. Collections are made for those looking to stand out of the crowd while supporting conscious clothing brands. The brand carries not only their own label but many independent designer brands with similar aesthetics.

## Sustainability

- Ethically produced
- Many items made in California
- Natural and deadstock materials
- Circular fashion approach
- Recycled materials used
- Size inclusive offerings
