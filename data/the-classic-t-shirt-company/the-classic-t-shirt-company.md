## Overview

The Classic T-Shirt Company specializes in sustainably sourced cotton t-shirts, offering every variation of the perfect tee including crewnecks, v-necks, boat necks, box tees, long sleeves, and raglans in a wide array of colors.

## Sustainability

- GOTS-certified organic cotton grown in India
- Fair labor practices and ethical manufacturing
- Plastic-free shipping and packaging
- 1% of revenue given to charity
- Every item is 100% sustainably sourced

## Product Range

- Crewnecks, v-necks, boat necks, box tees
- Long sleeve and raglan styles
- Wide array of colors
- Durable, soft organic cotton
- Sizes: XS – XXL
