## Overview

Ka-Sha is an Indian fashion brand gaining global recognition for its unique and sustainable approach to clothing. The brand uses clothing as an expression of hand-craft and celebrates life through its collections.

## Key Practices

- **Recycling & Upcycling**: The core philosophy revolves around recycling, upcycling, mending, and repairing clothing and textiles to minimize wastage
- **Post-Production Waste Reduction**: The brand aims to reduce wastage in the post-production stage and creates innovative designs using unwanted textile scraps
- **Sister Label — Heart to Haat**: Launched as a complementary label inspired by indigenous ideologies of reusing, re-purposing, and reclaiming
- **B2B Waste Repurposing**: Actively helps other brands utilize their post-production wastage to repurpose it into usable products

## Cultural Impact

Ka-Sha's collections are designed to tell India's story while being part of the global narrative. The brand redefines what it means to be a responsible fashion brand by breaking boundaries and reducing wastage.
