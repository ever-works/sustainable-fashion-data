## Overview

Anekdot is a Berlin-based lingerie brand founded by Swedish designer creating handmade pieces from upcycled and sustainable materials. Each piece is crafted with care in small batches.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Upcycled Fabrics**: Repurposes pre-existing materials
- **Deadstock**: Uses surplus fabrics from fashion industry
- **ECONYL®**: Regenerated nylon from recycled fishing nets
- **Zero Waste**: Minimizes material waste through thoughtful design

## Ethical Production

- **Handmade**: Each piece individually crafted
- **Berlin Atelier**: Produced in brand's Berlin studio
- **Women-Owned Polish Factory**: Additional production in ethical factory
- **Small Batch**: Limited quantities for quality control

## Design Philosophy

Anekdot creates romantic, feminine lingerie that celebrates individuality and body positivity while minimizing environmental impact through upcycling and sustainable materials.

## European Craftsmanship

All production takes place in Europe (Germany and Poland), ensuring fair labor standards and supporting local craftsmanship.

## Pricing

Mid-range to premium pricing for handmade, upcycled lingerie.
