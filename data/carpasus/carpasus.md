## Overview

CARPASUS is a Swiss menswear brand specialising in fine shirts, ties, socks, and pocket squares. The brand uses GOTS certified cotton and manufactures locally to reduce its carbon footprint. It traces its supply chain and ensures workers are paid a living wage.

## Features

- Fine shirts, ties, socks, and pocket squares for men
- Manufactured locally in Europe (EU sizes 36-46)
- GOTS certified organic cotton
- Full supply chain traceability
- Living wage guaranteed for all workers

## Products (EU Sizes 36-46)

- Shirts (Oxford, Poplin, Chambray)
- Ties and pocket squares
- Socks
- Knitwear

## Sustainability

- GOTS certified cotton
- Local manufacturing reduces transportation emissions
- Supply chain transparency
- Living wage payment to all workers
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology
