## Overview

Neem London is on a mission to build "the cleanest men's wardrobe" through exceptional material choices and transparent, ethical production. The British brand uses organic cotton, regenerative ZQ merino wool, and recycled Italian fabrics to create timeless menswear essentials.

## Features

- Organic cotton materials
- Regenerative ZQ merino wool
- Recycled Italian fabrics
- British design and values
- Transparent supply chain
- Ethical production practices
- Timeless menswear essentials
- High-quality construction
- Sustainable material sourcing

## Sustainability Practices

Neem London prioritizes the cleanest possible materials, using organic cotton, regenerative ZQ merino wool that supports soil health and animal welfare, and high-quality recycled fabrics from Italy. The brand maintains complete transparency about its supply chain and production practices.

## Product Range

Offers men's essentials including shirts, knitwear, trousers, outerwear, and basics designed for longevity and versatility.

Premium pricing reflecting high-quality sustainable materials and ethical production.
