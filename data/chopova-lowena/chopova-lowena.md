## Overview

Chopova Lowena is a London-based fashion label best known for its cult carabiner skirts. The brand's folkloric-inspired designs blend Eastern European heritage, particularly Bulgarian, with unique craftsmanship and sustainable material sourcing.

## Key Features

- Famous for signature carabiner skirts that achieved cult status
- Incorporates upcycled and recycled fabrics across collections
- Many materials sourced from co-founder Emma Chopova's home country of Bulgaria
- Features flocked dresses and upcycled pieces showcasing artisanal craftsmanship
- Distinctive folkloric design aesthetic with avant-garde elements

## Pricing

Premium contemporary pricing reflecting the brand's unique craftsmanship, cult status, and innovative material sourcing.
