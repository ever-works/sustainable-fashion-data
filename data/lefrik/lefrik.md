## Overview

Lefrik is a Spanish brand creating backpacks, bags, and accessories from Recycled PET (RPET) fabric, manufactured locally in Madrid.

## Sustainability Features

- Recycled PET (RPET) from post-consumer plastic bottles
- 100% vegan leather alternatives
- Manufactured locally in Madrid
- Fair working conditions
- Low-emission local production

## Materials

- RPET (Recycled PET)
- Vegan leather
- Recycled materials

## Products

- Backpacks
- Tote bags
- Crossbody bags
- Accessories
