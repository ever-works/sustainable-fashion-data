## Overview

Yoloha creates eco-friendly, high-quality yoga products. Founded by Chris Willey after his passion for eco-friendly surfboards inspired him to develop cork yoga mats that are durable, high-performing, and non-slip. These mats outlast other natural materials and don't require wax for traction.

## Ethics & Sustainability

- 100% natural and organic cork material
- Carbon neutral operations
- Cork renewable and sustainable
- Cork yoga mats made from renewable cork bark and recycled rubber
- 55% of Plant Foam made from sugarcane
- 70% of yoga mats are renewable materials
- Shipping using only recycled or recyclable cardboard and kraft paper
