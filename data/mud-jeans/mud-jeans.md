## Overview

MUD Jeans pioneered circular denim, proving that fashion can operate on a fully circular model.

## Sustainability Features

- World's first circular denim brand
- Recycles worn-out jeans into new ones
- Uses 92% less water than average jeans
- Lease A Jeans program allows customers to rent jeans
- Take-back program for old jeans
- Closed-loop production system

## Materials

- Recycled denim
- Organic cotton
- Post-consumer recycled materials

## Product Range

Denim jeans, jackets, and shorts for men and women in various fits and styles.

## Circular Model

Customers can lease jeans, return them when done, and MUD Jeans either repairs them for resale or recycles them into new denim, creating a truly circular system.
