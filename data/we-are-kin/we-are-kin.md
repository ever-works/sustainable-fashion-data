## Overview

We Are Kin is a British-based slow fashion brand that handmakes its clothes in London. The label focuses on inclusive sizing, timeless design, and manufacturing in limited runs to reduce waste, offering custom-made clothes for tall or petite heights.

## Features

- Handmade in London
- Custom-made for tall or petite heights
- Inclusive sizing from UK 4-26
- Timeless design philosophy
- Limited production runs to minimise waste

## Sustainability

- Local manufacturing in London reduces carbon footprint
- Limited production runs to reduce waste
- Timeless designs to extend garment lifespan
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Pricing

Premium pricing for London-handmade, custom-tailored womenswear.
