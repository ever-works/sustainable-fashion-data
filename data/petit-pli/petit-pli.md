## Overview

Petit Pli designs award-winning clothes that grow with children. The brand is engineered for play, planet and purpose, blending aerospace innovation with more ethical manufacturing to reshape fashion for the next generation.

## Features

- Clothes engineered to grow with children, reducing waste and the need for frequent replacements
- Blends aerospace innovation techniques with ethical manufacturing
- Award-winning design approach
- Focus on sustainability and reducing fashion waste in children's clothing
