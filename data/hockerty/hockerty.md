## Overview

Hockerty is a menswear brand focused on sustainability through made-to-order production, eliminating inventory surplus and minimizing textile waste.

## Sustainability Practices

- Every garment produced only after purchase, eliminating clothing surplus
- Minimizes textile waste through on-demand manufacturing
- Reduces environmental impact associated with mass production

## Products

- Personalized suits
- Custom dress shirts
- Bespoke dress shoes

## Key Features

- AI-based measurement tools for perfect fit
- Traditional tailoring techniques blended with modern technology
- Made-to-order production model
- Conscious style expression for men
