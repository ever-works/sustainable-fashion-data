## Overview

Gentle Herd is a sustainable accessories brand focused on creating high-quality essentials with both environmental and social responsibility.

## Features

- Organic cotton and recycled materials
- Donates one item for every purchase through 3,500+ giving partners
- Focus on comfort innovation
- Sustainable production methods

## Social Impact

- Partners with organizations supporting people experiencing homelessness
- Every purchase contributes to charitable giving

## Product Range

- Sustainable socks and accessories
- Items designed for everyday comfort and durability

## Pricing

- Mid-range pricing for sustainable accessories
