## Overview

OhSevenDays was started by Australian-Canadian Megan Mummery to promote slow fashion and the "power of circularity." Based in Istanbul, the brand reclaims end-of-roll fabrics from the city's garment factories to create sharp, everyday womenswear.

## Features

- Sharp, everyday womenswear designs
- Made in Istanbul
- Slow fashion philosophy
- Founded by Megan Mummery

## Materials

- End-of-roll fabrics from Istanbul's garment factories
- Reclaimed deadstock textiles
- Essentially makes slow fashion from fast fashion's leftovers

## Sustainability

- Promotes circularity through reclaiming waste fabrics
- Slow fashion approach
- Reduces textile waste by utilizing deadstock materials
- Based in Istanbul, utilizing local garment factory resources
- Independently assessed by Good On You

## Sizing

Available in sizes XS-XL, or with custom sizing options.
