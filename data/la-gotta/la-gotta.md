## Overview

La Gotta is a sustainable swimwear and wellness brand founded by Puerto Rican designer Valeria del Rey, who previously worked with several high-end brands including Oscar de la Renta.

## Sustainability Practices

- Swimwear and coverups provide UPF 50 sun protection
- Thread used in swimsuits, linen covers, and sarongs is derived from natural fibers
- Designed to protect beaches and waterways

## Product Range

- Swimwear with nature-inspired patterns and universally flattering colors
- Linen coverups and sarongs made from sustainable materials
- Wellness section with CBD-infused sunscreen and hair oils
