## Overview

Seek Collective is a US brand of thoughtfully made items with a dedication to transparency, authenticity, craft, and sustainability. The brand focuses on establishing connections between art, product, consumers, process, and makers.

## Features

- Thoughtfully made items with dedication to transparency and authenticity
- Items made in India through partnerships with like-minded communities and people
- Focus on craftsmanship and artisan techniques
- Most products available in XS-L with extended sizing range up to 4XL
- Strong emphasis on connecting consumers with makers and the production process
