## Overview

Oddobody creates 100% GOTS certified organic pima cotton underwear that is hand-harvested and produced in Peru. The brand is committed to creating the most sustainable underwear possible.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Organic Pima Cotton**: GOTS certified, hand-harvested in Peru
- **Compostable**: Garments fully compostable after removing elastic
- **Chemical-Free**: No harmful substances in production
- **Biodegradable**: Returns to earth at end of life

## Ethical Production

- **Women-Owned Factory**: Produced by women-owned factory in Peru
- **Fair Wages**: Ensures fair compensation for workers
- **Local Production**: Supports Peruvian textile industry
- **Quality Craftsmanship**: Hand-harvested and carefully produced

## Packaging

- **Plastic-Free**: All packaging free from plastic
- **Minimal Waste**: Simple, eco-friendly packaging
- **Compostable Materials**: Packaging can be composted

## Certifications

- **GOTS Certified**: Global Organic Textile Standard
- **100% Organic**: No synthetic materials

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for certified organic, compostable underwear.
