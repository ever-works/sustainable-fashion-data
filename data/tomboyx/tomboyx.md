## Overview

Founded in New York in 2013 by Fran Dunaway and Naomi Gonzalez, TomboyX creates clothing for everybody to feel comfortable in.

## Inclusivity

- Gender-inclusive designs
- Size-inclusive range
- Clothing for all body types
- Focuses on comfort for everyone
- Non-binary and inclusive marketing

## Sustainability Features

- Certified B Corporation
- OEKO-TEX® certified cotton
- BCI-certified cotton suppliers
- Recycled collection available
- Sustainable materials focus

## Materials

- TENCEL™ Modal from renewable beechwood pulp
- Closed-loop production system
- OEKO-TEX® certified cotton
- BCI-certified cotton
- Recycled materials

## Product Range

Underwear, bras, loungewear, and swimwear designed for comfort and inclusivity. Includes specialized items like recycled tucking underwear.

## Mission

Creates comfortable, sustainable clothing that anybody can feel confident wearing, regardless of gender expression or body type.
