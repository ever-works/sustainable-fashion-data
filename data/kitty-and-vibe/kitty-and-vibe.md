## Overview

Kitty and Vibe creates ethical swimwear from recycled materials in a woman-run factory in Bogota, Colombia. The brand combines sustainable fabric choices with fair labor practices and empowerment of female workers.

## Features

- 82% recycled polyester
- 18% X-Life Lycra for durability
- Made in woman-run factory
- Bogota, Colombia production
- Ethical labor practices
- Women's empowerment focus
- Sustainable materials
- Quality construction
- Fair wages

## Sustainability Practices

Kitty and Vibe uses fabric made from 82% recycled polyester and 18% X-Life Lycra, which provides exceptional durability and extends the life of each swimsuit. Production takes place in a woman-run factory in Bogota, Colombia, ensuring fair wages and empowering female workers.

## Product Range

Offers bikinis, one-pieces, and swim separates in contemporary styles and colors.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for ethical, sustainable swimwear.
