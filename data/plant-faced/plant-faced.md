## Overview

Plant Faced is a sustainable streetwear brand committed to demonstrating that ethical streetwear is achievable without the exploitation associated with traditional fashion production. The brand focuses on producing high-quality, sustainably-made clothing and accessories while actively working to educate consumers about the environmental impact of the fashion industry.

## Sustainable Practices

- **Sustainable fabrics**: All clothing and accessories are made using high-quality, sustainable materials
- **Fair trade manufacturing**: Ethically made with fair trade practices throughout the supply chain
- **Eco-friendly packaging**: Products are shipped in environmentally responsible packaging
- **Thoughtful design**: Garments are designed to be durable and long-lasting, reducing the need for frequent replacement
- **Consumer education**: Actively raises awareness about the negative environmental impact of conventional fashion practices and how consumers can avoid contributing to harmful industry practices

## Product Range

- Streetwear apparel including hoodies, t-shirts, and outerwear
- Accessories designed with sustainability as a core consideration
- Seasonal collections built around timeless designs intended for longevity

## Pricing

Mid-range streetwear pricing with a focus on quality and ethical production values.
