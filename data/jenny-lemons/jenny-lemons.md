## Overview

Founded by Jennie Lennick, Jenny Lemons creates playful, personality-filled socks featuring food motifs including strawberries, lemons, and pickles. The brand blends comfort with whimsical design while supporting small business artistry.

## Materials

- Combed cotton
- Polyester
- Spandex

## Key Features

- Designed in San Francisco
- Ethically manufactured in China
- One size fits most
- Soft combed cotton construction
- Three best-selling food motifs: strawberries, lemons, and pickles

## Sustainability Notes

While not made with organic materials, the brand supports a small business artist and prioritizes ethical manufacturing conditions.

## Sizing

- One size fits most
