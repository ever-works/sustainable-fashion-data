## Overview

Savannah Friedkin is a fine jewelry brand launched in spring 2024, focused on sustainable materials and ethical practices.

## Sustainability Features

- 100% certified recycled precious metals
- Climate-neutral lab-grown diamonds
- Early adopter of the SCS-007 Sustainability Rated Diamonds certification (developed by SCS Global Services)
- Founder personally audits suppliers for ethical practices, including renewable energy use and fair labor standards
- Emphasis on supply chain transparency and traceability

## Collections

- **Broken**: Designs featuring intentional cracks, symbolizing resilience
- **Emergence**: Inspired by plants growing in unexpected places, representing growth from adversity
- **SAV**: Focuses on simplicity and wearability

## Mission

The brand's mission is to spark conversations about sustainable jewelry production and celebrate women's unique journeys.
