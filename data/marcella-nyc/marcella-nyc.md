## Overview

Marcella NYC is a woman-owned, New York-based fashion brand built around the idea that fashion should be elevated and responsible. The label creates pieces with clean lines, modern silhouettes, and architectural detailing — refined without being overdone, designed for everyday city life.

## Production

- Designed in NYC and handcrafted in small European workshops
- Produced in limited runs to avoid excess inventory and unnecessary waste
- Quality over quantity approach to manufacturing

## Materials & Sustainability

- Works with low-impact and responsibly chosen materials
- Strong emphasis on craftsmanship and durability
- Minimalist supply chain with small-batch European production

## Design Philosophy

Marcella focuses on timeless design rather than trend-driven fashion. Each collection is meant to be worn season after season, offering versatile wardrobe staples that move effortlessly from day to night.
