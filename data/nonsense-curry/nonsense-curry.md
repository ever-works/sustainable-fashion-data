## Overview

Nonsense Curry is a lifestyle and slow fashion brand based in Goa, India, founded by Jolynn Carneiro. The brand creates products using durable and comfortable Khadi fabric, embracing slow fashion as an extension to mindful living.

## Sustainability Approach

- Uses Khadi, a traditional hand-spun and hand-woven Indian fabric
- Embraces slow fashion philosophy with minimal consumption
- Upcycles scraps and materials creatively into new products
- Promotes minimalist lifestyle and conscious consumption
- Transparent about social topics including skin color, feminism, and self-love

## Products

- Clothing including trousers and tops
- Upcycled and refurbished items
- Merchandise and curated collections
- Content around refurbishing, cartooning, and lifestyle design

## Brand Philosophy

Founder Jolynn Carneiro describes herself as a lifestyle designer who generates revenue through content creation, slow fashion, and speaking on societal topics. The brand emphasizes distinguishing between wants and necessities, advocating for conscious consumption and minimalism.
