## Overview

Established in 2001, Kuyichi was the first brand to produce 100% organic denim, named after the Peruvian God of the Rainbow.

## Sustainability Features

- First brand to produce 100% organic denim
- Uses only GOTS certified organic cotton
- Top-scoring labour and environmental ratings
- 'Trends are Circular' program for repair and resale
- Uses sustainable and recycled materials
- Focus on circular fashion

## Circular Fashion Program

- Customers can send back old jeans for repair or resale
- Extends garment life
- Reduces waste in fashion system
- Promotes circular economy

## Materials

- GOTS certified 100% organic cotton
- Sustainable materials
- Recycled cotton

## Product Range

Denim jeans, jackets, and shorts for men and women with focus on quality and longevity.

## Mission

Inspire positive change in the denim industry through organic materials and circular business practices.
