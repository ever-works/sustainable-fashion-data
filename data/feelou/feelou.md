## Overview

FEELOU is a Dutch brand based in The Hague, specializing in sustainable maternity and nursing bras. Designed to support women through the unforgettable journey of pregnancy and beyond, their bras combine comfort, adaptability, and style.

## Materials

- Made from TENCEL™ Modal, a natural material derived from sustainably sourced wood
- Soft, breathable, and moisture-absorbent fabric ideal for sensitive skin
- More sustainable than conventional alternatives

## Products

- Maternity bras with growth options to accommodate body changes during pregnancy
- Nursing bras suitable for use after pregnancy
- Easily adjustable design for comfort at every stage
- Available in two colors: basic black and cheerful coral pink

## Sustainability

- TENCEL™ Modal sourced from sustainably managed forests
- Designed for extended use through pregnancy and nursing phases
- Reduces need for multiple bra purchases by accommodating body changes
- Made with a more sustainable approach than conventional maternity lingerie

Mid-range pricing for specialty maternity and nursing lingerie.
