## Overview

Thesus is a Portuguese sustainable footwear brand that handcrafts shoes using natural and recycled materials. Each pair is made in Portugal with meticulous attention to both style and environmental impact, actively contributing to ocean cleanup efforts.

## Features

- Handmade in Portugal
- Over 95% natural and recycled materials
- Uses ocean plastics in construction
- Natural rubber soles
- Each pair removes up to 1.6 kg of waste from environment
- Contemporary, minimalist designs
- High-quality craftsmanship
- Comfortable and durable
- Eco-friendly production processes

## Sustainability Practices

Thesus shoes are handcrafted in Portugal using over 95% natural and recycled materials, including ocean plastics recovered from marine environments and natural rubber. Each pair of shoes contributes to environmental cleanup by helping remove up to 1.6 kg of waste from the environment, combining beautiful design with measurable positive impact.

## Product Range

Offers sneakers and casual footwear with minimalist, contemporary aesthetics suitable for everyday wear.
