## Overview

Conner Ives is a contemporary fashion label founded by New York-born, London-based designer Conner Ives. The brand has quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach to upcycling, transforming existing materials into highly sought-after fashion pieces.

## Key Features

- Signature designs include upcycled T-shirt dresses and repurposed piano shawl pieces
- Strong focus on creative material transformation and waste reduction
- Popular among A-list celebrities including Rihanna, Hailey Bieber, and Hunter Schafer
- Blends sustainable upcycling practices with high-fashion editorial aesthetics
- Rapidly grown from emerging label to Vogue office favourite

## Pricing

Contemporary designer pricing, with pieces reflecting the unique, one-of-a-kind nature of upcycled construction.
