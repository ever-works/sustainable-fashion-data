## Overview

LaBante London has been creating sustainable, sweatshop-free vegan bags since 2009 using recycled and bio-based materials.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses only recycled materials
- Polyester from recycled plastic bottles
- Outers made with 50%+ vegetable oil
- Renewable natural sources
- Sweatshop-free production
- Ethical manufacturing

## Materials

- Recycled polyester from plastic bottles
- Bio-based materials from vegetable oil
- Vegan materials
- Renewable resources

## Product Range

Clutches, crossbodies, backpacks, and tote bags with faux leather and quilted textures in natural colors.

## Specialty

Known particularly for elegant evening crossbody bags that combine style with sustainability.

## Environmental Impact

Diverts plastic bottles from landfills while using renewable plant-based materials for durable, beautiful bags.
