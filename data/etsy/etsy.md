## Overview

Etsy is a global online marketplace connecting buyers with independent sellers, artisans, and small businesses offering unique clothing and shoes. The platform specializes in pre-loved, custom-made, and upcycled apparel, providing an alternative to mass-produced fast fashion.

## Features

- Global marketplace of independent creators and small businesses
- Pre-loved, vintage, custom, and upcycled apparel options
- Endless variety across all styles and price points
- Carbon-neutral shipping — offsets 100% of carbon emissions from deliveries
- Powered by renewable electricity
- Committed to reaching net zero emissions by 2030
- Easy support for small businesses and independent artisans

## Pricing

Varies by seller. Prices depend on the individual creator and the specific item, allowing shoppers to find pieces across various budget ranges.
