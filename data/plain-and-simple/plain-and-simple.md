## Overview

Plain and Simple offers simple GOTS certified organic cotton t-shirts from fair labour factories in India, with a circular take-back model for end-of-life garments.

## Sustainability Practices

- GOTS certified organic cotton
- Made in SA 8000 certified factories in India
- Take-back service where customers can return garments to be remade into other goods at end of life

## Sizing

- XXS to XL

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for certified organic cotton basics.
