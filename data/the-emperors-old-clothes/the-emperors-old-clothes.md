## Overview

The Emperor's Old Clothes is handmade in the UK using only vintage and deadstock fabrics. The brand offers sizing from UK4 to UK32 with an additional online option for designing custom unique pieces.

## Features

- Handmade in UK
- Vintage fabrics only
- Deadstock materials
- Sizing UK4 - UK32
- Custom design option
- Size-inclusive
- Unique pieces
- Sustainable upcycling
- Zero new fabric

## Sustainability Practices

The Emperor's Old Clothes uses exclusively vintage and deadstock fabrics, requiring no new material production. The brand's handmade approach and size-inclusive offering (UK4-32) make sustainable fashion accessible to diverse body types.

## Product Range

Offers handmade clothing and custom pieces made from vintage and deadstock fabrics in sizes UK4-32.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for handmade UK upcycled fashion.
