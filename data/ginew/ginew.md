## Overview

Ginew is the only Native American-owned denim line, founded by husband and wife drawing inspiration from Ojibwe, Oneida, and Mohican heritage.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses organic cotton
- Premium selvedge denim
- Pendleton wool materials
- Sustainable material sourcing
- Quality construction for longevity
- Indigenous craftsmanship

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Premium selvedge denim
- Pendleton wool
- Sustainable fabrics

## Product Range

Contemporary Native Americana including denim, outerwear, and clothing blending traditional and modern aesthetics.

## Cultural Impact

Creates economic opportunities while celebrating and preserving Indigenous heritage through contemporary fashion.

## Mission

Combines sustainable materials with Indigenous design elements, proving traditional craftsmanship and modern sustainability can coexist beautifully.
