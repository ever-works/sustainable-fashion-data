## Overview

Jillian Boustred is an Australian-made fashion brand based in Sydney, operating since 2015. The brand offers classic designs that blend casual and chic aesthetics with a strong commitment to sustainability.

## Features

- Australian-made since 2015
- Works closely with suppliers and factories
- Each garment meticulously cut and sewn to reduce waste
- Classic designs designed for longevity
- Sydney-based design studio

## Sustainability Practices

- Close collaboration with suppliers and factories
- Pattern cutting designed to minimise textile waste
- Focus on garment durability and long wear life

## Pricing

Mid-range to premium pricing reflecting Australian manufacturing and quality construction.
