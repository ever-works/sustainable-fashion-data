## Overview

Embrace was founded by Claire, who couldn't find nursing bras that were soft, supportive, and made from natural fabrics. She spent a full year developing fabric from some of the finest yarns in the world to create her own line. The brand has since expanded beyond nursing into non-nursing bras and underwear.

## Materials

- US Supima organic cotton
- Austrian Micromodal
- Elastane for stretch and fit
- Plant-based, skin-safe fabrics
- Tag-free fabric construction

## Product Range

- **Nursing bras** — wireless, with padded convertible straps and removable padding
- **Non-nursing bras** — including the Lounge Bra with wireless support and lift
- **Underwear** — including the High Leg Brief made from 100% organic cotton
