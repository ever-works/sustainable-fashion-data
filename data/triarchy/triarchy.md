## Overview

Triarchy is a family-founded denim brand revolutionizing the industry with the only biodegradable plastic-free stretch denim.

## Sustainability Innovations

- World's only plastic-free stretch denim
- Carbon-neutral operations since 2018
- Uses 85% less water than cotton to grow and process
- Factory uses 85% recycled water
- Saves approximately 1,429 gallons of water per pair
- Invests in renewable energy projects

## Materials

- Tencel-cotton blend
- Plastic-free, biodegradable stretch denim
- Sustainably sourced materials

## Product Range

Women's and men's denim jeans known for innovative sustainable materials and modern fits.

## Family Values

Founded by two brothers and one sister committed to revolutionizing denim with environmental responsibility at the forefront.

## Environmental Impact

Combines water conservation, carbon neutrality, and biodegradable materials to create truly sustainable denim.
