## Overview

JulieMay creates luxury lingerie that combines the finest natural materials with sustainable practices. The brand specializes in elegant, comfortable intimates made from organic cotton and peace silk.

## Materials

### Organic Pima Cotton
- GOTS certified
- Exceptionally soft and durable
- Long-staple fibers
- Breathable and comfortable

### Mulberry Peace Silk
- Ethical silk production (no harm to silkworms)
- Natural lining material
- Moisture-wicking properties
- Hypoallergenic
- Temperature regulating
