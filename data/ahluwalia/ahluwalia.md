## Overview

Ahluwalia was founded by designer Priya Ahluwalia after she observed the extensive textile waste in Lagos, Nigeria, and Panipat, India during her studies. The brand has maintained sustainability as a core philosophy since its inception.

## Key Features

- Utilises upcycled, recycled, and organic materials across all collections
- Inspired by firsthand observations of global textile waste challenges
- Known for distinctive trademark printed pieces
- Blends cultural heritage with contemporary sustainable fashion practices
- Recognised for bridging traditional craftsmanship with modern design

## Pricing

Luxury contemporary pricing, reflecting the brand's design innovation and sustainable material sourcing.
