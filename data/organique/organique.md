## Overview

Organique is a Portuguese athleisure brand made for the contemporary woman. Entirely produced in a high-end local atelier, the brand promotes slow fashion by designing with longevity in mind.

## Features

- Athleisure designed for the contemporary woman
- High-quality organic materials
- Lower-impact fabrics including organic cotton and TENCEL Lyocell
- Completely vegan
- Produced in a high-end Portuguese atelier
- Sizes S-L

## Sustainability
