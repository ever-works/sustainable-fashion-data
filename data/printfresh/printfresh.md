## Overview

Printfresh offers vibrant sleepwear made from 100% organic cotton, ethically produced in a factory in Jaipur, India. The brand features colorful hand-drawn prints and traditional artisan block printing techniques.

## Features

- 100% organic cotton
- Made in Jaipur, India
- Ethical production
- Hand-drawn prints
- Traditional block printing
- Artisan techniques
- Vibrant colors and patterns
- Sustainable sleepwear
- Cultural craftsmanship

## Sustainability Practices

Printfresh uses 100% organic cotton and maintains ethical production practices in Jaipur, India. The brand preserves traditional block printing techniques, supporting artisan craftsmanship while creating colorful, sustainable sleepwear.

## Product Range

Offers pajama sets, nightgowns, and sleepwear featuring vibrant hand-drawn prints and block printing.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for organic cotton with artisan block printing.
