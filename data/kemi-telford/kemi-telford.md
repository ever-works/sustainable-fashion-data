## Overview

Kemi Telford is a Black-owned brand that designs and sells beautifully bold Nigerian-influenced clothing, with a Western twist. Sustainability lies at the heart of the brand.

## Features

- All clothes made to last and produced ethically
- Zero-waste approach: remnants turned into colourful scrunchies and gift bags
- Nigerian-influenced designs with Western styling
- Size range: S–XL
