## Overview

Vildnis is a UK brand making clothing from Tencel, certified organic cotton, certified organic linen, recycled merino, and recycled polyester in a small factory in Portugal. They avoid angora, fur, and any non-certified wool, and use environmentally friendly packaging with carbon neutral couriers.

## Sustainability Practices

- Materials: Tencel, certified organic cotton, certified organic linen, recycled merino, recycled polyester
- Production in a small Portuguese factory
- Avoids angora, fur, and non-certified wool
- Uses less toxic form of polyurethane
- Seacell fabric made from sustainably harvested seaweed, certified compostable
- Environmentally friendly packaging
- Carbon neutral courier delivery
- Note: some garments use blended fibres making them non-recyclable and non-compostable
