## Overview

White & Warren crafts sustainable, design-driven essentials for the next generation of fashion-forward women. The brand is known for its high-quality cashmere staples and commitment to sustainable material sourcing.

## Sustainable Materials

- Cashmere staples crafted from Mongolian cashmere
- Recycled yarn for non-cashmere sweaters made from bottles harvested from the ocean floor
- Focus on timeless, long-wearing designs to reduce wardrobe turnover

## Product Range

- Cashmere sweaters, cardigans, and knitwear
- Apparel and accessories
- Worldwide shipping available
- Available through multiple stockists and retail partners for in-person try-on

## Pricing

Starting at $335. Premium pricing reflects the quality of Mongolian cashmere and design-driven approach.

- Woman-owned business
