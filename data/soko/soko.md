## Overview

SOKO is a women-led Certified B Corp empowering Kenyan artisans through direct market access and mobile technology.

## Social Impact

- Connects artisans directly to global market
- Cuts out intermediaries
- Artisans earn 5X more than average workshop
- Women-led brand supporting women artisans
- Mobile technology enables direct connection
- Fair compensation and profit sharing

## Sustainability Features

- Certified B Corporation
- Uses 100% recycled gold
- Eco-friendly materials
- Recycled brass
- Reclaimed horn and bone
- Fair trade practices

## Materials

- Recycled brass
- Reclaimed horn and bone
- 100% recycled gold
- Sustainable and recycled materials

## Product Range

Handcrafted jewelry made by skilled Kenyan artisans with modern, contemporary designs.

## Technology Innovation

Uses mobile technology to connect artisans directly with customers, ensuring fair compensation and maintaining cultural craftsmanship.
