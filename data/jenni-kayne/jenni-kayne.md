## Overview

Jenni Kayne redefines everyday luxury with timeless wardrobe essentials designed to be cherished for generations. Rooted in a philosophy of effortless, enduring style, the brand offers vintage-inspired bags, cozy knits, and elevated staples that grow more beloved with time.

## Best For

Timeless Californian styles, size range XS–3X; up to US 24.

## Features

- Vintage-inspired bags
- Cozy knits and elevated staples
- Natural fiber focused collection
- Pieces designed to pair easily with existing wardrobe
- Sentimental, cozy pieces built for longevity

## Sustainability

- Local and ethical manufacturing where possible
- Focus on natural fibers
- Recycled materials
- Eco-friendly packaging
- Gives back to environmental causes
- Recycled and sustainable materials throughout
- Conscious production practices
