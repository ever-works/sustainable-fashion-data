## Overview

Gucci is a luxury fashion house making significant strides in sustainability while maintaining its position as a leading luxury brand.

## Sustainability Achievements

- Reduced greenhouse gas emissions across Scopes 1, 2, and 3 by 32% since 2022
- Achieved 99% overall traceability of raw materials in 2024
- Part of Kering Group's 24% absolute emissions reduction
- Guided by Environmental Profit & Loss (EP&L) tool
- Commitment to transparency in supply chain

## Environmental Initiatives

- Comprehensive emissions tracking and reduction
- Raw material traceability program
- Sustainable sourcing practices
- Focus on reducing environmental impact across supply chain

## Product Range

Luxury ready-to-wear, handbags, shoes, accessories, and jewelry for men and women.

## Kering Group Leadership

As part of Kering Group (also owner of Saint Laurent), benefits from shared sustainability initiatives and Environmental Profit & Loss methodology.
