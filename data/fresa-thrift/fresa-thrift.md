## Overview

Fresa Thrift is a Latina-owned online vintage shop that offers a financially inclusive way to shop sustainably. Items are more affordable than many slow fashion retailers, making it accessible for every eco-conscious shopper.

## Product Range

- Vintage clothing with bold patterns, textures, and colors
- Luxury bag finds
- Unique home items
- Curated thrifted pieces

## Community & Media

- Active presence on TikTok and Instagram showcasing behind-the-scenes thrift finds and hauls
- Highlights scavenging at outdoor markets
- Builds community around sustainable vintage shopping
