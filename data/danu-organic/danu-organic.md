# Danu Organic

Danu Organic partners directly with American organic farmers to source Colorgrown and plant-dyed organic cotton, crafting family-friendly casual wear and loungewear entirely in San Francisco. By using naturally pigmented cotton that requires no chemical dyeing and plant-based dyes for colored pieces, the brand eliminates the toxic wastewater that devastates communities near conventional textile plants. Where fast fashion brands rely on synthetic dyes that poison rivers worldwide, Danu Organic's Colorgrown cotton and botanical dyeing deliver rich, lasting color while keeping waterways clean.
