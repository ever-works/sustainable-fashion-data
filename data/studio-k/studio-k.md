## Overview

Studio K is an eco-friendly brand in Bali that creates organic yoga- and loungewear. Inspired by the 60's, their designs are timeless, sophisticated, and ethically produced. Their mission is to merge yoga teachings with meaningful, sustainable and wearable art to be a changemaker in the fashion industry.

## Ethics & Sustainability

- Bamboo viscose OEKO-TEX certified
- Low impact dyes
- Natural ramie fiber
- Ethical and transparent production
- Social justice and harmony with nature
- Educational focus on transparency
