## Overview

Founded by Araks Yeramyan in 2000, this New York-based brand creates sustainably made lingerie, swimwear, and pajamas.

## Sustainability Features

- ECONYL recycled nylon from ocean plastics
- Recycled polyester in swim collection
- OEKO-TEX 100 Certified fabrics
- Renewable energy sources in production
- Zero-water waste manufacturing
- Safe chemical management
- Methane emissions capture

## Materials

- ECONYL (recycled ocean plastics/fishing nets)
- Organic cotton
- Silk charmeuse
- Recycled nylon
- Recycled polyester

## Manufacturing

- Female-led factories in New York
- Sustainable fabrics made in Italy
- Renewable energy production
- Zero water waste

## Product Range

Lingerie, swimwear, and pajamas including soft-cup bralettes, stretch briefs, and classic swimsuits in signature high-octane colors.

## Organic Collection

Has 70% organic cotton and 30% upcycled cotton collection combining sustainability with luxury aesthetics.
