## Overview

Maria McManus is a fashion label founded by Irish-born, New York-based designer Maria McManus. The brand is distinguished by its holistic approach to sustainability, considering every detail from manufacturing origins to the buttons used on garments.

## Key Features

- Comprehensive eco-minded design philosophy encompassing materials, production, and hardware
- Focus on classic wardrobe staples reimagined with a contemporary aesthetic
- Supported by high-profile figures including Jennifer Lawrence
- Transparent sourcing and manufacturing processes
- Commitment to timeless design over seasonal trends

## Pricing

Premium contemporary pricing, reflecting the brand's elevated design approach and sustainable production standards.
