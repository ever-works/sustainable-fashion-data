## Overview

Bluebuck is a French menswear label built around a love of nature, specialising in technical menswear basics and swimwear designed for durability and longevity.

## Sustainability

- Produces locally to minimise environmental and carbon footprint impacts
- Uses GOTS certified organic cotton
- Incorporates recycled marine plastic into products
- Focuses on durable, long-lasting garments to reduce over-consumption

## Product Range

- Technical menswear basics
- Swimwear
- Organic cotton socks

## Sizing

Most items available in sizes XS-2XL.

## Ships Internationally

Ships from France to international destinations.
