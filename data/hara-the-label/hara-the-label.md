## Overview

Hara the Label is a Melbourne, Australia-based brand specializing in transparent supply chain fashion using bamboo lyocell and natural plant dyes for women's intimates, loungewear, and accessories.

## Product Details

- **Occasions**: Women's loungewear, underwear, accessories
- **Materials**: Bamboo lyocell
- **Dyeing**: Naturally plant dyed in-house in Melbourne, Australia
- **Price Range**: $$
- **Location**: Made and dyed in Melbourne, Australia

## Sustainability Focus

- Transparent supply chains
- All products naturally plant dyed in-house
- Bamboo lyocell fabric (sustainable fiber)
- Ethical practices and support for local economies
- Combination of bamboo and natural dyes designed to nourish skin and protect the body
- Local printing and dyeing at own Melbourne warehouse
