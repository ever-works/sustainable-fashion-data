## Overview

BRING is a Japanese brand with its own proprietary polyester recycling technology. The brand creates clothing from recycled materials through innovative chemical recycling processes that close the loop on textile waste.

## Features

- Proprietary recycling technology
- Chemical polyester recycling
- Japanese innovation
- Closed-loop system
- Recycled clothing
- Textile waste reduction
- Advanced sustainability
- Contemporary fashion
- Circular economy leader

## Sustainability Practices

BRING has developed its own polyester recycling technology that transforms textile waste back into high-quality materials for new clothing. This chemical recycling process creates a truly circular system, reducing reliance on virgin polyester and minimizing textile waste.

## Product Range

Offers contemporary clothing made from recycled polyester using proprietary recycling technology.
