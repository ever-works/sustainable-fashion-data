## Overview

ID.EIGHT creates ethical and sustainable sneakers made in Italy with materials derived from food industry waste. The brand transforms agricultural byproducts into stylish, eco-friendly footwear with Italian craftsmanship.

## Features

- Made in Italy
- Materials from food industry waste
- Ethical sneakers
- Sustainable footwear
- Agricultural byproduct use
- Italian craftsmanship
- Innovative materials
- Waste transformation
- Contemporary designs

## Sustainability Practices

ID.EIGHT innovatively uses materials derived from food industry waste, transforming agricultural byproducts that would otherwise be discarded into high-quality sneaker materials. Italian manufacturing ensures quality and supports local artisans.

## Product Range

Offers sustainable sneakers and casual footwear made in Italy from food waste-derived materials.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Italian sustainable sneakers.
