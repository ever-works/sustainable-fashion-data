## Overview

Solmate Socks manufactures all products at their own mill in Hickory, North Carolina. Working in collaboration with RecoverTex, they utilize recycled cotton yarns made from production scraps and used clothing.

## Features

- Made in Hickory, North Carolina
- Own mill production
- Recycled cotton yarns
- Production scrap use
- Used clothing recycling
- Partnership with RecoverTex
- American-made socks
- Sustainable manufacturing
- Mismatched designs

## Sustainability Practices

Solmate Socks diverts textile waste from landfills by using recycled cotton yarns made from production remnants and used clothing. All manufacturing takes place in their own North Carolina mill, ensuring quality control and supporting American workers.

## Product Range

Offers colorful, mismatched socks and accessories made from recycled cotton in unique patterns.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for American-made recycled cotton socks.
