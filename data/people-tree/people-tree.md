## Overview

Since 1988, People Tree has been running a fashion business that puts the planet first, pioneering fair trade fashion for over three decades.

## Sustainability Features

- Fair trade certified since inception
- Shares 90% of profits with charitable causes
- Uses certified sustainable materials
- Transparent supply chain
- Long-standing commitment to ethical fashion
- Planet-first business model

## Materials

- Organic linen
- TENCEL™
- Certified sustainable materials
- Organic cotton
- Low-impact fabrics

## Product Range

Women's clothing ranging from contemporary summer workwear to festival season specials, dresses, tops, and accessories.

## Legacy

As one of the earliest sustainable fashion brands, People Tree has spent over 30 years proving that fashion can be both stylish and ethical.
