## Overview

Untouched World is the first and only fashion label recognized by the United Nations for sustainability. The certified B-Corp brand produces 94% of its collection in New Zealand and has pioneered environmental responsibility since 1988.

## Features

- UN recognized for sustainability
- First fashion label with UN recognition
- Certified B Corporation
- 94% made in New Zealand
- Founded in 1988
- Environmental pioneer
- Social responsibility
- Local manufacturing
- Sustainable materials

## Sustainability Practices

Untouched World's UN recognition and B-Corp certification demonstrate exceptional commitment to sustainability. The brand maintains 94% local New Zealand production, supporting domestic workers and reducing transportation emissions while using sustainable materials.

## Product Range

Offers contemporary fashion for men and women including knitwear, outerwear, and essentials made primarily in New Zealand.
