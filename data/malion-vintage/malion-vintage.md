## Overview

MALION Vintage is a Japanese upcycled fashion brand founded in 2016 by designers Eriko Ishida and Aki Shimizu. The brand transforms vintage materials including men's ties, tweed jackets, and deadstock fabrics into entirely new contemporary designs.

## Upcycled Materials

### Vintage Sources

- Tweed jackets
- Vintage suits
- Deadstock fabrics
- Surplus textiles
- Pre-loved garments
- Designer fabric remnants
