## Overview

Message Factory designs and manufactures cheerful clothing in Quebec, Canada, using a high proportion of lower-impact materials including organic cotton. The brand inspires shoppers through sustainable, positive fashion.

## Features

- Designed in Quebec
- Manufactured in Canada
- Organic cotton
- Lower-impact materials
- Cheerful designs
- Positive messaging
- Canadian production
- Sustainable materials
- Inspiring fashion

## Sustainability Practices

Message Factory uses a high proportion of organic cotton and lower-impact materials while maintaining Canadian manufacturing. The brand's cheerful, inspiring designs promote positive values alongside environmental responsibility.

## Product Range

Offers casual clothing with cheerful designs and positive messages made from organic cotton.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Canadian-made organic cotton clothing.
