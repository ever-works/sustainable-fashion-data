## Overview

Astor & Orion is a circular jewelry brand focused on sustainable manufacturing through innovative recycling. They use recycled materials and environmentally friendly processes to create their pieces while ensuring safer working conditions for smiths.

## Recycling Program

- Customers can email the brand to ship old jewelry for recycling
- Old jewelry is processed through a metal recycling facility in Seattle
- Recycled metal is molded into new jewelry pieces
- US-based recycling reduces international shipping carbon footprint

## Sustainability Practices

- Circular design approach ensuring materials can be reused
- Environmentally friendly manufacturing materials and processes
- Safer workplace conditions for smiths
- Use of recycled metals in production
