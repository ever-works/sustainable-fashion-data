# Big Bud Press

Big Bud Press is a Los Angeles-based unisex clothing brand producing vibrant, size-inclusive garments (XXS-5XL) exclusively from plant-fiber fabrics with zero synthetics and sweatshop-free local production. By completely eliminating petroleum-based materials and keeping manufacturing in LA with fair wages, the brand proves that joyful, rainbow-colored fashion can be both deeply inclusive and genuinely sustainable. Where fast fashion relies on cheap microplastic-laden polyester and opaque overseas factories, Big Bud Press delivers biodegradable, naturally dyed workwear with full supply chain transparency and unapologetic body positivity.
