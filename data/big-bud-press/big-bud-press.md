## Overview

Big Bud Press is a Los Angeles-based unisex clothing brand using only plant fiber fabrics. Known for their vibrant colors and signature rainbow boiler suits, the brand combines sustainability with joyful, inclusive design.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Plant Fibers Only**: Uses exclusively plant-based fabrics
- **No Synthetics**: Completely avoids petroleum-based materials
- **Natural Fabrics**: Cotton, linen, and other plant fibers
- **Biodegradable**: Materials return to earth at end of life

## Ethical Production

- **Made in Los Angeles**: Local production reduces carbon footprint
- **Sweatshop-Free**: Fair wages and safe working conditions
- **Quality Control**: Oversight of ethical manufacturing
- **Supporting Local Jobs**: American garment workers

## Inclusivity

- **XXS-5XL Sizing**: Extensive size range
- **Unisex Design**: Clothing for all genders
- **Body Positive**: Celebrates diverse bodies
- **Inclusive Models**: Represents various body types

## Signature Style

Famous for bright, cheerful colors and their iconic rainbow boiler suits, Big Bud Press creates playful, functional clothing that brings joy to sustainable fashion.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for American-made, plant-fiber clothing.
