## Overview

SILVERWIND is a sustainable and ethical woman-owned business that specializes in creating unique and dreamy patterned activewear and swimwear inspired by nature.

## Sustainability Features

The brand is inspired by nature and crafts products from recycled bottles and fishing nets:

- **RPET Fabric**: Made from recycled plastic bottles
- **Recycled Nylon**: Swimwear made from recycled nylon fishing nets
- **100% Sustainable**: Products and packaging are 100% sustainable
- **Ocean Conservation**: Donates to Ocean Conservancy with every sale

## Ethical Manufacturing

Their partnered factory maintains an 87% workplace condition score by Intertek. Their fabrics are certified Standard 100 by Oeko-Tex, ensuring they're free from harmful substances.

## Product Range

Their ethical swimwear collection includes a range of unique styles with natural inspiration, including reversible bikinis and one-pieces featuring dreamy patterns inspired by the natural world.

## Pricing

Affordable to mid-range pricing for sustainable swimwear.
