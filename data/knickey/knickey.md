## Overview

Knickey creates sustainable underwear from organic cotton, prioritizing comfort, breathability, and environmental responsibility. The brand offers simple, well-designed intimates free from harmful chemicals.

## Features

- Organic cotton underwear
- Ethical production
- Chemical-free materials
- Breathable and comfortable
- Simple, classic designs
- Sustainable basics
- Women's intimates
- GOTS certified options
- Transparent practices

## Sustainability Practices

Knickey uses organic cotton that is free from harmful chemicals, pesticides, and synthetic materials. The brand maintains ethical production practices while creating comfortable, breathable underwear that is healthier for both people and the planet.

## Product Range

Offers women's underwear in various styles including bikinis, briefs, and high-waisted options in organic cotton.
