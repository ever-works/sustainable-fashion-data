## Overview

NAADAM specializes in ethical and fairly-made cashmere clothing, sourced from Mongolia's Gobi Desert. The brand is selective with the herders they work with, ensuring hand-combing practices and veterinary care is provided to all animals. The result is high quality, soft and sustainable cashmere spun into signature cashmere sweaters.

## Sustainability

- Invests in local Mongolian communities
- Natural and recycled materials
- Ethical conditions for animals
- Circular design principles
- Second hand options available
