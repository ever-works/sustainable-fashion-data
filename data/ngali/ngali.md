## Overview

Ngali is an Australian fashion and homewares brand that partners with Indigenous Australian artists to create beautiful and unique prints. The name translates to 'we' or 'us' in a number of Aboriginal languages, reflecting the brand's mission of creating a harmonious, sustainable, and equitable union of people with the planet.

## Features

- Fashion and homewares collections
- Unique prints created in partnership with Indigenous Australian artists
- Connects wearers to Country and culture
- Culturally meaningful storytelling through design

## Sustainability Practices

- Partnerships with Indigenous Australian artists
- Culturally respectful and equitable collaborations
- Focus on creating harmonious and sustainable practices
- Connection between people and the planet

## Pricing

Premium pricing reflecting unique artist collaborations and quality production.
