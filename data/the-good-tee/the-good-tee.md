## Overview

The Good Tee is a Canadian brand committed to producing responsibly made basics for the entire family, striving to humanise the fashion industry through ethical production practices.

## Features

- Fairtrade certified production
- GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) certified fashion
- Full supply chain tracing
- Supplier auditing practices
- Sizes XS-2XL
- Basics for the whole family
- Focus on positively impacting the fashion industry
