## Overview

Catori Life specializes in ethically made, nature-inspired jewelry crafted with care and respect for people and the planet. The woman-owned brand is continuously refining its business practices to improve both its production line and the lives of those involved.

## Sustainability & Ethics

- Mindfully made in the USA
- Ethically sourced and reclaimed materials
- Recycled gold used throughout collections
- All workers receive fair wages
- Biodegradable and compostable packaging
- Partners with The Eden Project — 10 trees planted with every piece purchased
- Supports several grassroots organizations

## Product Range

- Necklaces, earrings, rings, bracelets
- Artifact collection: standout nature-inspired statement pieces
- Talisman collection: sentimental, protective pieces
