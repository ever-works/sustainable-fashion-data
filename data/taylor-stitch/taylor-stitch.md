## Overview

Taylor Stitch crafts rugged, long-lasting menswear made with sustainable materials, designed to improve with age and wear.

## Sustainability Features

- Crowdfunding business model reduces overproduction waste
- Incorporates recycled materials in production
- Lower carbon footprint through demand-driven manufacturing
- Uses hemp, recycled wool, and upcycled cotton
- Made-to-order items reduce inventory waste
- Focus on durability and longevity

## Materials

- Hemp
- Recycled wool
- Upcycled cotton
- Sustainable fabrics
- Organic materials

## Product Range

Men's shirting, outerwear, denim, and everyday essentials with rugged, timeless style.

## Business Model

Crowdfunding approach means items are made based on actual orders, eliminating overproduction and reducing waste common in traditional fashion.

## Design Philosophy

Creates clothing that gets better with age, encouraging long-term ownership and reducing need for frequent replacement.
