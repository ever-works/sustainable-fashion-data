## Overview

Founded in 2016 by a collective of designers from Sweden and the UK, BITE Studios (an acronym for "By Independent Thinkers for Environmental Progress") is a luxury womenswear brand combining contemporary design with environmental responsibility.

## Sustainability Milestones

- **Material selection**: Prioritises organic and low-impact materials including organic cotton, responsible and organic wool, and forest-friendly viscose. The brand explicitly avoids animal skins, fur, feathers, virgin polyester, polyamide, and elastane. They also avoid conventional cotton.
- **Local production**: Manufactures products closer to home to reduce the climate impact of long-distance shipping.
