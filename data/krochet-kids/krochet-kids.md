## Overview

Krochet Kids (formerly Krochet Kids intl.) is a non-profit ethical fashion brand that produces hand-crocheted knitwear and accessories. The organization provides employment, mentorship, and empowerment programs to vulnerable women in Uganda and Peru.

## Products

- Crochet beanies
- Knitwear accessories
- Handcrafted crochet garments

## Social Impact

- Non-profit organization
- Provides employment opportunities to vulnerable women in Uganda and Peru
- Each product is signed by the artisan who made it
- Includes empowerment and mentorship programs
