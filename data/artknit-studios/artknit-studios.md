## Overview

Artknit Studios creates timeless knitwear made responsibly by Italian makers. The brand operates under the motto "buy less, buy better" and is committed to anti-waste practices.

## Features

- Timeless knitwear designs
- Responsibly made by Italian makers
- Anti-waste manufacturing practices
- Motto: "buy less, buy better"

## Materials

- 100% lower-impact materials
- Only certified fibres
- Locally-sourced fibres
- Commitment to minimizing textile waste

## Sustainability

- Partners committed to anti-waste practices
- Uses only certified and locally-sourced fibres
- Rated as "Good" or "Great" by Good On You

## Sizing

Items available in sizes XS-XXL.
