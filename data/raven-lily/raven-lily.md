## Overview

Raven + Lily creates handcrafted jewelry and accessories by skilled artisans using sustainable, regionally sourced materials.

## Sustainability Features

- Certified B Corporation
- Fair Trade Federation member
- 100% women-led
- 70% of artisans are women
- Regionally sourced materials
- Time-honored techniques
- Natural and sustainable materials

## Social Impact

- Supports skilled artisans globally
- Empowers women artisans
- Preserves traditional craftsmanship
- Fair wages and ethical practices
- Community development

## Materials

- Regionally sourced natural materials
- Sustainable materials
- Ethically sourced components

## Product Range

Handcrafted jewelry including earrings, necklaces, and accessories made by artisans worldwide.

## Mission

Empowers women through economic opportunity while preserving traditional craftsmanship and using sustainable materials.
