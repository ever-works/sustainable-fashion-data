## Overview

Fjällräven is a Swedish outdoor brand known for durable, functional outdoor equipment and clothing. The brand emphasizes longevity, repair, and environmental responsibility.

## Key Features

- Durable outdoor gear designed to last decades
- Recycled polyester and sustainable materials
- Repair guides and services for extended product life
- Arctic Fox Initiative funding environmental conservation
- Focus on functional design over trends

## Sustainability Initiatives

- Repair-first approach extends product lifespans
- Conservation funding through Arctic Fox Initiative
- Use of recycled and sustainable materials
- Designed for durability and longevity

## Product Range

- Outdoor clothing and equipment
- Backpacks and travel gear
- Sustainable outdoor essentials

## Pricing

- Mid to premium pricing reflecting quality and durability
