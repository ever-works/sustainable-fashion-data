## Overview

Achilles and the Tortoise is an Amsterdam slow fashion brand creating circular streetwear through two distinct collections that represent different approaches to sustainability.

## Two Collections

### Essentials Collection
- **Organic Materials**: GOTS certified organic cotton
- **Made in Portugal**: European production for quality
- **Timeless Basics**: Core wardrobe pieces
- **Sustainable Fabrics**: Environmentally responsible materials

### ALTER Collection
- **Upcycled Materials**: Repurposed parachutes and military blankets
- **Made in Amsterdam**: Local production
- **Unique Pieces**: One-of-a-kind items from surplus materials
- **Circular Design**: Transforms waste into fashion

## Slow Fashion Philosophy

The brand embraces slow fashion principles:
- Quality over quantity
- Timeless design
- Durable construction
- Minimal environmental impact

## Circular Economy

Both collections embody circular fashion by either using organic, biodegradable materials or upcycling existing textiles, keeping materials in use and out of landfills.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable streetwear made in Europe.
