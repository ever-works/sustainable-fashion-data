## Overview

Armoire is a women's clothing rental service that combines professional fashion stylists with algorithmic curation to build personalized wardrobes for each subscriber. After taking a style quiz, members receive a custom selection of apparel, including professional wear, everyday basics, and occasion pieces from brands like Equipment, Boden, and Argent.

## How It Works

Subscribers take a style quiz to inform their wardrobe preferences, then receive curated pieces selected by professional stylists and enhanced by a recommendation algorithm.

## Key Features

- Professional stylist curation combined with algorithmic recommendations
- Unused cases roll over — members never pay for extra deliveries they don't receive
- Free shipping both ways on all membership tiers
- Plans can be paused or canceled at any time
- Includes both everyday and professional attire

## Pricing

Plans start at $89/month for 4 items, with options for 7 items ($99 first month, $129/month thereafter) and unlimited items ($99 first month, $249/month thereafter). Free shipping included on all tiers.
