## Overview

Blumaka is an innovative sustainable brand that creates performance insoles using plant-based, bio-foam technology. The brand replaces traditional petroleum-based EVA foam with a renewable, carbon-negative material derived from natural sources, offering superior comfort and support for all types of footwear.

## Features

- Plant-based bio-foam insoles replacing petroleum foam
- Superior shock absorption and cushioning
- Enhanced breathability and moisture management
- Carbon-negative material production
- Compatible with all standard footwear
- Durable, long-lasting performance

## Technology

- Proprietary sustainable foam formulation
- Reduced carbon footprint compared to conventional foam
- High-performance athletic and lifestyle applications
- BLOOM algae-based material technology
