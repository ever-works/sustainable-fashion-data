## Overview

GANNI is Copenhagen's joyful export with undefeated cool-girl energy, combining playful design with honest sustainability efforts.

## Sustainability Honesty

- 44 responsibility goals implemented
- Full-time teams trace every supplier and worker
- Brutally honest: 'fashion can never be fully sustainable'
- Transparent about limitations and progress
- Continuous improvement focus

## Sustainability Initiatives

- Gradually removing leather
- Increased denim using recycled fibers
- 'Fabrics of the Future' investment
- Developing lower-impact materials
- GANNI Repeat rental program (Europe & US)
- Partnership with Levi's for upcycled denim

## Materials

- Recycled fibers in denim
- Innovative sustainable materials
- Reduced leather use

## Product Range

Scandinavian-inspired ready-to-wear known for playful prints, bold colors, and contemporary design.

## Philosophy

Honors modernist design while adding postmodern playfulness, creating unique sustainable fashion niche.
