## Overview

Poppy Barley is a Canadian Certified B Corporation originally known for its ethically made footwear and accessories. The brand has expanded into apparel with its first knitwear collection, maintaining its commitment to sustainability and ethical production.

## Materials

- OEKO-TEX® certified cotton
- OEKO-TEX® certified lambswool fibers
- RWS-certified (Responsible Wool Standard) merino wool
- Recycled cashmere
