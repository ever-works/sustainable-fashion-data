## Overview

Begg x Co is a UK-based heritage brand specializing in scarves and accessories. The brand combines traditional textile craftsmanship with modern, lower-impact production techniques to create high-quality pieces with a reduced environmental footprint.

## Sustainability Practices

- Low-waste production processes
- Local UK manufacturing and production
- Traceable materials throughout the supply chain
- Lower-impact techniques that reduce environmental harm

## Product Range

- Cashmere and wool scarves
- Wraps, shawls, and accessories
- Heritage-inspired designs with contemporary appeal

## Craftsmanship

The brand leverages generations of textile expertise while embracing modern sustainable approaches, ensuring each piece reflects both quality construction and environmental responsibility.
