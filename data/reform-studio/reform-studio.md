## Overview

Reform Studio is a Cairo-based design studio co-founded by Hazem and Hend Riad. The business was built around the invention of Plastex, a material made from discarded plastic bags.

## Sustainability Practices

- Inventors of Plastex, a material created from discarded plastic bags
- Helps alleviate Egypt's plastic waste problem
- Employs women from impoverished backgrounds
- Produces both fashion items and furniture from recycled materials

## Product Range

- Fashion items made from Plastex
- Furniture made from recycled plastic bags
- Upcycled design objects

## Social Impact

The studio combines environmental sustainability with social impact by providing employment opportunities for women from low-income communities.
