## Overview

Re/Done launched with unique concept of reconstructing vintage Levi's into modern fits for contemporary women.

## Sustainability Model

- Reconstructs vintage Levi's denim
- Gives new life to secondhand garments
- Pays royalties to Levi's for vintage pieces
- Upcycles existing materials
- Prevents waste through reconstruction
- Circular fashion pioneer

## Product Details

- Modern fits from vintage denim
- Contemporary styling
- Retail price $230–$270
- Each piece is unique
- Vintage Levi's collection

## Materials

- Vintage Levi's denim
- Secondhand garments
- Upcycled materials

## Product Range

Vintage-inspired denim and ready-to-wear reconstructed from authentic vintage pieces.

## Innovation

Pioneered the vintage reconstruction market, proving upcycled fashion can be both sustainable and desirable.
