## Overview

Westland Jewelry creates all pieces using 100% recycled metal sourced from post-consumer products including existing jewelry, electronics, and hardware. The brand is committed to ethical sourcing of diamonds and gemstones.

## Features

- 100% recycled metal from post-consumer products
- Recycled or fair-trade diamonds and gemstones
- 100% vegan brand
- Socially responsible production methods
- Ethical sourcing throughout supply chain
