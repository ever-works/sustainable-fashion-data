## Overview

Royal Robbins is a travel and outdoor clothing brand that incorporates approximately 30% hemp content into their adventure-ready apparel. Their hemp-infused clothing provides natural odor resistance and temperature control, prioritizing natural performance over chemical treatments.

## Key Features

- **~30% Hemp Content**: Significant hemp blend for performance benefits
- **Natural Odor Resistance**: Hemp's antimicrobial properties eliminate odors without chemical treatments
- **Temperature Control**: Hemp fibers naturally regulate body temperature
- **Travel-Ready Design**: Clothing designed for global adventure
- **Chemical-Free Performance**: Relies on natural hemp properties rather than synthetic additives

## Materials

Hemp blended into travel clothing at approximately 30% content, providing a balance of hemp's performance benefits with familiar comfort levels.

## Sustainability

- Reduces reliance on water-intensive cotton
- Natural performance properties reduce need for chemical treatments
- Hemp cultivation improves soil health and uses minimal water
