## Overview

Hello Adorn was founded by husband-and-wife team Jess and Adam Gardner. The brand offers earrings, rings, necklaces, and more made from pure gold and silver, and precious gems.

## Sustainability & Social Impact

- Supports hundreds of organizations with in-kind donations and a portion of sales
- On the first Friday of each month, 10% of purchases at its brick-and-mortar location in Eau Claire, Wisconsin support the organization of the month
- Cause-specific collections, such as the Dolly Necklace symbolizing body positivity with 50% of proceeds donated to families battling breast cancer
- Uses pure gold and silver materials
