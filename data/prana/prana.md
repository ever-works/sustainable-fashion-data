## Overview

prAna is a pioneer in sustainable fashion, recognized for minimizing environmental impacts and upholding high ethical standards throughout its supply chain.

## Sustainability Features

- First North American apparel company to bring Fair Trade Certified™ apparel to market
- Majority of sewing factories are Fair Trade certified
- Working towards 100% Fair Trade certified factories by 2028
- Significantly uses eco-friendly materials
- Sustainability is brand heritage
- Transparent about environmental practices

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Hemp
- Recycled wool
- Recycled polyester
- Sustainable fabrics

## Product Range

Yoga wear, activewear, outdoor clothing, and travel apparel for men and women.

## Heritage

From delivering clothing in upcycled fruit boxes to becoming a Fair Trade leader, sustainability has always been at prAna's core.
