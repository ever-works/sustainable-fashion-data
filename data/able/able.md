## Overview

ABLE is an ethical fashion brand dedicated to creating sustainable fashion while empowering women through fair wages and employment opportunities.

## Social Impact

- Employs and empowers women as solution to end poverty
- Provides fair wages and training opportunities
- Helps women break the cycle of poverty
- Publishes all worker wages online for transparency
- Focus on women in developing countries
- Size-inclusive (XXS to 3X, up to US 26/28W)

## Sustainability Features

- Certified B Corporation
- Lifetime guarantee on products
- Size swap collection for body changes
- Repairs or replaces products not holding up
- Fair labor practices throughout supply chain

## Product Range

Women's clothing, bags, shoes, and accessories made by fairly paid women worldwide.

## Transparency

ABLE is a leader in wage transparency, publicly sharing what workers are paid to ensure accountability and fair compensation.
