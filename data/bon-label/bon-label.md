## Overview

Bon Label was born from brand owner Linda Smyth's search for the perfect white tee. The Australian brand's products emulate timeless French simplicity and elegance, with t-shirts made from 100% organic cotton designed to pair seamlessly with any wardrobe.

## Key Features

- Timeless, elegant designs inspired by French simplicity
- T-shirts made from 100% organic cotton
- Versatile pieces suitable for casual weekend or office wear
- No animal products used in manufacturing
- Sizes available in AU 6-14

## Sustainability Practices

- Uses 100% organic cotton for t-shirts
- No animal products in manufacturing
- Strong focus on responsible labour practices
- Timeless design philosophy that counters fast fashion cycles

## Size Range

- AU sizes 6-14
