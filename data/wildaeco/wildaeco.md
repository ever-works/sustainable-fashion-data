## Overview

WILDA.ECO is a vegan fashion brand that combines minimalism with timeless design. The brand offers inclusive sizing and allows customers to personalise clothes for different heights, ensuring a better fit for diverse body types.

## Features

- Minimalist, timeless design aesthetic
- 100% vegan fashion — no animal-derived materials
- Inclusive sizing across the range
- Personalisable garments for different heights
- Based in Germany

## Sustainability

- Fully vegan product line
- Uses lower-impact materials
- Made-to-order elements to reduce waste
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for vegan contemporary fashion.
