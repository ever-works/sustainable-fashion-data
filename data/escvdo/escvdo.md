## Overview

ESCVDO is a Peruvian-inspired luxury sustainable fashion brand founded by sisters Chiara and Giuliana Macchiavello, originally from Lima and studying fashion in London. The brand blends ancestral Andean craftsmanship with contemporary fashion design.

## Sustainability Practices

- Clothing items are ethically made
- Incorporates stunning ancestral weaving techniques to create contemporary fashion
- Organic Pima cotton sourced from the coastal region of Pisco, Peru
- Eco-friendly Alpaca wool sourced from the Peruvian Andes
- Partners with local craftspeople to support preserving traditional sheering and hand spinning techniques

## Product Range

- Luxury contemporary fashion incorporating traditional Peruvian techniques
- Artisan-made clothing pieces combining Latin American heritage with modern design
