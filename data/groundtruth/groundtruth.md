## Overview

GROUNDTRUTH is a UK-based sustainable travel goods brand founded by three sisters united by a shared belief in the power of collaboration and a drive to protect people and nurture the planet. The brand designs problem-solving travel goods that drive positive change, reducing plastic pollution and improving people's lives.

## Features

- Sustainable travel goods and luggage
- Founded by three sisters
- Focus on reducing plastic pollution
- Problem-solving travel accessory design
- Ethical supply chain practices
- Improving people's lives through purposeful design
