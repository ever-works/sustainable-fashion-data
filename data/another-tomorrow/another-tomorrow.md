## Overview

Founded by Vanessa Barboni Hallik, Another Tomorrow is a B Corporation-certified luxury brand pushing boundaries in sustainable fashion.

## Sustainability Features

- B Corporation certified
- QR code technology for complete supply chain transparency
- Technology-based circular economy platform
- Resale program on company website
- Responsible chemical standards in dyeing and weaving
- Investment pieces designed to last

## Three-Pronged Approach

1. Create ethically and responsibly made clothing
2. Educate consumers about sustainability
3. Create platform for activism

## Materials

- Ethical wool
- Organic cotton
- Organic linen
- Recycled cashmere
- High-end sustainable fabrics

## Product Range

Luxury investment pieces including ready-to-wear and timeless staples.

## Transparency

QR codes on garments provide complete information about materials, production, and supply chain.
