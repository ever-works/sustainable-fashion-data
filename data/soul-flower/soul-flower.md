## Overview

Soul Flower is an independent, woman-owned company established in 1999. The Soul Flower Brand is an eco-friendly clothing line that is ethically made, using organic and recycled fabrics that are much better for the planet than conventional materials.

## Sustainable Materials

Their eco friendly clothing features organic cotton, hemp and recycled fibers:

- **Organic Cotton**: GMO-free, GOTS certified organic cotton that is soft to the touch and free of harmful chemicals
- **Hemp**: This sustainable fiber revives soil and requires no herbicides, pesticides, or fungicides to grow
- **Recycled Materials**: Recycled Plastic pieces are made by transforming post-consumer plastic (like soda bottles) into eco-friendly polyester

## Certifications

GOTS is the world's leading textile standard for organic fibers and ensures the organic status of their organic cotton fabric, from harvesting of the materials through the entire manufacturing process. Much of its Supply Chain is certified by Fair Trade USA, and Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS) certified organic cotton.

## Sustainability Rating

Good On You rates Soul Flower "Good" overall (last updated February 2023), noting their use of lower-impact materials and fair trade certifications.

## Pricing

Affordable pricing for organic and eco-friendly clothing.
