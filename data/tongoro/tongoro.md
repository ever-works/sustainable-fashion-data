## Overview

Tongoro is a Senegalese brand launched in 2016 by Sarah Diouf. It has since gained international prominence with notable figures including Burna Boy, Alicia Keys, and Beyoncé wearing the brand.

## Sustainability Practices

- Works with local Senegalese tailors
- Sources materials on the African continent
- Supports local craftsmanship and employment
- Promotes African fashion on the global stage

## Brand Recognition

The brand has achieved international recognition, with high-profile individuals including musicians and entertainers embracing its designs.
