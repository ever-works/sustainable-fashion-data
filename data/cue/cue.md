## Overview

CUE is a classic Australian fashion label that has been in operation since 1968. Growing with the times, the brand offers modern, fashion-forward pieces in luxe fabrics while maintaining timeless and wearable designs. It is the largest local manufacturer of fashion in Australia and is accredited by Ethical Clothing Australia.

## Features

- Established in 1968
- Modern, fashion-forward designs
- Luxe fabrics
- Timeless and wearable styles
- Largest local fashion manufacturer in Australia

## Sustainability Practices

- Accredited by Ethical Clothing Australia
- Local Australian manufacturing
- Commitment to ethical production standards

Mid-range to premium pricing reflecting quality fabrics and Australian manufacturing.
