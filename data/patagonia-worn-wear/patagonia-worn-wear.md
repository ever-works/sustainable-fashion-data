## Overview

Patagonia Worn Wear is Patagonia's official secondhand marketplace where customers can buy and sell used Patagonia gear. The program is a cornerstone of the brand's circular economy approach, keeping high-quality outdoor clothing in use and out of landfills.

## How It Works

- Browse a rotating selection of used Patagonia puffer jackets and other apparel
- Each jacket is refurbished and refreshed as needed before reselling
- Trade in used Patagonia gear for store credit towards a new purchase
- Each piece is unique with varying shapes, styles, and sizes
- Inventory is continuously replenished

## Services

- **Repair & Care**: Patagonia repairs and refurbishes returned garments before resale
- **Trade-In Credits**: Send in used Patagonia gear for store credit
- **Refurbishment**: Jackets are cleaned and refreshed to ensure quality before reselling
- **Diverse Selection**: Wide array of puffer jackets and other outdoor apparel across seasons and styles

## Environmental Impact

- Reduces consumption by extending the lifespan of existing garments
- Prevents quality outdoor clothing from ending up in landfills
- Offers a sustainable alternative to fast-fashion puffers
- Saves money for consumers while cutting down on overall consumption

## Pricing

Worn Wear items are priced below new retail, offering significant savings typically ranging from 30-60% off original prices. Puffer jackets and insulated outerwear generally range from $50 to $250 depending on model, condition, and rarity.
