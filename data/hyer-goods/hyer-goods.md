## Overview

HYER GOODS creates versatile women's bags from recycled luxury deadstock leather, handcrafted in Italy with purpose.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses deadstock leather from luxury brands
- Rescues abandoned yarns and fabrics
- Small-batch production
- Prevents waste in fashion industry
- Italian craftsmanship
- Sustainable values

## Materials

- Deadstock Italian leather
- Recycled luxury materials
- Rescued fashion industry fabrics
- High-quality sustainable materials

## Product Range

Small-batch bags and wallets combining timeless design with quality craftsmanship and environmental responsibility.

## Quality

Crafted from same premium materials used by luxury brands, ensuring exceptional quality while reducing waste.

## Mission

Transforms fashion industry waste into beautiful, long-lasting accessories, proving sustainability can be luxurious.
