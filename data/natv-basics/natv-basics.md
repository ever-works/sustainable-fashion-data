## Overview

nat'v basics is an Australian responsible underwear label designed for the everyday woman. The brand focuses on creating the most comfortable basics using recycled and lower-impact materials, with a no-frills design philosophy.

## Key Features

- Underwear and basics designed for everyday wear
- No frills, trims, or awkward bits for maximum comfort
- Made from recycled and lower-impact materials
- Designed in Australia
- Sizes available in AU 6-20

## Sustainability Practices

- Uses recycled materials in production
- Incorporates lower-impact material alternatives
- Responsible design and manufacturing approach

## Size Range

- AU sizes 6-20
