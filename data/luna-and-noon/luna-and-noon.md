## Overview

UK-based Luna and Noon was launched by two friends who share a passion for travel-inspired pajamas. Each set features hand-painted designs inspired by destinations around the world — from the sea and forests to the hills of Rajasthan, India.

## Materials & Production

- 100% cupro vegan silk (regenerated cellulose from recycled cotton)
- Organic cotton piping
- Natural shell buttons
- Ethically made with hand-painted prints

## Certifications & Initiatives

- Vegan
- Natural materials
- Recycled materials (cupro from recycled cotton)
- Gives back: portion of each purchase supports various causes, depending on the collection

## Product Range

- Adult pajama sets
- Travel-inspired designs (sea, forest, Rajasthan)

Price: £160 per set.
