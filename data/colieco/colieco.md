# ColieCo

ColieCo is a Portuguese lingerie, underwear, and swimwear brand handcrafting made-to-order garments exclusively from lower-impact and responsibly sourced fabrics. Every piece is sewn only after receiving a customer order, virtually eliminating the overproduction and deadstock waste endemic to conventional intimate apparel. Where fast fashion lingerie brands produce millions of synthetic, disposable garments, ColieCo's handcrafted, made-to-order approach delivers personalized, responsibly sourced intimates with a fraction of the waste.
