## Overview

Hasla is a Norwegian jewelry company based in Setesdal, Norway. The brand creates sustainable jewelry from recycled metals, making beautiful accessories while minimizing environmental impact.

## Features

- Based in Setesdal, Norway
- Recycled metals
- Sustainable jewelry
- Norwegian craftsmanship
- Environmental focus
- Beautiful designs
- Minimal impact
- Quality construction
- Ethical production

## Sustainability Practices

Hasla uses recycled metals throughout its jewelry collections, eliminating the need for new mining and reducing environmental impact. The Norwegian brand combines sustainable materials with beautiful design and quality craftsmanship.

## Product Range

Offers jewelry including rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets made from recycled metals.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Norwegian recycled metal jewelry.
