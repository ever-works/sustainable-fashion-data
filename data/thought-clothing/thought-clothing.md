## Overview

Established in 1995 in Australia, Thought Clothing creates timeless, eco-friendly apparel emphasizing natural materials and longevity.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses medium proportion of eco-friendly materials
- Focus on durability and longevity
- Encourages repair and garment passing
- Rated 'Good' for environment by Good On You
- Natural, organic, and recycled materials

## Philosophy

"Wear me, love me, mend me, pass me on" - encouraging consumers to cherish and extend garment life.

## Materials

- Organic cotton
- Hemp
- Bamboo
- Recycled polyester
- TENCEL™
- Natural fibers

## Product Range

Men's and women's apparel including dresses, tops, sweaters, and basics with timeless designs.
