## Overview

Black By Maria Silver is an ethically produced fashion brand founded by Maria Silver, who was featured on a Cosmopolitan list for Latina designers. The brand combines streetwear with modern art-inspired aesthetics.

## Cultural Impact

- Latina-owned and operated
- Founder recognized by Cosmopolitan as a notable Latina designer
- Celebrates Latinx design heritage and cultural aesthetics

## Product Range

- Trendy streetwear with fun, streamlined designs
- Funky patterns and bold floral motifs that can be dressed up or down
- Bathing suits and bold one-piece swimsuits
- Jumpsuits and versatile pieces

## Sustainability

- Ethically produced garments
- Responsible manufacturing practices

## Pricing

Affordable to mid-range pricing for ethically produced contemporary fashion.
