## Overview

Rustek makes "the most sustainable and only plastic-free hats on earth" from their Portland base. The brand features patent-pending CorkFlex natural brim technology, constructing brims from cork and bamboo that are softer, lighter, and more comfortable than plastic.

## Features

- Completely plastic-free
- Patent-pending CorkFlex technology
- Cork and bamboo brims
- Portland-based brand
- Softer than plastic brims
- Lighter weight
- More comfortable fit
- Sustainable headwear
- Innovative materials

## Sustainability Practices

Rustek eliminates all plastic from hat production, using innovative CorkFlex technology with cork and bamboo. These natural materials provide superior comfort while being completely biodegradable and sustainable.

## Product Range

Offers plastic-free hats with cork and bamboo brims in various styles.

Mid to premium pricing for plastic-free innovative sustainable hats.
