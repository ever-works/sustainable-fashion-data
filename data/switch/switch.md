## Overview

Switch is a designer accessory rental platform specializing in handbags, jewelry, and watches from luxury fashion houses. The service spans seasons and styles, offering everything from vintage Chanel to the latest Bottega Veneta collections, plus pieces from Bvlgari, Hermès, Cartier, Prada, Miu Miu, Fendi, and Alighieri.

## How It Works

Members choose from one of four subscription plans, receive items at their doorstep within two days, and can keep pieces for as long or as short as desired with an active membership.

## Membership Plans

| Plan | Price/Month | What's Included |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Core 1 | $59 | One Core piece at a time (avg. retail value ~$1,000) |
| Core 3 | $126 | Three Core pieces at a time (avg. retail value ~$1,000 each) |
| Select | $164 | One Select piece at a time (avg. retail value ~$4,000) |
| Select+ | $254 | One Select piece + three Core pieces at a time |

## Key Features

- Access to both vintage and contemporary designer pieces
- Two-day delivery to member's doorstep
- No time limits on how long items are kept (with active membership)
- Tiered system differentiating Core (~$1,000 avg. value) and Select (~$4,000 avg. value) pieces
- Spans seasonal collections and iconic vintage pieces

## Pricing

Plans start at $59/month for a single Core item, up to $254/month for the Select+ tier offering the mix of premium and Core pieces.
