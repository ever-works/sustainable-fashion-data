## Overview

Levi's SecondHand is the iconic brand's first buy-back program enabling customers to purchase and sell back used jeans and jackets.

## Environmental Impact

- Buying used saves approximately 80% CO2 emissions
- Saves 700 grams of waste per pair
- Significantly reduces environmental footprint
- Extends product lifecycle

## Program Features

- Buy secondhand jeans and jackets on Levi.com
- Turn in worn items in Levi's stores
- Receive gift card for future purchase
- Items cleaned and resold
- Circular economy model

## 2026 Circular Goals

- Comprehensive plan to be 'circular ready' by 2026
- Products that can be used more
- Made to be made again
- Safe, recycled, or renewable inputs
- Developing circular design system

## Innovation

Levi's 501 Circular jeans use Circulose from Renewcell, combining recycled and organic cotton for true circularity.

## Mission

Transforming from linear to circular business model, ensuring products can be remade repeatedly.
