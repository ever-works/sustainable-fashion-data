## Overview

Pure Waste is a Finnish brand that manufactures garments entirely from recycled cotton and recycled polyester in a wind-powered factory in India. They use reusable mailers for shipping to minimize packaging waste.

## Sustainability Practices

- Garments made from 100% recycled cotton and recycled polyester
- Production in a wind-powered factory in India
- Reusable mailers for shipping
- No virgin materials used in production

## Materials

- 100% recycled cotton
- Recycled polyester
- Zero virgin fiber inputs

## Product Range

Basics and casual wear including t-shirts, hoodies, and loungewear in sizes XXS–XXL.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for recycled fashion basics.
