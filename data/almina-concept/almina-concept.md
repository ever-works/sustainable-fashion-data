## Overview

Almina Concept is a woman-owned affordable contemporary fashion brand working directly with family-run factories in Seoul, South Korea to produce quality wardrobe staples at accessible prices with plastic-free shipping.

## Features

- Affordable contemporary fashion
- Direct partnerships with family-run factories
- Quality wardrobe staples
- Plastic-free shipping
- Ethical production conditions

## Product Range

Contemporary wardrobe staples including tops, bottoms, dresses, and outerwear for modern lifestyles.
