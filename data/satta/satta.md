## Overview

Satta was founded by Joe Lauder, who started out producing classic '60s-style skateboards before unexpected press led him to creating a sustainable clothing brand.

## Products

- Sustainable clothing with a laid-back aesthetic
- Classic skateboards and skate decks
- Incense products, including sustainably harvested white sage incense bundles from the coastal mountains of California
- Nag Champa incense sticks

## Philosophy

- Heavy focus on ethics, organics, and sustainability
- Laid-back aesthetic with touches of spirituality
- Roots in skateboarding culture

## Availability

The brand has been picked up by retailers such as Kinoko, Goodhood, and Slam City Skates in its home city of South London.
