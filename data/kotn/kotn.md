## Overview

Kotn is a certified B Corporation creating sustainable clothing and home decor with a focus on quality, comfort, and ethics.

## Sustainability Features

- Certified B Corp voted Best for the World™
- Fourth-highest B Impact Score of apparel brands in North America
- Works directly with small farms in Egypt for cotton sourcing
- Ethically sources cotton and linen
- Uses Oeko-Tex-certified dyes
- Packages orders in reusable cotton totes
- GOTS-certified cotton

## Materials

- GOTS-certified cotton
- Organic linen
- Tencel
- Merino wool
- Ethically sourced Egyptian cotton

## Product Range

Basics and essentials including t-shirts, sweaters, pants, and home goods. Known for high-quality, minimalist designs.

## Direct Trade Model

By working directly with farmers, Kotn ensures fair compensation and maintains quality control throughout the supply chain.
