# Kotn

Kotn is a Certified B Corporation creating premium basewear from direct-trade Egyptian cotton, linen, and merino wool with the fourth-highest B Impact Score of all North American apparel brands. By working directly with small farms in Egypt using GOTS-certified organic cotton and OEKO-TEX-certified dyes, the brand ensures traceable, fair-trade supply chains from field to garment. Where mainstream cotton sourcing relies on opaque intermediaries that exploit farmers and pollute waterways, Kotn's direct-trade model and reusable cotton tote packaging prove that affordable, everyday essentials can uphold the highest verified ethical standards.
