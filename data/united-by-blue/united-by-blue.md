## Overview

United by Blue is a sustainable outdoor brand that removes one pound of trash from oceans and waterways for every product sold. The brand uses eco-friendly materials, water-saving dyes, and plastic-free packaging.

## Features

- Removes 1 lb trash per product sold
- Ocean and waterway cleanup
- Eco-friendly materials
- Water-saving dyes
- Plastic-free packaging
- Sustainable outdoor wear
- Environmental action
- Quality construction
- Mission-driven brand

## Sustainability Practices

United by Blue combines sustainable production with direct environmental action. For every product sold, the brand removes one pound of trash from oceans and waterways through organized cleanup events. The brand uses eco-friendly materials, water-saving dyeing processes, and completely plastic-free packaging.

## Product Range

Offers outdoor clothing, bags, accessories, and gear made from sustainable materials for adventure and everyday use.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable outdoor gear with ocean cleanup impact.
