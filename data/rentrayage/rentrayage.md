## Overview

Rentrayage creates unique, sustainable clothing from upcycled vintage fabrics and deadstock garments. The brand transforms old button-down shirts, graphic tees, lace, denim, and accessories into contemporary fashion pieces.

## Upcycled Materials

- Vintage button-down shirts
- Graphic tees and band shirts
- Vintage lace and trims
- Denim and jeans
- Vintage accessories
- Deadstock fabrics
- Textile remnants
- Pre-loved garments

## Design Process

- Deconstructing vintage pieces
- Creative pattern making
- Hand-cut and sewn
- Unique combinations
- Artisan craftsmanship
- One-of-a-kind designs

## Product Range

- Dresses and skirts
- Tops and blouses
- Jackets and outerwear
- Pants and shorts
- Accessories
- Reconstructed denim
- Mixed-media pieces

## Sustainability Benefits

- Zero new fabric production
- Diverts textiles from landfills
- Extends garment life
- Reduces waste
- Minimal water usage
- No chemical processing
- Circular fashion model

## Design Aesthetic

- Bohemian and eclectic
- Vintage-inspired
- Contemporary edge
- Artistic expression
- Unique character
- Handmade quality

## Uniqueness

Every piece is one-of-a-kind due to the vintage and deadstock nature of materials. No two items are exactly alike.

## Craftsmanship

- Handmade construction
- Attention to detail
- Quality workmanship
- Artistic vision
- Careful material selection

## Target Customer

Fashion-conscious individuals seeking unique, sustainable pieces with vintage character and contemporary styling.

Upcycled artisan fashion, typically $60-$300, reflecting handmade quality and unique materials.
