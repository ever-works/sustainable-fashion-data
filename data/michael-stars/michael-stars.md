## Overview

Founded in 1986, Michael Stars has evolved from a collection of iconic graphic tees into a diverse range of versatile apparel. The brand is committed to social activism and philanthropy through the Michael Stars Foundation, which focuses on promoting equality and supporting grassroots initiatives.

## Sustainability Practices

- Over 80% of the workforce is composed of women
- More than 70% of garments are locally produced in Los Angeles
- Made in Los Angeles using natural materials
- B Corp Certified
- Partners with ThredUp for preloved shopping
- Gives back through the Michael Stars Foundation promoting equality and supporting grassroots initiatives

## Product Range

- Graphic tees
- Tops and blouses
- Pants and trousers
- Dresses
- Sweaters and knitwear
- Loungewear

US XS – XL
