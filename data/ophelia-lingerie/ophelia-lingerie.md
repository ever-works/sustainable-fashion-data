## Overview

Ophelia Lingerie is a Belgian brand creating handmade lingerie in Antwerp using certified sustainable fabrics. Each piece is wire-free and designed for comfort and longevity.

## Sustainable Materials

- **OEKO-TEX Certified**: Fabrics tested for harmful substances
- **European Sourcing**: Materials from Italy, Germany, and France
- **Quality Fabrics**: Premium materials for durability

## Handmade in Belgium

- **Antwerp Atelier**: Each piece handcrafted in Antwerp
- **Artisan Quality**: Traditional lingerie-making techniques
- **Made in Europe**: Supports European textile craftsmanship
- **Small Batch Production**: Ensures quality control

## Design Philosophy

- **Wire-Free**: Comfortable, natural support without underwires
- **Lasting Quality**: Designed to withstand years of wear
- **Timeless Style**: Classic designs that don't follow trends
- **Body-Positive**: Celebrates natural body shapes

## Certifications

- **OEKO-TEX Standard 100**: Fabrics certified free from harmful chemicals

## Environmental Impact

By producing locally in small batches with certified materials, Ophelia Lingerie minimizes environmental footprint while ensuring fair labor practices.

Premium pricing for handmade, European-crafted lingerie.
