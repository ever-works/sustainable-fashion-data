## Overview

LA RELAXED is a sustainable fashion brand that designs and manufactures all garments under one roof in Los Angeles. The brand measures its impact by footsteps, creating each garment in under 100 steps, and holds itself accountable to six pillars of responsible design.

## Features

- GOTS-certified organic cotton and TENCEL modal & lyocell fabrics
- Deadstock fabrics utilized to reduce waste
- Non-toxic dyes and recycled buttons
- All design and manufacturing under one roof in LA
- Sources materials within a 10-mile radius
- Zero garment waste policy — never sends garments to landfill
- Eco-friendly packaging
- Size range XS to XXL
- Casual, everyday relaxed styles

## Pricing

Budget-friendly pricing for sustainable, locally-made casual wear.
