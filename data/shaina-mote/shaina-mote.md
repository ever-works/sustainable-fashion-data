## Overview

Shaina Mote founded her eponymous label at 21 after her early career as a buyer revealed the unsustainable practices of fast fashion. With an ethos about the interconnectedness of our lives, businesses, and nature, the brand seeks to create slow-made, thoughtful pieces with low-impact production and sustainable sourcing practices.

## Sustainability

- Made in Los Angeles with fair labor practices
- Water and energy efficient production
- GOTS-certified organic cotton when possible
- Plastic-free, biodegradable, and recyclable packaging
- Recycling and repair programs for circularity
- Low-impact production and sustainable sourcing

## Product Range

- Elevated basics and capsule wardrobe pieces
- Simple and timeless collections
- Thoughtfully designed garments
- Sizes: 0 – 18, XS – XL
