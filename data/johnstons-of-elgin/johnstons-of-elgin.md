## Overview

Johnstons of Elgin is a Scottish heritage mill that has been weaving cashmere and fine wool since 1797. With over two centuries of continuous craftsmanship, the brand represents one of the longest-standing cashmere producers in the world.

## Craftsmanship

- Every piece made in their own mills in Scotland
- Over 225 years of weaving expertise
- Tradition and precision combined in every stitch
- Fully vertical production from spinning to finishing

## Product Range

- Premium cashmere and fine wool garments
- Scarves, shawls, and accessories
- Sweaters, cardigans, and knitwear
- Luxury home textiles

## Heritage & Location

Based in Elgin, Scotland, the mill has maintained its production on-site for over two centuries, preserving traditional textile craftsmanship while adapting to modern quality standards.
