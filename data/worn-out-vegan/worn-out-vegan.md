## Overview

Worn Out Vegan is an ethical vegan apparel brand focused on spreading compassion through bold, creative designs. The brand converts fashion into a force for good by donating a portion of proceeds to animal charities.

## Features

- 10% of profits donated to animal charities
- Bold, creative designs that spark conversation
- 100% vegan and cruelty-free materials
- Apparel designed to inspire kindness
- Promotes compassionate fashion choices
