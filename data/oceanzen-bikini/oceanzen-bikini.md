## Overview

OceanZen Bikini is a sustainable swimwear brand that combines style and sustainability through the use of regenerated ocean plastic. The brand's designs evoke a sense of adventure and environmental consciousness.

## Product Range

- Sustainable swimwear and bikinis
- Ocean-inspired swim collections

## Sustainability Practices

- Uses regenerated ocean plastic materials
- Removes waste from marine environments
- Environmentally conscious production
- Focus on ocean conservation

## Design Inspiration

Collections are inspired by the colors and patterns of the ocean, creating pieces that evoke both the beauty of marine environments and a sense of adventure.
