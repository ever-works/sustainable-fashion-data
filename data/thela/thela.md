## Overview

Théla is a Greek brand that creates handcrafted fashion and lifestyle accessories using plastic waste. Founded by Diti Kotecha, the brand is on a mission to inspire change by diverting plastic from landfills, oceans, and harm to wildlife.

## Features

- Handcrafted fashion and lifestyle accessories
- Made from recycled plastic waste
- Works with partners from disadvantaged communities
- Addresses the global plastic waste and over-consumption problem
- Entirely vegan product line

## Sustainability

- Diverts plastic waste from landfills and oceans
- Partners with disadvantaged communities for production
- Uses lower-impact materials in crafting pieces
- 100% vegan
- Raises awareness about disposable plastics and over-consumption
- Rated 'Good' or 'Great' by Good On You's methodology

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for handcrafted sustainable accessories.
