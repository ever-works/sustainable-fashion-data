## Overview

Namaste Clothing is a UK brand that claims to sell clothes made fairly, recognised by BAFTS (the British Association for Fair Trade Shops) as a fair trade importer.

## Sustainability Practices

- Printed with azo-free dyes
- Many products made entirely from recycled materials
- Recognised by BAFTS as a fair trade importer
- Not independently accredited for fair trade production by other independent organisations
