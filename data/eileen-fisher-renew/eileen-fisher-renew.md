## Overview

Renew is EILEEN FISHER's garment take-back and reuse program embodying commitment to circular design and future without waste.

## Program Milestones

- Over 15 years of operation
- 2.3 million garments resold, donated or remade
- 2 million+ units collected (2009-2023)
- 660,885 garments re-sold
- 57% of damaged inventory recycled

## How It Works

- Bring clothes to any US EILEEN FISHER or Renew store
- Receive $5 in Renew Rewards per piece
- Any condition accepted
- Mail-in option available

## Garment Pathways

- Refurbished items sold in-store and online
- Fiber-to-fiber recycling with Re-Verso (Italy) and Hallotex (Spain)
- Mechanically shredded and respun into new yarns
- Some donated or repurposed
- Downcycled into auto carpets or seating

## Recent Achievement

Fall 24 Collection featured sweater with 80% recycled wool from take-back program.

## Impact

Creating circular design system where garments can live multiple lives, reducing waste and environmental impact.
