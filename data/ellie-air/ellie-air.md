## Overview

Ellie Air is a B Corp certified jewelry brand committed to zero-waste production and ethical materials. The brand creates beautiful fine jewelry using only Fairmined or recycled gold, proving luxury can be sustainable.

- Safe working conditions
- Environmental protection

- Certified responsible mining

### Recycled Gold
- Repurposed precious metals
- No new mining required
- Reduced environmental impact
- Same quality as virgin gold
