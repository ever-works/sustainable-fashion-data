## Overview

Lolë is a Montreal-based technical outerwear brand that blends warmth, luxury, and sustainability throughout its collection of jackets, parkas, and trenches. The brand is committed to producing outerwear that performs in cold and rainy climates while minimizing environmental impact.

## Features

- **Sustainable Materials**: Jackets and parkas made with RDS-certified down insulation and responsibly sourced vegan fabrics
- **Water-Resistant Coatings**: PFOA/PFOS-free water-resistant treatments
- **Versatile Design**: Incorporates removable hoods and adaptable features for varying weather conditions
- **Warranty Offered**: Products backed by warranty for longevity assurance
- **Cruelty-Free**: No animal-derived materials; focuses on vegan alternatives

## Product Range

- Insulated parkas
- Technical jackets
- Trench coats
- Outerwear designed for cold and rainy climates

## Pricing

Outerwear starts at approximately $142+, with seasonal collections featuring parkas, jackets, and trench coats.
