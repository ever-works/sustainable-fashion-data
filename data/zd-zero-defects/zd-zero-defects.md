## Overview

ZD Zero Defects is a Spanish underwear brand with over a century of heritage, having produced high-quality and timeless underwear for both women and men since 1920.

## Materials

- Egyptian cotton for premium softness and durability
- Organic cotton for environmental responsibility
- Soy thread — a pioneering material that is antibacterial (ideal for sensitive skin), recyclable, and biodegradable

## Products

- Women's underwear: timeless styles in natural materials
- Men's underwear: premium basics in natural materials
- Specialty soy thread items for sensitive skin

## Sustainability

- Over 100 years of European manufacturing heritage
- Pioneering use of soy thread as a sustainable material alternative
- Combines recyclable and biodegradable fabrics
- Natural materials that reduce environmental impact

## Pricing

Mid-range to premium pricing reflecting heritage quality and specialty materials.

## Availability

Available via the ZD Zero Defects webshop and at select boutiques including Lingerie BRA in Kortrijk.
