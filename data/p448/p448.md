## Overview

P448 elevates casual footwear styles with innovative, eco-conscious designs inspired by artists, athletes, and change makers. The brand pushes the boundaries of fashion with responsible sourcing, reduced waste, and a focus on minimizing environmental impact.

- Sneakers (primary collection)
- Sandals
- Flats

## Sizing

- Women's 5.5 – 11.5
- Men's 6.5 – 16

## Price Range
