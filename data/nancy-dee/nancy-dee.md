## Overview

Nancy Dee is a UK-based brand that manufactures all garments in Britain. Their fabrics are mostly made from renewable natural sources such as soya, bamboo, and organic cotton, or rayon. Some items are made using up-cycled material that would otherwise be consigned to landfill.

## Sustainability Practices

- All garments manufactured in Britain
- Fabrics from renewable natural sources (soya, bamboo, organic cotton, rayon)
- Some items made from up-cycled materials
- Patterns designed to minimize fabric wastage
- Leftover fabric used for following season's samples or re-dyed for new designs
- Most pieces machine washable

## Sizing

- 8 – 16

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for British-made sustainable fashion.
