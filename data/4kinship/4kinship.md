## Overview

4KINSHIP is Diné woman-owned brand empowering Indigenous communities through fashion, transforming found fabrics into chic wearable art.

## Sustainability Features

- Upcycled clothing exclusively
- Transforms found fabrics
- Uses vintage materials
- Hand-painted designs
- Handmade by local artisans
- Most pieces one-of-a-kind or small batch

## Materials

- Found fabrics
- Upcycled materials
- Vintage textiles

## Product Range

Modern wearable art combining Indigenous design elements with contemporary fashion, each piece unique.

## Artisan Impact

- Made by local Indigenous artisans
- Empowers Indigenous communities
- Creates economic opportunities
- Preserves traditional techniques

## Philosophy

Transforms waste materials into art while empowering Indigenous communities through the language of fashion.
