## Overview

Warren was founded in tribute to the founder's grandfather, whose wardrobe embodied simplicity, refinement, and durability. Rooted in the culture and craft of the New England coast, the brand designs and manufactures clothing locally with an emphasis on thoughtful construction, responsible sourcing, and fabrics built to last. Every garment is produced in small batches and made to be modern heirlooms.

## Materials & Production

- Made in the USA (New England)
- Small-batch production
- Family-owned business
- Handcrafted garments with thoughtful construction
- Responsible fabric sourcing
- Shirts and pants product range
