## Overview

Bhava creates thoughtfully designed vegan footwear combining style, comfort, and sustainability with handcrafted attention to detail.

## Materials

- Vegan leather (PU-free options available)
- Organic cotton
- Cork
- Natural rubber
- Recycled materials
- Plant-based alternatives

## Sustainability Practices

- 100% vegan and cruelty-free
- Handmade production
- Ethical labor practices
- Low-impact manufacturing
- Minimal waste production
- Recyclable packaging

## Product Range

Flats, sandals, boots, booties, heels, wedges, sneakers, mules, and slides designed for women with classic, versatile aesthetics.
