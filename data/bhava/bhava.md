## Overview

Bhava creates thoughtfully designed vegan footwear that combines style, comfort, and sustainability. Each pair is handcrafted with attention to detail and environmental responsibility.

## Materials

- Vegan leather (PU-free options available)
- Organic cotton
- Cork
- Natural rubber
- Recycled materials
- Plant-based alternatives

## Sustainability Practices

- 100% vegan and cruelty-free
- Handmade production
- Ethical labor practices
- Low-impact manufacturing
- Sustainable material sourcing
- Minimal waste production
- Recyclable packaging

## Product Range

- Flats and sandals
- Boots and booties
- Heels and wedges
- Sneakers
- Mules and slides

All styles designed for women with classic, versatile aesthetics.

## Design Philosophy

Bhava focuses on creating timeless, elegant footwear that doesn't compromise on ethics or style. Each design is carefully crafted to be comfortable, durable, and beautiful.

## Pricing

Mid to premium range, typically $120-$280 per pair, reflecting the handmade quality and sustainable materials used.
