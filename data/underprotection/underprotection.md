## Overview

Underprotection is a Danish Certified B Corporation offering romantic, high-quality lingerie made from sustainable materials.

## Sustainability Features

- Certified B Corporation
- Uses sustainable materials exclusively
- Recyclable and biodegradable packaging
- Size-inclusive for all ages
- Focus on quality and longevity
- Transparent production

## Materials

- TENCEL™ Lyocell
- Recycled polyester
- Recycled polyamide
- Organic cotton
- Sustainable fabrics

## Product Range

Romantic, sexy lingerie and intimates for women of all ages and sizes with Danish design aesthetic.

## B Corp Commitment

Certified B Corporation status demonstrates commitment to social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency.

## Packaging

All packaging is either recyclable or biodegradable, eliminating waste and environmental impact.
