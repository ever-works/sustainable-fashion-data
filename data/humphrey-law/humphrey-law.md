## Overview

Humphrey Law is a family-run sock specialist business from Melbourne, operating since 1947. They produce high-quality socks from the finest natural yarns with a focus on comfort, durability, and health-conscious design.

## Sustainability Features

- 357 solar panels on facility roof generating electricity for every second sock produced
- Certified by Australian Made and Owned
- Certified by Ethical Clothing Australia
- Full sock-making process is transparent and viewable online

## Products

- Socks made from finest natural yarns including wool and cotton
- Trademarked Health Sock design providing no slip and no circulation restriction
- Wide range of designs and sizes
- Products designed for long-lasting trust and reliability
