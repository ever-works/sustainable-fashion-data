## Overview

Since launching in 2001, Stella McCartney has been a trailblazer in sustainable luxury fashion, proving that high-end fashion can be both beautiful and ethical.

## Sustainability Innovations

- Never used leather, fur, feathers or skins throughout brand history
- SS2026 collection included air-purifying denim
- Introduced FEVVERS, a plant-based alternative to feathers
- First luxury handbag made from Mirum, a plant-based material
- Uses innovative materials like Mylo™ (mushroom leather)
- Utilizes VEGEA (grape-based leather)

## Materials

- Mirum (plant-based leather alternative)
- Mylo™ (mushroom leather)
- VEGEA (grape-based leather)
- Plant-based feather alternatives
- Sustainable and cruelty-free textiles

## Product Range

Luxury ready-to-wear clothing, handbags, shoes, and accessories for women and men.
