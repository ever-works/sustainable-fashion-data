## Overview

The Fever Few Clothing makes beautiful jacket-style tops to order. Each piece is designed, cut, and sewn in its Bristol studio for the smallest carbon footprint possible. The brand creates pieces that become wardrobe favourites, encouraging a connection with your garment as it is worn through the seasons.

## Features

- Made-to-order production minimizing waste
- Locally made in a Bristol studio
- Small carbon footprint through local production
- Slow fashion ethos — pieces designed to be long-lasting and well-worn
- Jacket-style tops as specialty items
- Size range: UK sizes 8–16
