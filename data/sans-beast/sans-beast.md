## Overview

Sans Beast was founded in 2018 with the sole purpose of delivering pieces that combine beauty without cruelty. The Australian brand creates luxury vegan bags and accessories that are entirely cruelty-free, using innovative and sustainable materials.

## Key Features

- 100% cruelty-free and vegan products
- Luxury bags and accessories
- Uses Global Recycled Standard certified materials
- Innovative plant-based leathers made from cactus and apple
- Particular and selective about material sourcing

## Materials

- Global Recycled Standard certified materials
- Cactus-based leather alternatives
- Apple-based leather alternatives
- No animal-derived materials used

## Sustainability Practices

- Entirely cruelty-free production
- Innovative plant-based material development
- Use of certified recycled materials
- Responsible sourcing standards
