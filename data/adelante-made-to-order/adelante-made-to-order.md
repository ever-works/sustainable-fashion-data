## Overview

Adelante operates on a made-to-order model, producing each garment only after a customer places an order. This eliminates the massive waste problem of overproduction in the fashion industry.

## Key Features

- 100% made-to-order production model
- Zero inventory waste
- Women's clothing including dresses, tops, and bottoms
- Ethically manufactured in small batches
- Sustainable fabric selection
- Designed and produced in Guatemala

## Sustainability

- Zero waste from overproduction (no unsold inventory)
- Fair wages and safe working conditions
- Transparent supply chain
- Local production to reduce transportation emissions

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing reflecting the made-to-order, ethically produced nature of the garments. Dresses typically range from $100–$200.
