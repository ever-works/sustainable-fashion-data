## Overview

Born Again is a UK brand that screen prints onto existing sweatshirts, upcycling pre-made garments to extend their life rather than producing new items from raw materials.

## Sustainability Practices

- Screen prints onto existing sweatshirts
- Upcycles pre-made garments to extend their usable life
- Reduces demand for new textile production

## Sizing

- XS – XXXL

## Pricing

Affordable pricing for upcycled streetwear.
