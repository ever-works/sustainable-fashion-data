## Overview

Jyoti – Fair Works is a German brand making GOTS certified cotton products with fair trade practices. The brand creates ethical clothing using certified organic materials and maintains transparent production processes.

- German brand

Jyoti – Fair Works maintains GOTS certification, ensuring the highest environmental and social standards throughout production. The brand's commitment to fair trade ensures workers receive fair wages and safe working conditions.
