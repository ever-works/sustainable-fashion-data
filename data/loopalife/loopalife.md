## Overview

LoopaLife produces circular fashion in Europe from post-consumer salvaged materials without dyeing.

## Materials

- Recycled cotton salvaged from post-consumer clothing
- Closed-loop Tencel
- Recycled polyester

## Sustainable Production

- Made in Europe
- Sorting and manufacturing processes eliminate the need to dye fabric
- Reduces water and chemical consumption by avoiding dyeing

## Sizing

- Women: S-XL
- Men: S-XXL
