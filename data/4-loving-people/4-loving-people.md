## Overview

4 Loving People is a cashmere brand born from a life-changing trip to Mongolia, where the founders discovered not just exceptionally soft cashmere but the people, animals, and traditions behind it. The brand focuses on sustainably sourced, beautifully made pieces designed for longevity rather than trends.

## Sourcing & Ethics

- Cashmere sourced directly from nomadic herders in Mongolia
- Hand-combed goats following age-old traditions that respect both animals and the land
- Transparent, traceable supply chain from herd to finished garment
- Gentle, sustainable dyeing and production methods at every step

## Product Range

- Elevated essentials including dresses, cardigans, sweaters, skirts, pants, and beanies
- Timeless, versatile designs meant to be worn repeatedly across seasons
- Focus on durability and ultra-soft quality

## Design Philosophy

The brand rejects fast fashion trends in favor of pieces that last season after season. Collections emphasize comfort, style, and the belief that true luxury comes from care and intention rather than excess.
