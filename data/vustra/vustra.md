## Overview

Vustra is a sustainable menswear brand specializing in shirts made from natural, eco-friendly fabrics. The brand focuses on creating breathable, comfortable garments suitable for both casual and semi-formal occasions.

## Materials

- Organic cotton shirts
- Linen garments for warm weather
- Hemp blends for durability
- Natural dyes and low-impact production methods

## Features

- Men's shirts as core product line
- Natural fabric focus for breathability and sustainability
- Timeless, versatile designs
- Ethical manufacturing practices

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing ($), accessible sustainable menswear.
