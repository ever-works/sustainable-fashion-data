## Overview

Outland Denim makes premium sustainable denim while providing life-changing employment to women who have experienced exploitation.

## Social Impact

- Provides employment for women rescued from human trafficking
- Founded as avenue for training and employment of trafficking survivors
- Based in Cambodia with ethical manufacturing
- Fair wages and safe working conditions
- Skills training and career development
- Life-changing employment opportunities

## Sustainability Features

- Sustainable denim production
- Ethical manufacturing practices
- Premium quality for longevity
- Transparent supply chain
- Focus on worker welfare

## Product Range

Premium denim jeans, jackets, and shorts for men and women. Known for exceptional quality and fit.

## Mission

Combines fashion with freedom, creating beautiful products while supporting survivors and fighting modern slavery.
