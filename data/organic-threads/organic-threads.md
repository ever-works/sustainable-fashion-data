## Overview

Organic Threads was founded by Gail and Lonny Richards as a sustainable clothing brand focused on a single product: socks.

## Materials & Production

- 100% organic cotton
- Cotton grown and manufactured entirely in the USA
- No dyes used in production, resulting in natural earthy tones

## Products

- Socks in undyed, natural tones

## Philosophy

The brand maintains a simple, focused product line, taking pride in the quality of their fabrics and the sustainability of their production process. By avoiding dyes, the brand reduces chemical use and creates versatile, earthy-toned socks.
