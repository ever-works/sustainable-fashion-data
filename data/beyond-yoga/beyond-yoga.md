## Overview

Beyond Yoga is a female-founded sustainable activewear and athleisure brand. The company offers a dedicated maternity collection designed to accommodate a changing body throughout pregnancy and postpartum.

## Features

- **Spacedye Fabric**: Lightweight, stretchy, and breathable material described as buttery soft
- **Maternity Collection**: Bike shorts, leggings, and tanks designed for pregnancy and postpartum
- **Ethical Manufacturing**: Many pieces are made in Los Angeles using low-impact dyes
- **Social Impact**: Part of the Don't Ban Equality Coalition, has contributed over $185k to organizations including ACLU, The Downtown Women's Center, and Embrace
