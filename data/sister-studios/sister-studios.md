## Overview

Sister Studios is a Melbourne-based sustainable fashion brand known for fun, flirty designs with a gentle nod to 90's styles. The brand proves that style and sustainability can joyfully coexist through playful designs and vibrant prints.

## Features

- Melbourne-based design and production
- 90's-inspired aesthetic with vibrant prints
- Fun, flirty, and sustainable designs

## Sustainability Practices

- All collections made in Melbourne
- Produced in Ethical Clothing Australia certified factories
- Patterns designed to reduce textile waste
- Local manufacturing to reduce carbon footprint

## Pricing

Premium pricing reflecting ethical Australian manufacturing.
