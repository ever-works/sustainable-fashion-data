## Overview

Sofie Schnoor is a fashion brand that creates Scandinavian-style jeans combining minimalism with playful, feminine details. The brand is known for its effortlessly chic jeans that are both versatile and stylish, offering comfortable and sophisticated denim for everyday wear.

## Features

- Scandinavian elegance with a playful twist
- Flattering cuts and soft washes with subtle finishes
- Relaxed, straight leg and fashionable fits
- Minimalist yet feminine design aesthetic
- Available at Ensemble in Kortrijk

## Sustainability

- Effortless versatility reduces the need for multiple garments
- Focus on timeless Scandinavian design over fast-fashion trends
- Quality construction for elevated everyday wardrobe staples
