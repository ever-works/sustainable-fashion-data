## Overview

Patagonia has been a leader in sustainable outdoor clothing since 1973, combining high-performance gear with environmental responsibility.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses over 70% recycled materials across product lines, including recycled polyester made from plastic bottles and reclaimed fishing nets
- Worn Wear program encourages repair and reuse of garments
- Publishes supply chain details openly
- Supports Fair Trade Certified factories
- Footprint Chronicle allows customers to track the exact process of how clothing was made
- Fair labor practices and safe working conditions throughout supply chain

## Materials

- Recycled polyester from plastic bottles
- Reclaimed fishing nets
- Organic cotton
- Recycled nylon

## Product Range

Outdoor apparel, activewear, swimwear, and accessories for men, women, and children.
