## Overview

Loop Swim is a brand on a mission to close the loop on waste and promote circular design. Founded by two women from the US and India and headquartered in Shanghai, it transforms post-consumer plastic bottles into REPREVE UP50+ sun protective swimwear for men, women, and kids.

## Features

- Transforms post-consumer plastic bottles into swimwear
- REPREVE UP50+ sun protective fabric
- Sizes XS-XL
- Trendless, high-quality designs developed to retain shape and colour swim after swim
- Circular design philosophy
- For men, women, and kids
