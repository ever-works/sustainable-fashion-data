## Overview

Founded in 2020 by environmentalist Marianna Sachse, Jackalo creates durable children's clothing designed to last at least two years instead of one.

## Innovation

- 2-Year Pants fit children for 2+ years
- Extended sizing reduces waste and saves money
- Reinforced knees on best-selling pants
- Designed for durability and longevity
- Versatile, well-made basics

## Sustainability Features

- 100% organic cotton
- Responsibly sourced deadstock fabrics
- Fully circular materials
- Trade-Up program for resale
- Focus on strength and resilience
- Designed for kids who play seriously

## Trade-Up Program

- Send back outgrown garments for store credit
- Company inspects, repairs, and refreshes items
- Garments placed with new families
- Extends product lifecycle
- Promotes circular fashion

## Product Range

Children's clothing including signature 2-Year Pants, basics, and play-ready pieces built to last.

## Mission

Reduces waste in children's fashion through innovative extended sizing and circular business model.
