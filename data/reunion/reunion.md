## Overview

Reunion is an Irish brand producing clothing from organic and fairtrade cotton with a strong focus on sustainable materials and compostable packaging.

## Materials

- Organic and fairtrade cotton
- Labels made from offcuts
- Plant-based inks for printing
- Organic or recycled organic cotton thread
- Recycled brass chrome-free eyelets
- Dead-stock laces

## Packaging

- Reusable mailers that can be composted at home
- Tags and wrap made from recycled cotton printed with plant-based ink
- Twine made from organic cotton or jute

## Sizing

- S to XL
