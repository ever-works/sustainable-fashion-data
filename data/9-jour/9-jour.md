## Overview

9-jour is a Japanese denim brand specializing in jeans colored in every hue imaginable. The brand uses only selvedge denim that's dyed naturally with low-impact solutions, creating vibrant, sustainable denim.

## Features

- Selvedge denim exclusively
- Natural dyeing processes
- Low-impact dye solutions
- Wide color range
- Japanese craftsmanship
- Sustainable denim production
- Colorful jeans
- Traditional selvedge techniques
- Environmental commitment

## Sustainability Practices

9-jour uses natural, low-impact dyeing solutions to create colorful denim without harsh chemicals. The brand's commitment to selvedge denim and natural dyes reduces environmental impact while producing high-quality, vibrant jeans in colors beyond traditional indigo.

## Product Range

Offers selvedge denim jeans in a variety of colors and fits, all naturally dyed with low-impact methods.

## Pricing

Premium pricing for Japanese selvedge naturally-dyed denim.
