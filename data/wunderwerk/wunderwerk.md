## Overview

wunderwerk is a German brand combining contemporary design and eco-innovation with denim to produce premium jeans for men and women. The brand creates timeless and eco-friendlier jeans while minimizing environmental impact.

## Features

- Premium jeans for men and women
- Made from GOTS-certified organic cotton and TENCEL™
- Range of fits from relaxed to modern and tailored
- Timeless style combined with sustainability
- Stores in Düsseldorf, Nettetal, and Kempen

## Sustainability

- GOTS-certified organic cotton
- TENCEL™ fibers
- Produced more fairly in Europe
- Designed to minimize environmental impact
- Quality construction without compromising on durability
