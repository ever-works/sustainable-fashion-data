## Overview

The Body Shop is a globally recognized PETA-certified cruelty-free beauty and personal care brand. Founded in 1976, the brand is celebrating its 50th anniversary in 2026, with ongoing efforts to maintain its UK and global presence following a challenging 2024.

## Sustainability Highlights

- PETA-certified cruelty-free brand
- In-store "Return, Recycled, Repeat" scheme for product packaging recycling
- Expanding product ranges include the "Spa Of The World" collection
- Classic staple ranges based on ginger and vitamins
- Introduction of new body mist fragrances, moving beyond traditional fruit-based scents

## Product Range

- Skincare products
- Body care and body mists
- Hair care
- Fragrances
- Makeup

## Brand History

Celebrating 50 years in 2026, The Body Shop has been a pioneer in ethical beauty and has maintained its commitment to cruelty-free practices throughout its history.
