## Overview

Clara Himmel is a fashion brand focused on comfort and style, offering easy-to-wear and easy-to-combine clothing for women. The brand's denim pieces are produced in Turkey from organic cotton and designed for year-round wear.

## Features

- Denim jackets and mom-fit pants
- Produced from organic cotton in Turkey
- Available in white and indigo colourways
- Timeless and ageless designs
- Ready-to-wear collection
- Physical stores in Hamburg and Dortmund, plus online store

## Sustainability

- Organic cotton production
- Timeless designs that transcend seasonal trends
- Focused on durability and versatility
