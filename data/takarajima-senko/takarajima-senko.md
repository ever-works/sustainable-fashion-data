## Overview

Takarajima Senko is a small two-story workshop in the countryside of Fukuoka Prefecture, Japan. The brand aims to "mid-to-mass-produce" traditional plant-dyed goods, preserving natural dyeing techniques with sustainable botanical dyes.

## Features

- Fukuoka Prefecture workshop
- Traditional plant dyeing
- Natural botanical dyes
- Japanese craftsmanship
- Artisan production
- Cultural preservation
- Sustainable dyeing
- Mid-scale production
- Heritage techniques

## Sustainability Practices

Takarajima Senko preserves traditional Japanese plant-dyeing techniques using natural botanical dyes. The workshop's approach to mid-scale production makes these sustainable, culturally significant goods more accessible while maintaining artisan quality.

## Product Range

Offers plant-dyed clothing and textiles using traditional Japanese natural dyeing methods.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Japanese plant-dyed artisan goods.
