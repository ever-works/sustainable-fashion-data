# Howlin' by Morrison

Howlin' by Morrison is a Belgian knitwear brand producing garments from wool sourced locally from farms in Scotland and Ireland, with select pieces handmade in-house at their own Belgian studio. By maintaining control over sourcing and craftsmanship, the brand delivers premium natural-fiber knitwear with full traceability from farm to garment. Where fast fashion knitwear relies on imported acrylic blends that shed microplastics with every wash, Howlin' by Morrison preserves traditional wool heritage through locally sourced, responsibly crafted pieces designed to last for generations.
