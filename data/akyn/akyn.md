## Overview

Akyn was founded by creative director Amy Powney after 19 years at Mother of Pearl. The brand focuses on refined, elevated pieces that strive for real change within the fashion industry.

## Key Features

- Aims to be the most responsible and traceable brand
- Commitment affirmed through certification
- Focuses on authentic storytelling
- Founded by experienced industry professional Amy Powney
- Creates refined, elevated pieces designed for longevity

## Pricing

Premium contemporary pricing, reflecting the brand's elevated design approach and commitment to responsible production.
