## Overview

Gnarly Tree makes all their t-shirts and hoodies in Fair Wear Foundation affiliated factories from GOTS standard organic cotton, Tencel and Modal, printed with water-based inks.

## Sustainability Practices

- Factories affiliated with Fair Wear Foundation
- GOTS standard organic cotton, Tencel and Modal
- Printed with water-based inks
- Recyclable sleeves and paper bags for packaging

## Sizing

- Women: XS – XL
- Men: S – XL

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for ethically made casual wear.
