## Overview

SKFK is a Spanish clothing brand committed to slow fashion and was the first in Spain to achieve both Fair Trade and GOTS certifications. The brand creates sustainable women's fashion with ethical production practices.

## Features

- First in Spain with Fair Trade certification
- First in Spain with GOTS certification
- Spanish brand
- Slow fashion commitment
- Organic materials
- Ethical production
- Women's sustainable fashion
- Pioneering certifications
- Transparent practices

## Sustainability Practices

SKFK pioneered sustainable fashion in Spain by being the first brand to achieve both Fair Trade and GOTS certifications. The brand maintains these rigorous standards while creating contemporary women's fashion committed to slow fashion principles.

## Product Range

Offers women's clothing including dresses, tops, knitwear, and accessories with Fair Trade and GOTS certifications.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Fair Trade GOTS certified Spanish fashion.
