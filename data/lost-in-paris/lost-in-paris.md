## Overview

Lost in Paris creates bridal dresses ethically in Sydney from vintage European lace found at antique markets. Dressmakers restore and refashion these materials into one-of-a-kind wedding dresses through sustainable upcycling.

## Features

- Made in Sydney, Australia
- Vintage European lace
- Sourced from antique markets
- Restored and refashioned
- One-of-a-kind dresses
- Ethical production
- Sustainable upcycling
- Unique bridal wear
- Artisan craftsmanship

## Sustainability Practices

Lost in Paris gives new life to vintage European lace through careful restoration and refashioning. Each one-of-a-kind dress preserves historical textiles while eliminating the need for new lace production, creating truly sustainable, unique bridal wear.

## Product Range

Offers one-of-a-kind wedding dresses made from restored vintage European lace.
