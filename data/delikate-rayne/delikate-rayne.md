## Overview

Delikate Rayne continually strives to uncover inventive, cruelty-free, and sustainable textiles to create pieces that change perceptions about animal-free fashion.

## Features

- PVC-free vegan leather sourcing
- Uses sustainable PU (polyurethane) as a safer alternative
- Eco-friendly material selection
- Innovating animal-free garment design
- Cruelty-free and sustainable production
