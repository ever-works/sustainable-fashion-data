## Overview

Avocado is a certified B Corporation known for organic mattresses and yoga products using GOTS and GOLS certified materials. Sourced from organic farms, the brand prioritizes sustainability and ethical practices throughout its supply chain.

## Ethics & Certifications

- Certified B Corporation
- GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) certified
- Climate Neutral certified
- Fair Trade certified
- MADE SAFE certified
- Vegan certified
- Zero Waste certified
- Won the Pinnacle Award from 1% for the Planet

## Product Range

- GOTS certified organic yoga mats
- Organic Pranayama Yoga Pillow (supports spine, opens chest)
- Oval and Square Meditation Pillows
- Bolster pillows (made from organic and plant-based materials)
- Pillows filled with vegan buckwheat hulls
