## Overview

Haeckels is a small skincare brand based in the town of Margate on the southeast coast of England, creating perfumes and skincare products from locally-sourced ingredients.

## Products

- Seaweed-based skincare products
- Perfumes made from locally-sourced ingredients
- Products using pine trees, roses, chalk, and blackberries

## Sustainability Practices

- One of just two companies licensed to harvest seaweed from the English coast
- Committed to maintaining the health of the coastline
- Uses glass packaging wherever possible to avoid harmful plastics
- All products are rigorously tested
- Meticulously documents practices including day of harvest, weather conditions, and precise ingredient location

## Transparency

The brand provides extreme transparency to customers, sharing details about when ingredients were harvested, the weather on that day, and the precise location where ingredients were sourced.
