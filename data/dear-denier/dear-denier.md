# Dear Denier

Dear Denier is a Danish hosiery brand combining contemporary design with sustainable production in Italian factories, using recycled nylon, elastane, and wool to create eco-friendly tights and stockings. By replacing virgin synthetic materials with recycled alternatives, the brand eliminates the petroleum-based waste cycle endemic to traditional hosiery manufacturing. Where mainstream hosiery brands produce disposable tights from new nylon that shed microplastics, Dear Denier proves that recycled-material legwear can deliver the same sleek design with dramatically lower environmental impact.
