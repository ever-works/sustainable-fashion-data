## Overview

Dear Denier is a Danish hosiery brand that combines design with state-of-the-art sustainable production in Italian factories. The brand uses recycled materials including nylon, elastane, and wool to create eco-friendly tights and stockings.

## Features

- Danish design
- Sustainable production in Italy
- Recycled nylon
- Recycled elastane
- Recycled wool
- Eco-friendly hosiery
- Quality Italian manufacturing
- Contemporary designs
- Sustainable legwear

## Sustainability Practices

Dear Denier uses sustainable materials including recycled nylon, recycled elastane, and recycled wool throughout its collections. Production takes place in state-of-the-art sustainable factories in Italy, ensuring quality while minimizing environmental impact.

## Product Range

Offers tights, stockings, and hosiery in various styles and deniers made from recycled materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable designer hosiery.
