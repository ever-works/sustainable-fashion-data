## Overview

Founded by Sugandh Agrawal in her Manhattan studio apartment, Gunas has been a pioneer in fully vegan fashion for over 10 years.

## Sustainability Features

- One of the first fully vegan brands
- Uses upcycled materials
- Cruelty-free and sweatshop-free
- High fashion with zero cruelty mission
- Simple yet durable design
- NYC-based production

## Materials

- Canvas
- Nylons
- Upcycled materials
- Sustainable alternatives to leather

## Product Range

Vegan handbags and shoes including the popular Emily bag with detachable straps in various color combinations.

## Philosophy

"High fashion; Zero cruelty" - proving luxury fashion can be completely animal-free.

## Heritage

One of the original vegan fashion brands, pioneering cruelty-free alternatives before sustainable fashion became mainstream.
