## Overview

Frankremme is a Norwegian brand founded by designer Frank Remme. The brand uses deadstock fabric and produces extremely limited quantities to minimize waste and create exclusive, sustainable fashion.

## Features

- Founded by Frank Remme
- Norwegian designer
- Deadstock fabric use
- Extremely limited quantities
- Waste minimization
- Exclusive pieces
- Sustainable fashion
- Contemporary designs
- Small-batch production

## Sustainability Practices

Frankremme uses deadstock fabrics that would otherwise go to waste, transforming surplus materials into contemporary fashion. The brand's extremely limited production quantities ensure exclusivity while minimizing environmental impact.

## Product Range

Offers limited-edition contemporary fashion made from deadstock fabrics.

## Pricing

Premium pricing for Norwegian limited-edition deadstock fashion.
