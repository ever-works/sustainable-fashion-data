## Overview

Not Basics is a zero-waste women's ethical clothing brand. All of its underwear is made from deadstock cotton fabric, while its range of basics is made from organic cotton. Deadstock fabrics are those that, for whatever reason, do not get used for their original intended purpose and may end up in landfill or be incinerated.

## Features

- Underwear exclusively made from deadstock cotton fabric, reducing demand for new made-to-order fabrics
- Range of basics (leggings, tank tops, body suits) made from organic cotton
- All items made ethically
- Plastic-free packaging
- Size range: UK sizes 6–22
