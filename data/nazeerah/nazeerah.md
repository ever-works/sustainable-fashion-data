## Overview

Nazeerah is a US-based brand featuring organic Egyptian cotton from regenerative farms. The brand partners with family farmers using biodynamic methods to restore soil health affected by the Aswan Dam.

## Sustainability Features

- **Regenerative Agriculture**: Works with Egyptian farms using biodynamic methods to restore soil health
- **Organic Cotton**: Sources premium organic Egyptian cotton known for its long staple fibers
- **Plastic-Free**: Entire collection is 100% plastic-free and biodegradable
- **Regional Supply Chain**: Made in a regional Egyptian supply chain, supporting local communities

## Materials

Organic Egyptian cotton is renowned for its exceptional softness, durability, and breathability. The regenerative farming practices help sequester carbon and restore ecosystem health in regions affected by conventional agricultural practices.

## Pricing

Mid-range to premium pricing for high-quality organic Egyptian cotton garments.
