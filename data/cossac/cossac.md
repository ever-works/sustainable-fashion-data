# COSSAC

COSSAC is a contemporary fashion brand using responsible production processes and a made-to-order model that eliminates overproduction waste while designing timeless, versatile pieces for the modern wardrobe. Each garment is produced only after receiving orders, dramatically reducing the excess inventory that drives fashion's waste crisis. Where fast fashion brands manufacture millions of garments in advance of uncertain demand, COSSAC's on-demand approach ensures every piece produced has a guaranteed home — delivering conscious design with minimal environmental impact.
