## Overview

Good For Sunday offers quality clothing made in Canada, focusing on sustainable essentials and comfortable loungewear. The brand uses eco-friendly materials including 100% organic cotton for soft, durable pieces.

## Features

- Made in Canada
- 100% organic cotton options
- Sustainable essentials
- Matching loungewear sets
- Eco-friendly materials
- Quality construction
- Comfortable designs
- Basics and lifestyle wear
- Local production

## Sustainability Practices

Good For Sunday prioritizes eco-friendly materials, particularly 100% organic cotton, and maintains Canadian manufacturing to support local workers and reduce transportation emissions. The brand focuses on quality essentials designed for long-term wear.

## Product Range

Offers loungewear sets, t-shirts, sweatshirts, basics, and comfortable essentials made from organic cotton and sustainable materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Canadian-made organic cotton essentials.
