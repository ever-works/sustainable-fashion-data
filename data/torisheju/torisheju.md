## Overview

Torishéju is a fashion label founded by British-Nigerian-Brazilian designer Torishéju Dumi, a Central Saint Martins graduate who made a significant impact with her debut Paris Fashion Week show in 2023.

## Key Features

- Founded by Torishéju Dumi, a Central Saint Martins graduate
- Debuted at Paris Fashion Week in 2023
- Naomi Campbell and Paloma Elsesser walked in the debut show
- Known for custom looks inspired by Nigerian wrappers
- Zendaya has been seen wearing Torishéju designs
- Featured on the January 2024 cover of British Vogue
- Blends heritage Nigerian design elements with contemporary fashion

## Pricing

Premium designer pricing, reflecting the couture-inspired craftsmanship and heritage-innovative design approach.
