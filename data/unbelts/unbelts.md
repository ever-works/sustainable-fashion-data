## Overview

Unbelts creates innovative sustainable accessories that challenge traditional design approaches. The brand focuses on creating functional, ethical alternatives to conventional accessories.

## Features

- Elastic-free belt design
- Made from sustainable materials
- Adjustable sizing
- Vegan and cruelty-free
- Comfortable alternative to traditional belts

## Sustainability Initiatives

- Uses eco-friendly materials
- Animal-free production methods
- Reduces manufacturing waste

## Product Range

- Innovative belt designs
- Accessories made from sustainable materials

## Pricing

- Mid-range pricing for sustainable accessories
