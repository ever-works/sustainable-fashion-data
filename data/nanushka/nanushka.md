## Overview

Founded in 2006 by Sandra Sandor, Nanushka is a Budapest-based brand known for supple vegan leather and modern, versatile designs.

## Sustainability Features

- Developed OKOBOR™ proprietary vegan leather
- Local production in Hungary and Serbia
- Season-less line of timeless staples
- Commitment to animal-free materials
- Reduces transportation emissions through local manufacturing

## Materials

- OKOBOR™ (56% recycled polyester, 44% polyurethane)
- Vegan leather alternatives
- Sustainable fabrics
- Recycled materials

## Product Range

Contemporary ready-to-wear including outerwear, dresses, and accessories. Known for minimalist aesthetic and vegan leather pieces.

## Season-less Fashion

Offers continuously available timeless staples, reducing waste from seasonal overproduction.
