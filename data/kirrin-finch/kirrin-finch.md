## Overview

Kirrin Finch's goal is to do good in this world. Clothes that fit great and make you feel confident and comfortable in who you are is what this menswear-inspired brand is all about. Apparel is crafted in ethical and sustainable factories while maintaining close relationships with the teams making the products.

## Sustainability

- All natural fabrics and materials
- Sustainable and ethical practices
- Ethically made in NYC, Japan, and Italy
- Gives back to important causes
- Pre-loved (second hand) options available
- Low-impact dyes
- Organic fabrics prioritized

## Size Range

US 0 – 24
