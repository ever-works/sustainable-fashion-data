## Overview

Les Récupérables is a French sustainable brand embodying a true circular economy by focusing on reusing already-produced materials. The brand creates clothing from repurposed and deadstock materials, eliminating the need for new resource extraction.

## Circular Economy Model

- Reusing existing materials
- No new fabric production
- Deadstock utilization
- Material recovery
- Zero waste approach
- Circular design principles

## Materials

- Already-produced textiles
- Deadstock fabrics
- Surplus materials
- Pre-consumer waste
- Repurposed garments
- Textile remnants

## Environmental Impact

- No new resource extraction
- Diverts waste from landfills
- Reduces water usage (no new fabric)
- Lower carbon footprint
- Minimal chemical use
- Extends material lifecycle
