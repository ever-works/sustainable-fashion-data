## Overview

AK Threads is a vegan brand that works with women's cooperatives and charities, including one in London, to make long-lasting clothing from natural fibres, some sourced from deadstock materials.

## Sustainability Practices

- Works with women's cooperatives and charities for ethical production
- Clothing made from natural fibres, including deadstock materials
- All products shipped without plastic - in muslin or calico, or reusable bags made by a women's cooperative from off-cut material
- Vegan brand throughout
- Can make clothing to order in custom sizes

## Sizing

- Made to order in custom sizes

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing reflecting fair, cooperative-based production.
