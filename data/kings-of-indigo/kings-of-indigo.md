## Overview

Kings of Indigo (KOI) is a Dutch denim brand founded in 2011 by Tony Tonnaer. The brand has grown into one of Europe's leading sustainable jeans brands, offering complete outfits for both men and women.

## Features

- Ethical and ecological production methods
- High-quality, timeless designs intended for long-term use
- Full clothing range: jeans, trousers, sweaters, T-shirts, blouses, and jackets
- Trendy and casual styles combined with sustainability
- Widely available at sustainable fashion retailers across Europe

## Sustainability

- Produced in a more ethical and ecological way
- Focus on high quality and timeless design to extend garment lifespan
- Available through sustainable retail partners in the Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany
