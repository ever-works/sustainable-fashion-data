## Overview

Maium produces sustainable rainwear from recycled materials with a comprehensive approach to reducing environmental impact across materials, carbon, and packaging.

## Environmental Impact

- Carbon offset via tree planting project
- Recycled materials in all products
- Rainwear from recycled sources
- Full product lifecycle consideration

## Packaging

- Recycled packaging materials
- Biodegradable mailing materials
- Home-compostable mailers
- No single-use plastic

## Product Range

Sustainable rainwear designed for everyday use in all weather conditions.
