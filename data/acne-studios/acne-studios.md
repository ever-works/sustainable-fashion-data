## Overview

Acne Studios is a luxury fashion label founded in 1990s Stockholm, Sweden. Known for its elevated minimalist aesthetic that is both timeless and innovative, the brand has attracted a broad, diverse, and loyal clientele. Along with a unique and multidisciplinary approach to design, Acne Studios has become a champion of sustainable clothing production on a large scale.

## Sustainability Practices

- **Longevity-focused design**: From its inception, the brand has kept longevity at the forefront of its identity, emphasizing high-quality products made for long-term usage.
- **Thoughtful material selection**: Acne Studios carefully selects materials and construction techniques to reduce clothing waste and its larger carbon footprint.
- **Repurposed collection**: Launched in 2020, the "Repurposed" line uses discarded offcuts and recycled fabrics to craft quarterly collections.
- **Waste reduction**: The brand aims to reduce clothing waste in the future through its material and construction choices.
