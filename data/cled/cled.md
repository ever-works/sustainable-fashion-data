## Overview

CLED is a Los Angeles-based brand transforming discarded glass from local businesses into beautiful fine jewelry. Each piece is handmade by a women-led team with zero waste principles.

## Upcycled Materials

- **Discarded Glass**: Sources glass waste from local LA businesses
- **Zero Waste**: Transforms what would be trash into treasured jewelry
- **Local Sourcing**: Supports Los Angeles circular economy

## Ethical Values

- **Women-Led**: Created and crafted by women artisans
- **Plastic-Free**: No plastics in products or packaging
- **No Mined Gemstones**: Avoids environmentally destructive mining
- **Animal-Free**: 100% vegan jewelry

## Handmade Process

Each piece is individually handcrafted, making every item unique. The artisans carefully transform reclaimed glass into wearable art.

## Environmental Impact

By upcycling local glass waste, CLED:
- Diverts materials from landfills
- Reduces demand for newly mined materials
- Supports local circular economy
- Creates beauty from waste

## Pricing

Affordable to mid-range pricing for unique, handmade upcycled jewelry.
