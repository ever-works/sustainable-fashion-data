## Overview

Birdsong is a UK brand that produces some garments from Tencel and organic cotton. All garments are made either directly to women paid a London living wage or to charities that support them, offering a supportive work environment with counsellors, friends, and professional mentors.

## Social Impact

- All garments made by women paid a London living wage
- Partners with charities supporting women
- Supportive work environment with counsellors and mentors
- Refuses to use photoshopping in marketing photographs

## Materials

- Tencel (lyocell)
- Organic cotton

## Inclusivity

- No photoshopped imagery — celebrates authentic body diversity
- Size range: UK 6–30
- Inclusive sizing that actually serves a wide range of bodies

## Sizing

- UK 6–30

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for ethically-made London fashion.
