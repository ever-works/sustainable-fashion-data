## Overview

Save The Duck is committed to valuing animals, nature, and people at every step of its manufacturing process. Every garment is 100% animal-free and supply chains are third-party audited. The company is a founding member of the UN Global Compact Network Italy, promoting commitments to human rights, fair labor, environmental protection, and anti-corruption principles.

## Sustainability

- B Corp certified
- 100% animal-free garments
- Third-party audited supply chains
- Founding member of UN Global Compact Network Italy
- OEKO-TEX certified
- Uses recycled polyester
- PFCEC (perfluorocarbon)-free DWR coating
- Circular fashion practices
- Gives back to environmental causes

## Products

- Wind-resistant and waterproof raincoats
- Detailed product pages with traceability, certifications, warmth ratings, and materials information
- Breathable and waterproof fabric with DWR coating
