## Overview

A pioneer in sustainable maternity wear since 1999, Boob Design revolutionizes pregnancy fashion with innovative "nursing-friendly" designs. The Swedish brand uses GOTS-certified organic cotton and recycled polyester throughout its collections.

- Sustainable materials

## Sustainability Practices

Boob Design has championed sustainable maternity wear for over two decades, using GOTS-certified organic cotton and recycled polyester. The brand's innovative nursing-friendly designs ensure garments remain functional throughout pregnancy and postpartum, extending their useful life.

## Product Range

Offers maternity and nursing clothing including tops, dresses, nursing bras, and essentials with functional, innovative designs.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Swedish sustainable maternity wear.
