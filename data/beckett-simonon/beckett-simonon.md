## Overview

Beckett Simonon creates made-to-order footwear handcrafted in Bogotá, Colombia by skilled artisans. The pre-order business model eliminates overstock and waste while ensuring fair wages for craftspeople.

## Made-to-Order Model

The brand operates on a crowdfunding-style pre-order system:
- Customers order in advance
- Shoes are handcrafted after orders are placed
- Zero overstock or excess inventory
- 4-6 week delivery time
- Sustainable production without waste

## Ethical Production

- **Fair Wages**: Artisans in Bogotá earn fair, living wages
- **Skilled Craftsmanship**: Each pair handcrafted by experienced shoemakers
- **Working Conditions**: Safe, ethical working environment
- **Community Support**: Supports Colombian artisan communities

## Materials

Uses ethically sourced leathers from Gold-rated sustainable tanneries that prioritize environmental responsibility and animal welfare.

## Product Range

Offers dress shoes, sneakers, boots, and leather accessories for men and women, all made with traditional craftsmanship techniques.

## Pricing

Affordable luxury pricing ($100-$250) made possible by eliminating middlemen and retail markups through direct-to-consumer model.
