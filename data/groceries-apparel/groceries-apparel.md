## Overview

Groceries Apparel is an innovative sustainable clothing brand that creates activewear and basics from upcycled food waste and recycled materials. All production takes place in Los Angeles using solar power and a closed-loop water system.

## Features

- Made from upcycled food waste
- Recycled materials
- Made in Los Angeles
- Solar-powered production
- Closed-loop water system
- Activewear and basics
- Zero waste manufacturing
- Innovative material technology
- Local production

## Sustainability Practices

Groceries Apparel pioneers the use of upcycled food waste in textile production, transforming agricultural byproducts into wearable fabrics. The brand's Los Angeles facility operates on solar power and uses a closed-loop water system to minimize resource consumption, while local production supports American workers and reduces transportation emissions.

## Product Range

Offers t-shirts, tanks, leggings, activewear, sweatshirts, and basics made from innovative sustainable materials.

Mid-range pricing for innovative sustainable apparel made in USA.
