## Overview

Arnsdorf is a thoughtful Australian womenswear label that prioritizes transparency and ethical production. The brand is recognized for its commitment to fair labor practices and the integration of sustainable materials, including recycled cashmere blends, across select collections.

## Sustainability Practices

- Use of recycled cashmere blends in select pieces
- Commitment to supply chain transparency
- Ethical labor standards for all workers in the production chain
- Thoughtful material selection to minimize environmental impact
- Price transparency: publishes full cost breakdowns per garment

## Design Approach

- Clean, contemporary womenswear designs
- Versatile pieces built to last beyond seasonal trends
- Focus on quality construction and sustainable fabrics

## Transparency

The brand maintains openness about its production processes and labor conditions, allowing consumers to make informed choices about their purchases.
