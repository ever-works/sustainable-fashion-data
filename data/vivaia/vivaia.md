## Overview

VIVAIA creates comfortable, fashion-forward, and affordable footwear using recycled materials with the lowest impact on the planet.

## Sustainability Features

- 3D knit technology transforms plastic into threads
- Uses 6 recycled bottles per pair
- Commitment to minimizing environmental impact
- Partners with global suppliers for fair working conditions
- Operates in over 61 countries
- Focus on sustainable and recycled materials

## Technology

- Special 3D knit technology
- Converts plastic bottles into wearable threads
- Reduces waste in manufacturing
- Creates comfortable, seamless construction

## Materials

- Recycled plastic bottles
- Sustainable materials
- Eco-friendly components

## Product Range

Flats, sneakers, loafers, and boots with emphasis on comfort and affordability, typically under $150.

## Global Impact

Operates internationally with commitment to fair working conditions and environmental responsibility throughout supply chain.
