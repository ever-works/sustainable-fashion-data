## Overview

Kimai is a UK-based fair trade fine jewelry brand creating luxurious pieces in Antwerp using 18K gold and lab-grown diamonds. The brand proves that fine jewelry can be both beautiful and ethical.

## Lab-Grown Diamonds

### Benefits
- Chemically identical to mined diamonds
- Conflict-free and ethical
- Lower environmental impact
- No mining required
- Transparent sourcing
- More affordable
- Same brilliance and durability

### Quality
- Certified gemstones
- Same grading standards
- IGI and GIA certified
- Excellent cut and clarity
