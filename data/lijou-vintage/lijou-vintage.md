## Overview

LIJOU VINTAGE is a specialized vintage lingerie boutique in Arnhem's Modekwartier, offering a curated collection of unique, unworn deadstock lingerie, bustiers, and corsets from the 1920s to present day.

## Sustainability

- All items are deadstock — never worn before, sourced from unsold inventory or overstock
- Extends the lifecycle of existing garments through reuse
- Circular approach to fashion — no new production required
- Each item is unique, reducing waste from mass production

## Products

- Vintage lingerie from the 1920s to present day
- High-quality bustiers and corsets
- All items are unworn deadstock finds
- Custom alteration services available to ensure perfect fit
- Many corsets double as outerwear tops

## Pricing

Pricing varies based on the uniqueness and condition of each vintage piece.

## Location

Located in the Modekwartier of Arnhem, Netherlands.
