## Overview

Albero Natur has been making quality underwear from GOTS-certified organic cotton since 1994. The German brand focuses on comfortable everyday basics crafted from soft, natural materials that are better for both the skin and the environment.

## Sustainability

- GOTS-certified organic cotton throughout the collection
- Produced in textile factories in Turkey with safer, fairer, and more ethical working conditions
- Focus on natural materials beneficial for sensitive skin
- Established track record since 1994

## Products

- Everyday underwear basics
- GOTS-certified organic cotton fabric
- Soft, skin-friendly designs

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for quality organic cotton underwear.

## Availability

Available via the Albero Natur webshop and at select sustainable retailers including Sus en So (Utrecht), Meer dan Mooi (Geel), Anders Getint (Leuven), and Maria's Corner (Kortrijk).
