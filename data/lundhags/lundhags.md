## Overview

Founded in 1932 by a Swedish shoemaker, Lundhags crafts jackets and coats designed to withstand the most extreme climates. The brand is rooted in Nordic heritage and produces durable, sustainable outdoor apparel.

## Sustainability

- All products are free of intentionally added fluorocarbons (PFAS/PFCs)
- Uses toxin-free DWR finishes
- OEKO-TEX certified
- Made with recycled polyester and organic cotton
- Exceptionally durable fabrics designed to last for years

## Products

- Heavy-duty raincoats and parkas
- Jackets built for extreme European/Nordic climates
- In-store availability for Nordic region customers

## Availability

Currently available only in Europe.
