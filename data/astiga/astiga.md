## Overview

Astiga is a Certified B Corp working closely with Kenyan artisans, small suppliers, and tribal groups. The brand provides interest-free loans to improve artisan working conditions and has completely eliminated polyester and plastic-based materials from its collections.

## Features

- Certified B Corporation
- Works with Kenyan artisans, small suppliers, and tribal groups
- Provides interest-free loans to improve artisan working conditions
- No polyester or plastic-based materials
- Uses Lenzing and organic cotton exclusively
- All suppliers SEDEX or SA8000 certified
- 100% compostable cornstarch bags
- FSC certified paper mailing bags and tape
- Measures and actively reduces carbon footprint
- No plastic hangers in stores
- Uses all fabric scraps

## Social Impact

- Supports an orphanage in Kenya
- Provides scholarships for education
- Beach clean program in Kenya
- Direct community investment through artisan partnerships

## Sizing

- XS – XL

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for ethical artisan-made fashion.
