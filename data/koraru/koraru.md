## Overview

Koraru is a Japanese brand redefining swimwear with a commitment to circular fashion. The brand uses ECONYL regenerated nylon made from ocean waste to create sustainable, high-quality swimwear.

## Features

- ECONYL regenerated nylon
- Made from ocean waste
- Circular fashion commitment
- Japanese brand
- Sustainable swimwear
- Eco-friendly production
- Contemporary designs
- Ocean cleanup contribution
- Quality construction

## Sustainability Practices

Koraru uses ECONYL, a regenerated nylon made from ocean waste including discarded fishing nets. This material choice contributes to ocean cleanup while creating durable, high-performance swimwear that supports circular fashion principles.

## Product Range

Offers sustainable bikinis, one-pieces, and swimwear made from ECONYL regenerated nylon.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Japanese sustainable swimwear.
