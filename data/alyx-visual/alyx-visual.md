## Overview

ALYX Visual is the sustainable sub-line of Matthew Williams' 1017 ALYX 9SM label, focusing on environmentally-conscious production methods.

## Materials & Production

- Cotton jersey textile made in collaboration with Recover Tex, a Spanish recycler
- Pre-worn clothes and ocean waste are converted into upcycled yarn
- Yarn is spun into fabric used to create tees and hoodies

## Products

- T-shirts (including designs such as the "Reverse Cowboy Tee")
- Hoodies

## Brand

Part of the 1017 ALYX 9SM fashion house founded by designer Matthew Williams, known for blending streetwear with high fashion aesthetics.
