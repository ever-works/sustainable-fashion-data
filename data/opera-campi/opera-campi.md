## Overview

Founded in 2017, Opera Campi creates premium quality garments from locally-sourced raw materials in Italy. The brand donates 4% of its profits to social causes while maintaining transparent, ethical production.

## Features

- Founded in 2017
- Italian brand
- Locally-sourced materials
- Premium quality
- 4% profit donation to social causes
- Transparent production
- Ethical manufacturing
- Made in Italy
- Social impact focus

## Sustainability Practices

Opera Campi uses locally-sourced raw materials, reducing transportation emissions and supporting Italian suppliers. The brand's commitment to donating 4% of profits to social causes demonstrates dedication to positive impact beyond environmental sustainability.

## Product Range

Offers premium Italian-made women's clothing using locally-sourced materials.

## Pricing

Premium pricing for Italian locally-sourced sustainable fashion.
