# Alice Halliday

Alice Halliday is a Cork-based ethical fashion designer crafting made-to-measure Bohemian bridal couture and event wear entirely by hand from vintage, antique, and sustainably sourced materials. Each gown diverts discarded textiles from landfills while transforming them into one-of-a-kind bridal artistry rooted in Irish craft traditions. Where mainstream bridal fashion relies on virgin fabrics and mass production, Alice Halliday's bespoke approach ensures zero waste through careful sourcing of existing materials and made-to-order construction.
