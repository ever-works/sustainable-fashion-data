## Overview

Yoga Democracy is a leading brand in recycled, eco-friendly women's activewear. The brand creates vibrant, high-performance workout clothes from recycled plastic bottles, combining sustainability with style and functionality.

## Features

- Made from recycled plastic bottles
- Eco-friendly activewear
- High-performance fabrics
- Vibrant prints and colors
- Yoga and fitness focus
- Sustainable manufacturing
- Comfortable, stretchy materials
- Moisture-wicking properties
- Women's activewear specialist

## Sustainability Practices

Yoga Democracy transforms recycled plastic bottles into high-performance activewear fabrics, giving new life to materials that would otherwise pollute the environment. The brand maintains eco-friendly manufacturing processes while creating workout wear that performs as well as or better than conventional synthetics.

## Product Range

Offers leggings, sports bras, tops, shorts, and yoga wear for women in a variety of bold prints and solid colors.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable activewear, with leggings typically $70-90.
