## Overview

Tradlands is on a mission to make clothes you'll be excited to wear time and time again. Effortless designs and sustainable garments are built through a slow and sustainable process that focuses on incorporating signature details to ensure each item remains durable and timeless.

## Sustainability

- Natural fibers and deadstock fabrics
- Small-batch production across two family-run factories
- Compostable polybags
- FSC-certified packaging
- Focus on durability and timelessness
- 'Wear It Well' blog for garment care and styling

## Product Range

- Neutral basics and workwear
- Tiered dresses
- Signature detail pieces built for longevity
- Sizes: XXS – 4X
- Women's apparel
