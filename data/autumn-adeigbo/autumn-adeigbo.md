## Overview

Autumn Adeigbo is a Nigerian designer renowned for her vibrant and fashion-forward creations. Her mission revolves around empowering women of all cultures while ensuring fair wages for global artisans.

## Sustainability Practices

- Produces garments on demand, reducing waste and overproduction
- Promotes environmental cleanliness and safety in manufacturing
- Ensures fair wages for global artisans
- Embraces ethical production practices

## Design Philosophy

The brand is known for vibrant, color-rich designs inspired by African heritage and culture, creating fashion-forward pieces that celebrate diversity and empower women globally.
