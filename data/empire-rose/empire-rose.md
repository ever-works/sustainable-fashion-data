## Overview

Empire Rose is a sustainable fashion label based in Fremantle, Western Australia, that has been producing limited-run, locally made party wear since 1998. Owned by Kathryn Cizeika, known as Perth's "queen of sustainable style," the brand challenges conventional expectations of what sustainable fashion looks like with a glamorous aesthetic that includes sequins and evening wear.

## Features

- Limited-run production model to minimise waste
- All garments locally made in Fremantle, Western Australia
- Small-scale business approach to environmental sustainability
- Provides training and mentoring for local makers in the workshop
- Product range includes party wear with sequins and glamorous loungewear
- Over 25 years of operation since 1998

## Sustainability Practices

- Produces limited runs to avoid overproduction and waste
- Supports local makers through training and mentoring programs
- Maintains a small-scale production model to reduce environmental footprint
