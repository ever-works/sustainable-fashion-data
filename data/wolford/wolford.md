## Overview

Wolford is a European luxury legwear brand that has been producing premium hosiery since 1950. Products are exclusively manufactured in Europe to high environmental standards.

## Sustainability Features

- Tights made from ECONYL® regenerated nylon from recycled fishing nets
- Recently introduced biodegradable and recyclable basics product line
- All products exclusively made in Europe to stringent environmental standards
- Products designed for longevity given premium quality

## Products

- Luxury tights and stockings
- Fishnets and stay-ups
- Socks in a range of premium styles
- Designer collaborations offering distinctive looks

## Note

Most materials remain petroleum-based, so the brand recommends ensuring products last as long as possible.
