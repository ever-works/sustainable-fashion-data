## Overview

Tennen is a Japanese sustainable fashion brand creating simple, timeless clothing that is 100% biodegradable. Made in Japan using natural fibers, the brand embodies minimalist Japanese aesthetics and environmental responsibility.

## Materials

### Natural Fibers
- Organic cotton
- Merino wool
- Washi paper (traditional Japanese paper)
- Hemp
- Linen
- Natural silk

### Biodegradable Promise
- 100% biodegradable materials
- No synthetic fibers

- Circular lifecycle
- Earth-friendly

Tennen uniquely incorporates washi paper, a traditional Japanese material, into clothing. Washi is:
- Strong and durable
- Breathable
- Naturally antibacterial
- Biodegradable
- Cultural heritage material
