## Overview

Studio 189 was co-founded by Rosario Dawson and Abrima Erwiah in 2013 as a fashion and lifestyle brand dedicated to championing craftsmanship and supporting artisanal communities in West Africa, specifically Ghana, Mali, and Burkina Faso.

## Key Features

- Collaborates directly with artisanal communities in West Africa
- Specialises in hand-batiked, hand-painted, and handwoven textiles
- Promotes cultural preservation and fair trade manufacturing
- Recognised for its vibrant, colourful design aesthetic
- Worn by prominent figures including Michelle Obama and Lupita Nyong'o

## Pricing

Mid-to-premium pricing reflective of artisanal craftsmanship and ethical production standards.
