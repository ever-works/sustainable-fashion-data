## Overview

Lavender Hill specialises in affordable yet luxurious wardrobe staples that are always in style and work with everything you already own. From unfussy t-shirts, vests, and tank tops designed for layering, to roll necks for cooler days.

## Features

- Majority of clothing made in the UK
- Environmentally friendly dyes used throughout
- Traceable natural fabrics including linen and Tencel
- Range includes t-shirts, vests, tank tops, roll necks, casual trousers, and leggings
- Size range: UK sizes 8–16
