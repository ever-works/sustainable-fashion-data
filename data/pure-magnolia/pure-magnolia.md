## Overview

Each Pure Magnolia dress is made in their Canadian studio by seamstresses earning fair wages. The brand uses eco-fabrics such as organic cotton and hemp silk, incorporating European laces, Indian silks, and vintage recycled fabrics into classic designs.

## Features

- Made in Canadian studio
- Fair wages for seamstresses
- Organic cotton and hemp silk
- European laces
- Indian silks
- Vintage and recycled fabrics
- Ethical production
- Classic designs
- Eco-friendly materials

## Sustainability Practices

Pure Magnolia ensures fair wages for all seamstresses and uses eco-fabrics including organic cotton and hemp silk. The brand incorporates vintage and recycled fabrics into designs, giving new life to beautiful materials while reducing waste.

## Product Range

Offers wedding dresses featuring European laces, Indian silks, and sustainable fabrics in classic, romantic designs.

## Pricing

Mid to premium bridal pricing for Canadian-made sustainable wedding dresses.
