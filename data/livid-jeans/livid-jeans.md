## Overview

Livid Jeans is a Trondheim-based Norwegian denim brand committed to sustainable production and circularity. The brand focuses on creating long-lasting jeans while offering repair services to extend their life.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Organic Cotton**: Uses GOTS certified organic cotton
- **Recycled Materials**: Incorporates recycled fibers
- **Low-Impact Production**: Minimizes environmental footprint

## Circular Fashion Model

- **Repair Service**: Offers professional repairs to extend jeans' life
- **Longevity Focus**: Designs for durability
- **Take-Back Program**: Encourages return of worn jeans

## Norwegian Craftsmanship

Produced with attention to quality and detail, representing Norwegian commitment to sustainability and ethical production.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable Nordic denim.
