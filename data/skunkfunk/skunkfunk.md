## Overview

Skunkfunk is a brand producing clothing from sustainable and recycled materials with a focus on reducing their carbon footprint.

## Materials

- Recycled polyester
- Tencel
- Organic cotton
- Recycled leather
- Linen
- Ramie
- Hemp

## Production

- Works with suppliers in China and India to ensure compliance with international standards (BSCI, ETI, FLA, FWF, SA 8000 or WRAP)
- 90% of goods transported by sea freight (lower carbon than air)
- Headquarters 100% powered by renewable energy (certified by Goiener)

## Packaging

- Reuses 2% of cardboard boxes from shipments
- Bioplastic bags instead of traditional plastic
- Recycled cotton paper for tags

## Carbon Footprint

- 42% of online store shipments are carbon neutral

## Retail

- Store at 2 Blooms Lane, Italian Quarter, Dublin 1

## Sizing

- Women: 26-34 (jeans), 34-46, XS-L
- Men: S-XXL
