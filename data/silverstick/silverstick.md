## Overview

Silverstick is a UK brand that makes long-lasting adventure clothing from GOTS certified organic fabric, dyed with azo and chlorine free clothing dyes in an accredited factory in Portugal.

## Sustainability Practices

- GOTS certified organic fabric
- Azo and chlorine free clothing dyes
- Made in an accredited factory in Portugal
- Long-lasting adventure clothing design

## Sizing

- Women: XXS – M (not across all styles)
- Men: S – XXL

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable adventure wear.
