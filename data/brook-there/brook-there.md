## Overview

Brook There is a thoughtful intimates brand that uses GOTS-certified yarn to craft organic undergarments and minimalist loungewear. Each piece is carefully cut, sewn and dyed in California, resulting in lightweight, buttery-soft fabrics.

## Materials

- GOTS-certified organic yarn
- Buttery-soft lightweight fabrics
- Some sets feature elegant silk trims

## Sustainability Practices

- GOTS-certified yarn for organic undergarments
- Low-impact fabrics and production practices
- American-made in California
- Recycled poly-bags for shipping
- Eco-friendly packaging that avoids single-use plastic

## Product Range

- Organic bralettes
- Underwear and panties
- Minimalist loungewear sets
- Intimates with silk trim details

## Size Range

32AB – 38CD; XS – XXL

## Pricing

Premium pricing for handcrafted, organic intimates made in California.
