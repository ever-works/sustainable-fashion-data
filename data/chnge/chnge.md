## Overview

CHNGE is a US-based sustainable fashion brand focused on creating durable, long-lasting clothing from 100% organic materials. The brand is designed to reduce environmental impact through material choice while producing garments built for longevity.

## Features

- Uses 100% organic materials across its collection
- Inclusive sizing from 2XS to 4XL
- Built-to-last construction philosophy
- Made in the United States
- Focus on timeless, statement pieces
