## Overview

DL1961 is a sustainable apparel brand known for responsibly made denim. Their maternity line features over-belly panels that are soft, supportive, and never restrictive, designed for everyday comfort.

## Features

- **Sustainable Materials**: Uses certified cotton and post-consumer recycled fibers
- **Waterless Technologies**: Employs Ozone and Laser techniques to create the lived-in look without the environmental cost of traditional denim washes
- **Resource Efficient**: Each pair uses less than 10 gallons of water (less than a 5-minute shower) and under 1 kWh of energy
- **Vertical Supply Chain**: Provides more transparency, fewer emissions, and less waste across the supply chain
- **Maternity Fit**: Over-belly panel design that is soft, supportive, and non-restrictive

## Sizing

- Size Range: 23 – 34; XS – XL
- Recommendation: Size up one size in shorts and jeans to accommodate pregnancy changes
