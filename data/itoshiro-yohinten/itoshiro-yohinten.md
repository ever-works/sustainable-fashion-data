## Overview

Itoshiro Yohinten is a Japanese sustainable fashion brand that embraces straight-cut designs to minimize fabric waste while utilizing natural plant dyes. The brand collaborates with local artisans to preserve traditional heritage techniques.

- Sustainable construction
