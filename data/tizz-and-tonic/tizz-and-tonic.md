## Overview

Tizz & Tonic produces organic cotton underwear in fun prints, quirky designs, and bright colors. All pieces are digitally printed and manufactured in small batches at their studio in Germany, ensuring quality and minimizing waste.

## Features

- GOTS certified organic cotton underwear
- Digitally printed fabrics in fun prints and bright colors
- Small batch production at their in-house studio in Germany
- Size range: XXS – XXL

## Pricing

- Starting at €30 EUR per piece

## Sustainability Credentials

- Organic cotton materials
- Digital printing (lower environmental impact than conventional screen printing)
- Small batch production reduces overproduction and waste
- Manufactured in-house in Germany, reducing supply chain emissions
- Made-to-order approach minimizes inventory waste

## Shipping

- Based in Germany
- Ships internationally
