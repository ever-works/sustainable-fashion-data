## Overview

OZMA of California is a Los Angeles-based brand focused on clothing that embraces the simplicity of the natural world. The brand creates intentional pieces that dismiss trends in favor of longevity.

## Features

- Designed and manufactured in Los Angeles and Peru
- Focus on sustainably sourced and biodegradable textiles
- Regenerative farming practices
- Signature palette of earthy tones
- High-quality fabrics including organic cotton, linens, and raw silk
- Proceeds from select items (such as the sisterhood bandana) donated to organizations centered on equality, wellness, human rights, and education

## Ethics

- Made in USA (Los Angeles) and Peru
- Sustainably sourced biodegradable materials
- Regenerative agriculture practices
- Supports non-profit organizations through product proceeds
