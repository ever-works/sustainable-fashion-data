## Overview

Reima is an outdoor apparel company specializing in children's clothing, prioritizing chemical-free designs given kids' propensity for hand-to-mouth behaviors. The brand's rain jackets are designed to keep children safe, dry, and protected in all weather conditions.

## Sustainability

- Free from PFAS and other toxins
- Waterproof and dirt-resistant materials
- Uses recycled polyester
- Plants trees as part of environmental commitments
- Budget-friendly pricing makes sustainable options more accessible

## Products

- Waterproof children's raincoats with cute patterns and colors
- Matching rain pants available
- Designs are highly visible to help recognize kids in inclement weather

## Pricing

Raincoats start at $50.
