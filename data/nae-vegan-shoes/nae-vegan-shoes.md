## Overview

NAE (No Animal Exploitation) Vegan Shoes is a Portuguese brand dedicated to creating sustainable, cruelty-free footwear. The brand uses innovative materials to craft stylish shoes without harming animals or the environment.

## Materials

- **Piñatex**: Made from pineapple leaf fibers
- **Cork**: Natural and renewable material
- **Organic cotton**: GOTS certified
- **Recycled tire soles**: Repurposed rubber
- **Apple leather**: Made from apple waste
- **Microfibers**: Eco-friendly synthetic materials

## Sustainability Features

- 100% vegan and cruelty-free
- Use of recycled and upcycled materials
- Innovative plant-based leather alternatives
- Ethical manufacturing in Portugal
- Sustainable packaging
- Carbon-neutral shipping options

## Product Range

- Sandals
- Sneakers
- Boots
- Heels
- Flats
- Accessories

Available for men and women with worldwide shipping.
