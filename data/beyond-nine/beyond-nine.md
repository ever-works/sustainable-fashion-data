## Overview

Beyond Nine creates durable, long-lasting women's clothing that adapts to changing life needs using high-quality sustainable materials.

## Sustainability Features

- Organic cotton and recycled polyester
- Deadstock fabric utilization
- Limited stock production
- Fair manufacturing with living wage
- Size-inclusive: UK sizes 6-22

## Materials

- Deadstock materials
- Low-impact dyes
