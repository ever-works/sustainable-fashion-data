## Overview

NICO is an Australian brand founded in 2012 that creates high-quality basics and underwear from luxury, functional, and sustainable materials.

## Sustainability Features

- Socks made from recycled cotton textile waste
- Manufacturing in Bangladesh significantly reduces water and CO2 emissions compared to conventional cotton sock production
- Focus on durable, long-lasting quality to reduce replacement frequency

## Products

- Recycled cotton socks in basic colours (white, black, grey)
- Small curated collection of essential styles
- High-quality basics and underwear for everyday wear
