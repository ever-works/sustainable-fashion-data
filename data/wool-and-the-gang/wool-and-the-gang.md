## Overview

Wool and the Gang is a sustainable crafting brand that provides knit-your-own sweater and accessory kits. The brand uses sustainably sourced yarns and materials, making it easy for consumers to create their own garments at home.

## Product Features

- DIY knitting kits for sweaters and accessories
- Sustainably sourced yarns and materials
- Project difficulty levels from Beginner to Advanced
- Kits also suitable as gift ideas
- Enables consumers to create their own garments at home
