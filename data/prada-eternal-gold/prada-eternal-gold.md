## Overview

Prada has ventured into the world of fine jewelry with its Eternal Gold collection, extending the Italian luxury label's commitment to recycled materials into jewelry.

## Sustainability Features

- All pieces made from upcycled gold
- Made-to-order pieces designed to reduce waste
- Commitment to recycled materials consistent with the brand's clothing line approach
- Jewelry designed as an emotional journey to be passed on through generations
- Led by Prada's jewelry director Timothy Iwata

## Collections

- **Eternal Gold**: Fine jewelry line entirely crafted from upcycled gold, with made-to-order options
