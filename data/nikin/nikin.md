## Overview

NIKIN is a Swiss brand where a tree is planted every time you purchase a NIKIN product. The brand lets customers wear their morals with tree pride visible across its product range.

## Features

- Tree planted for every product purchased
- Tree-themed design elements throughout the collection
- Swiss brand origin
- Direct participation in environmental action

## Sustainability

- Tree planting program with every purchase
- Ethical business model tied to environmental impact
- Independently assessed and rated by Good On You

## Sizing

Products available in sizes XS-2XL.
