## Overview

Circular is a sustainable fashion platform named for the "circular economy" concept, founded by Vanessa. The brand focuses on extending the lifecycle of clothing items by facilitating resale and consignment.

## How It Works

- Items purchased can be used and then passed on or sold when still in good condition
- Makes thrifting easy with a curated selection of high-end brands at more affordable prices
- Consignment program allows customers to sell their own items and receive 50% of the profits once sold

## Mission

Vanessa founded Circular to make a difference in the fashion industry while sharing amazing and unique finds with others who love fashion.
