## Overview

Nomads Hemp Wear is a British Columbia-based brand specializing in hemp clothing for women and men. The brand harnesses hemp's natural sustainability and durability to create eco-friendly garments.

## Features

- BC-based Canadian brand
- Specializes in hemp fabric
- Women's and men's clothing
- Eco-friendly materials
- Durable construction
- Naturally antibacterial
- Sustainable fiber
- Low environmental impact
- Comfortable hemp garments

## Sustainability Practices

Hemp is one of the most sustainable fibers available, requiring minimal water, no pesticides, and improving soil health. Nomads Hemp Wear capitalizes on these benefits to create durable, eco-friendly clothing that is naturally antibacterial and becomes softer with each wash.

## Product Range

Offers hemp clothing including shirts, dresses, pants, tops, and accessories for women and men.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for sustainable hemp clothing.
