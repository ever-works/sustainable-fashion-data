## Overview

ONDO is a small business brand specializing in fashionable and functional low-cut, no-show socks with a no-slip guarantee. Working with ethical manufacturers, the brand focuses on organic cotton and merino wool sock designs.

## Materials

- Certified organic cotton
- Merino wool

## Key Features

- No-slip guarantee — ensures zero slippage while walking
- Thermoregulating properties
- Moisture-wicking
- Odor prevention
- Low-cut, no-show styles
- Ethically manufactured

## Social Impact

- Gives back 1% of revenue to support student access to professional clothing

## Sizing

- Women's 4.5–16.5
- Men's 3–15

## Pricing

Starting at $12 per pair.
