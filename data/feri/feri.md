## Overview

Feri is a Wexford-based label making small quantities of timeless clothing in Ireland and India.

## Materials - India Production

- Locally produced GOTS certified organic cotton
- GOTS certified organic orange fabric made from citrus fruit by-products
- Produced in a Sedex approved small-scale factory

## Materials - Ireland Production

- Locally sourced fabric
- Vintage fabric
- Deadstock fabric

## Additional Practices

- Upcycles garments and fabric into new items
- Small batch production

## Product Range

- Timeless clothing for women
