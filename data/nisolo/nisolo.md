## Overview

Nisolo crafts sustainable footwear and accessories while ensuring artisans receive fair wages and work in safe conditions.

## Sustainability Features

- Certified B Corporation
- Climate Neutral Certified
- Adheres to Leather Working Group guidelines
- Uses environmentally-friendly materials
- Rated 'Good' by Good On You for environment and workers' rights
- Thoughtful and intentional crafting approach

## Ethical Practices

- Fair trade wages for all producers and artisans
- Safe working conditions guaranteed
- Healthcare provided to workers
- Partners with artisans in Peru and Mexico
- Focus on craftsmanship and quality

## Product Range

Shoes, boots, sneakers, sandals, belts, and bags known for timeless design and quality construction.

## Mission

Combines beautiful design with ethical practices and environmental responsibility to create footwear and accessories that make a positive impact.
