## Overview

Plasticity is a Japanese upcycling brand addressing Japan's plastic waste problem by transforming discarded plastic umbrellas into stylish, practical bags. The brand gives new life to materials that would otherwise end up in landfills.

## Features

- Upcycles plastic umbrellas
- Japanese brand
- Tackles plastic waste
- Cool, practical designs
- Waterproof materials
- Unique bags and accessories
- Waste reduction focus
- Innovative upcycling
- Environmental impact

## Sustainability Practices

Plasticity diverts plastic umbrellas from landfills, transforming them into durable, waterproof bags and accessories. This innovative approach addresses Japan's specific waste challenges while creating functional products from materials traditionally considered single-use.

## Product Range

Offers bags, totes, and accessories made from upcycled plastic umbrella materials.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for unique upcycled accessories.
