## Overview

BRGN is a Norwegian outerwear brand creating durable, weather-resistant clothing designed specifically for Nordic climates. The brand combines functionality with environmental responsibility.

## Sustainable Materials

- **Recycled Polyester**: Uses recycled synthetic fibers
- **Eco-Friendly Materials**: Environmentally conscious fabric choices
- **Durable Construction**: Built to withstand harsh weather

## Nordic Design

Designed for the challenging weather conditions of Norway and Scandinavia:
- Weather-resistant
- Functional details
- Minimalist aesthetic
- Long-lasting quality

## Environmental Commitment

Focuses on creating products that last, reducing the need for frequent replacement and minimizing overall environmental impact.

## Pricing

Mid-range to premium pricing for quality Norwegian outerwear.
