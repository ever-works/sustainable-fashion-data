## Overview

Seventh Pocket is a slow fashion boutique located in a former billiards room in Yackandandah, a historic gold-mining village three hours from Melbourne in Victoria's High Country. The store aims to shine a light on slow fashion by curating locally designed and ethically made clothing and accessories from brands such as Lois Hazel, Keegan, and Wolf & Mishka.

## Features

- Curated selection of locally designed clothing and accessories
- Features ethical brands including Lois Hazel, Keegan, and Wolf & Mishka
- Housed in a historic former billiards room in Yackandandah
- Focus on slow fashion and purposeful shopping
- Located in Victoria's High Country region

## Sustainability Practices

- Stocks only ethically made garments from verified brands
- Promotes slow fashion over fast fashion consumption
- Prioritises locally designed labels to reduce supply chain emissions
