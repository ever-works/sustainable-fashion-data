## Overview

Rhumaa is a Netherlands-based brand making garments from natural fabrics including organic cotton in factories across Peru, Portugal, and Turkey.

## Sustainability Practices

- Fabric recycling and waste reduction
- Repurposing wherever possible

## Social Impact

- Rhumaa Foundation supports projects in art communities, fair fashion, and sustainability
- Donations via foundation to organisations like 'Learn to Earn' for creative skills development and job placement for young people

## Sizing

- Women: XS-XL
- Men: 41-50, S-XL
