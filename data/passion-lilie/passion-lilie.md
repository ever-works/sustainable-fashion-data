## Overview

Passion Lilie is a fair trade brand celebrating heritage textile techniques with classic silhouettes in unique handcrafted fabrics.

## Artisan Support

- Made by artisans in India earning fair wages
- Preserves heritage textile techniques
- Supports craftspeople and traditional methods
- Ethically handmade in small batches
- Fair trade verified by Fair Trade Federation

## Sustainability Features

- Uses 100% GOTS certified organic cotton
- Handwoven ikat fabrics
- Hand block printed textiles
- Small batch production
- Fair trade practices
- Ethical manufacturing

## Materials

- GOTS certified organic cotton
- Handwoven ikat fabric
- Hand block printed textiles

## Product Range

Button-down shirts, dresses, tops, and women's apparel featuring unique handcrafted patterns and traditional textile techniques.

## Design Philosophy

Blends classic, streamlined silhouettes with playful hand-printed and handwoven fabrics that celebrate artisan craftsmanship.
