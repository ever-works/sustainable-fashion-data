# North Face Renewed

The North Face Renewed is a refurbished gear program selling pre-owned, inspected, and restored outdoor apparel and equipment at discounted prices to extend product lifespans and keep quality gear out of landfills. Each renewed item undergoes rigorous cleaning and quality checks before being resold with full brand warranty backing. Where outdoor brands typically push seasonal new purchases, Renewed proves that extending the life of existing performance gear through professional refurbishment delivers equal value while dramatically reducing textile waste and manufacturing emissions.
