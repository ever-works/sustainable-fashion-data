## Overview

ASK Scandinavia is a Helsinki-based brand making sustainably made bags from plant leather, 100% regenerated nylon fiber, or 100% recycled polyester. The brand creates eco-friendly accessories with Scandinavian design principles.

## Features

- Helsinki-based brand
- Plant leather materials
- 100% regenerated nylon fiber
- 100% recycled polyester
- Sustainable bags
- Scandinavian design
- Finnish brand
- Eco-friendly accessories
- Innovative materials

## Sustainability Practices

ASK Scandinavia uses innovative materials including plant leather, regenerated nylon fiber, and recycled polyester to create sustainable bags. The brand combines Finnish design with environmental responsibility through material innovation.

## Product Range

Offers bags and accessories made from plant leather, regenerated nylon, and recycled polyester.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for Scandinavian sustainable bags.
