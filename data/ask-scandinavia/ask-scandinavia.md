## Overview

ASK Scandinavia is a Helsinki-based brand making sustainably made bags from plant leather, 100% regenerated nylon fiber, or 100% recycled polyester. The brand creates eco-friendly accessories with Scandinavian design principles.

## Features

- Helsinki-based brand
- Plant leather materials
- 100% regenerated nylon fiber
- 100% recycled polyester
- Sustainable bags
- Scandinavian design
- Finnish brand
- Eco-friendly accessories
- Innovative materials
