## Overview

Endangered Tees is a zero waste apparel brand featuring plastic-free packaging. Hang tags are made from recycled material, printed with water-soluble ink, and contain Non-GMO wildflower seeds that customers can plant.

## Features

- Zero waste approach
- Plastic-free packaging
- Recycled hang tags
- Water-soluble ink
- Non-GMO wildflower seeds
- Plantable tags
- Sustainable apparel
- Environmental focus
- Biodegradable materials

## Sustainability Practices

Endangered Tees eliminates plastic from all packaging and creates plantable hang tags from recycled material. The water-soluble ink and Non-GMO wildflower seeds transform packaging waste into environmental benefit.

## Product Range

Offers sustainable t-shirts and apparel with zero-waste, plastic-free packaging.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for zero-waste sustainable apparel.
