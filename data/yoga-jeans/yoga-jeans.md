## Overview

Yoga Jeans makes handcrafted jeans in their own factory in Quebec, Canada. The brand combines eco-friendly practices with comfortable stretch denim technology for jeans that move with you.

## Features

- Made in Quebec, Canada
- Own factory production
- Handcrafted jeans
- Eco-friendly practices
- Comfortable stretch denim
- Canadian manufacturing
- Quality construction
- Versatile fits
- Local production

## Sustainability Practices

Yoga Jeans maintains control over its entire production process through its owned factory in Quebec, ensuring eco-friendly practices and ethical working conditions. The brand's focus on quality and comfort promotes longevity and reduces the need for frequent replacement.

## Product Range

Offers a variety of denim styles including skinny, straight, bootcut, and wide-leg jeans in multiple washes and fits.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Canadian-made stretch denim.
