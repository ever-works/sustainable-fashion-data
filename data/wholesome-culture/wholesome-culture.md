## Overview

Wholesome Culture is a USA-based sustainable fashion brand focused on making the world a kinder and more sustainable place through eco-friendly apparel.

## Product Range
- Casual wear
- Activewear
- Accessories

## Materials & Practices
- 100% organic cotton
- Recycled plastic bottles
- Bamboo
- Eco-friendly water-based inks
- Biodegradable or recycled packaging
- Gives back 10% of profits to animal rescues and environmental organisations
