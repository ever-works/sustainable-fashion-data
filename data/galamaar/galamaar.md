## Overview

Galamaar is a Los Angeles-based swimwear brand focused on waste-to-fashion, transforming ocean waste into beautiful, retro-inspired swimwear. The brand uses ECONYL® — regenerated nylon made from discarded fishing nets, which account for nearly 10% of ocean pollution — to create durable, stylish suits designed to last a lifetime.

## Features

- **ECONYL® fabric**: Made from recycled nylon sourced from trashed fishing nets and ocean waste
- **Made in Los Angeles**: Produced in a small, family-owned and operated manufacturer
- **Fair-wage production**: Ethically manufactured with fair labor practices
- **Retro-inspired designs**: Chic prints and silhouettes with a vintage aesthetic
- **Built to last**: Following the brand's care guidelines, suits are designed for lifelong durability
- **OEKO-TEX certified**: Fabrics certified to be free from harmful chemicals
- **Separates and one-pieces**: A range of styles to choose from

## Sustainability

- Uses ECONYL® regenerated nylon from ocean waste and fishing nets
- Small-batch, local production in Los Angeles reduces transportation emissions
- Fair-wage, family-owned manufacturing ensures ethical labor
- OEKO-TEX certified materials
- Designed for longevity to reduce fashion waste
- American-made, supporting local production
