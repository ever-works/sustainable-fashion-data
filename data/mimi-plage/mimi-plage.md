## Overview

Founded by Ghanaian American designer Mimi Plange, the eponymous brand prioritizes creativity, craftsmanship, and textile recycling.

## Brand History

- Established in 2010
- Founded by Mimi Plange, Ghanaian American designer
- Collaborations with notable figures such as Manolo Blahnik and LeBron James

## Sustainability Practices

- Textile recycling as a core priority
- Emphasis on craftsmanship and quality construction
- Creative approach to sustainable fashion design

## Design Philosophy

The brand combines African heritage with contemporary design, focusing on creative expression, artisanal craftsmanship, and sustainable textile practices.
