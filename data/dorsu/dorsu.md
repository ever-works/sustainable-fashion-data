# Dorsu

Dorsu is a Cambodia-based ethical brand crafting everyday wardrobe basics and signature pieces with a focus on fair employment and responsible garment-making in the local community. By building a transparent supply chain rooted in Cambodian craftsmanship, the brand ensures workers benefit directly from every garment produced. Where fast fashion exploits developing nations' labor forces for rock-bottom prices, Dorsu proves that locally-based, community-centered manufacturing can deliver quality essentials while uplifting the people who make them.
