## Overview

JAN 'N JUNE is a more sustainable, fair and transparent fashion label from Hamburg. Created by young female entrepreneurs who could not find what they were looking for and decided to make it themselves.

## Features

- Minimalistic, super stylish designs
- Fair fashion label
- More sustainable and affordable simultaneously
- Founded by young female entrepreneurs

## Sustainability

- Transparent practices
- Fair fashion principles
- More sustainable manufacturing
- Independently assessed by Good On You

## Sizing

Available in sizes XS-XL.
