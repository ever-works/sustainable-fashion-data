## Overview

Matt & Nat has been a beacon of ethical style since 1995, dedicated to crafting high-quality vegan alternatives to leather.

## Sustainability Features

- All interior linings made from 100% recycled plastic bottles
- No leather or animal-based materials ever used
- Premium-quality vegan leather
- Commitment to cruelty-free fashion
- Recycled materials throughout
- Ethical manufacturing

## Materials

- Vegan leather
- Recycled plastic bottles (interior linings)
- No animal-derived materials
- Sustainable alternatives

## Product Range

Handbags, crossbody bags, shoulder bags, bucket bags, wallets, and accessories in contemporary designs.

## Philosophy

Named for MAT(erial) and NAT(ure), the brand believes in respecting materials and nature by creating beautiful products without harming animals.

## Longevity

Nearly 30 years of vegan leather innovation, proving cruelty-free materials can be luxurious, durable, and stylish.
