## Overview

Loop Cashmere is a UK-based brand rethinking the cashmere supply chain to reduce environmental impact while maintaining luxury quality standards. The brand is committed to making every part of production softer on the planet.

## Sustainability Initiatives

- Increasing use of recycled cashmere fibers
- Recyclable packaging across all products
- Low-water printing processes
- Electric vehicle couriers for delivery
- Rethinking every stage of the cashmere production chain

## Product Range

- Cashmere sweaters, cardigans, and knitwear
- Accessories and seasonal pieces
- Luxury essentials designed for longevity

## Approach

By addressing the entire supply chain rather than isolated elements, Loop Cashmere aims to create a truly lower-impact luxury product, proving that high-quality cashmere can be produced responsibly.
