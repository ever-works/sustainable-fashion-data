## Overview

Sentient creates bio-based vegan leather products using cutting-edge plant materials. The brand is at the forefront of sustainable materials innovation, offering beautiful accessories made without plastic or animal products.

## Innovative Materials

### Mirum
- Plastic-free plant-based material
- Made by Natural Fiber Welding
- 100% bio-based
- Circular design
- No petroleum products

### Desserto Cactus Leather
- Made from nopal cactus
- Grown in Mexico
- Low water usage
- No toxic chemicals
- Biodegradable

### Apple Leather
- Made from apple waste
- Upcycled material
- Soft, leather-like texture
- Sustainable production
