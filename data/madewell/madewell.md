## Overview

Madewell is a denim-focused fashion brand known for well-fitting jeans and contemporary casual wear. The brand has made strides toward responsible manufacturing and utilizes several sustainable fabrics across its product lines.

## Materials Used

- Cotton
- TENCEL Lyocell
- Recycled fibers
- Wool
- Cashmere

## Certifications & Initiatives

- Fair Trade Certified factories
- bluesign certified (tracks chemicals and materials used in textile production)
- Global Recycled Standard (GRS)
- The Good Cashmere Standard
- U.S. Cotton Trust Protocol sourcing program

Note: Madewell does not have a single brand-wide certification but works with multiple certifying bodies depending on the product.

## Size Range

XXS to XXL

## Notable Products

- Jeans
- Cotton T-shirts
- Wool sweaters

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing with emphasis on durable, long-lasting pieces. Known for quality denim that justifies the investment through longevity.
