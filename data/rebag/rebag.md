## Overview

Rebag is a luxury resale platform that enables consumers to buy and sell authenticated pre-owned designer handbags, accessories, and fashion items. By extending the lifecycle of luxury goods, Rebag plays a significant role in sustainable and circular fashion.

## Key Features

- **Authentication**: Every item undergoes rigorous authentication by expert professionals to guarantee authenticity
- **Luxury Brands**: Carries pre-owned pieces from top designer brands including Hermès, Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Prada, and many others
- **Buy and Sell**: Platform enables consumers to both purchase and consign their own luxury items
- **Clair AI**: Proprietary AI-powered valuation tool that provides instant price estimates based on brand, condition, and market demand
- **Rebag Infinity**: A resale program allowing customers to exchange purchases within a specified timeframe for Rebag credit
- **Quality Guarantee**: Items are professionally inspected, cleaned, and graded for condition before sale

## Sustainability Impact

- Extends the lifecycle of existing luxury goods, reducing demand for new production
- Diverts high-quality fashion items from landfills
- Reduces carbon emissions and resource consumption associated with new luxury manufacturing
- Promotes circular economy principles within the fashion industry

## Pricing

Prices vary widely based on brand, item, and condition, ranging from several hundred to tens of thousands of dollars for highly sought-after designer pieces.
