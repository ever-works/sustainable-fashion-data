## Overview

I Was a Kimono is a Japanese brand that upcycles used kimono into gorgeous fashion accessories and home decorations. The brand preserves traditional textiles while creating sustainable, unique pieces with cultural significance.

## Features

- Upcycles used kimono
- Japanese brand
- Fashion accessories
- Home decorations
- Traditional textile preservation
- Cultural significance
- Sustainable upcycling
- Unique pieces
- Heritage materials

## Sustainability Practices

I Was a Kimono gives new life to used kimono, preserving beautiful traditional Japanese textiles that might otherwise be discarded. Each piece carries cultural heritage while demonstrating sustainable practices through thoughtful upcycling.

## Product Range

Offers fashion accessories and home decorations made from upcycled Japanese kimono.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for upcycled kimono accessories.
