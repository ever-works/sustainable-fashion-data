## Overview

Ninety Percent operates on a revolutionary profit-sharing model, distributing 90% of profits to those who make the clothes and charitable causes.

## Sustainability Features

- Donates 90% of profits to garment workers and charities
- Uses sustainable materials exclusively
- Fair and transparent supply chain
- Ethical labor practices
- Transparent profit distribution

## Materials

- 100% organic cotton
- Tencel
- Hemp
- Sustainable and natural fibers

## Product Range

Contemporary clothing including basics, dresses, outerwear, and accessories for women.

## Impact Model

Customers can choose which charitable causes receive a share of profits from their purchase, creating direct impact through their shopping decisions.
