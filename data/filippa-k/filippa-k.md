## Overview

Filippa K is an established Swedish fashion brand known for clean, understated menswear and womenswear designs, now making commendable progress as a sustainable clothing brand.

## Front Runners Collection

- Pieces heavily scrutinized from material sourcing and production to destruction and disposal
- Recycled wool garments blended with durable recycled polyester
- Constructed without the use of any dyes
- Truly sustainable fashion pieces

## Lease Initiative

- Service allowing customers at Scandinavian and European flagships to rent garments
- 4-day rental at 20% of retail price
- All cleaning costs included
- Reduces consumptive practices by promoting rental over purchase

## Philosophy

- Focuses on creating sustainably produced pieces that last a lifetime
- Two-pronged attack on modern consumptive practices: rental for variety, quality pieces for longevity
