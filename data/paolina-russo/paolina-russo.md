## Overview

Paolina Russo is a craft-focused fashion label that has rapidly gained recognition in the industry, winning the Zalando Visionary Award in 2023 and being selected as a finalist for both the LVMH Prize and Woolmark Prize.

## Key Features

- Works with artisans in London to create collections
- Known for signature size-inclusive illusion knits and warrior dresses
- Winner of the 2023 Zalando Visionary Award
- Finalist for the LVMH Prize and Woolmark Prize
- Commitment to size inclusivity and artisan collaboration
- Combines traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design

## Pricing

Contemporary designer pricing, reflecting the artisan-crafted nature of the pieces and the brand's award-winning innovation.
