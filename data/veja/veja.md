## Overview

Veja is a French sneaker brand committed to ecological and fair trade practices throughout its supply chain.

## Sustainability Features

- Uses GOTS certified organic cotton
- Vegetable-tanned leather
- Ecological production methods
- Fair trade certified
- Transparent supply chain
- No advertising budget (reinvested in production)

## Fair Trade Practices

- Pays cotton growers 30-100% above world market price
- Pays rubber tappers 30-100% above world market price
- Direct partnerships with cooperatives
- Long-term relationships with producers

## Materials

- GOTS certified organic cotton
- Vegetable-tanned leather
- Wild rubber from Amazonian cooperatives
- Recycled materials

## Product Range

Sneakers and casual footwear for men, women, and children in various styles and colorways.
