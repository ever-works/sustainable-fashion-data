## Overview

Jessica Rey designs swimsuits and swim dresses that are full coverage and fun to wear. The brand uses high-quality Italian fabrics from regenerated pre- and post-consumer waste, including rescued fishing nets.

## Materials

- Regenerated luxe Italian fabrics
- Fabric made from rescued pre- and post-consumer waste (e.g., fishing nets)

## Sustainability Practices

- Cut and sewn in Los Angeles
- Ethically made in sweatshop-free factories
- Workers paid living wages
- Responsible sourcing practices
- Regenerated fabric from ocean waste materials

## Product Range

- Swim dresses
- Tankinis
- One-piece swimsuits
- Cover-ups

## Size Range

US XXS – 1X

## Pricing

Premium pricing for designer-quality swimwear made with sustainable regenerated fabrics.
