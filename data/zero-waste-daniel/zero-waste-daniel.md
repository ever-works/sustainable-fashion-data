## Overview

Zero Waste Daniel creates every piece from 100% pre-consumer materials, including cutting-room scraps, design-room leftovers, and unused fabrics headed for landfill. The brand extends its zero-waste philosophy to all packaging and operations.

## Features

- 100% pre-consumer materials
- Cutting-room scrap use
- Design-room leftovers
- Zero waste production
- Recycled packaging
- Recyclable office materials
- Upcycled clothing
- No new fabric production
- Comprehensive waste elimination

## Sustainability Practices

Zero Waste Daniel diverts pre-consumer textile waste from landfills by transforming cutting-room scraps and unused fabrics into contemporary fashion. The brand's commitment extends to using recycled and recyclable materials for shipping, packaging, and all office operations.

## Product Range

Offers upcycled clothing and accessories with each piece being unique due to the nature of reclaimed materials.

## Pricing

Mid to premium pricing for zero-waste upcycled fashion.
