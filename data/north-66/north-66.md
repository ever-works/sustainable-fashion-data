## Overview

North 66 is an Icelandic brand specializing in long-lasting, multi-functional clothing. The company has been carbon neutral since 2019 and offers both a repair service for any garment they have ever made and a take-back program for resale or recycling.

## Sustainability Practices

- Carbon neutral since 2019
- Repair service available for any garment ever made
- Take-back program for resale or recycling
- Focus on durable, long-lasting, multi-functional design
- Stores in Iceland and Denmark

## Sizing

- Women: XS – XXL
- Men: S – XXL

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for Icelandic outdoor-inspired fashion.
