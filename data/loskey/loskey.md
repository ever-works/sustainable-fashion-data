## Overview

Loskey makes timeless Fairtrade t-shirts from GOTS certified organic cotton, demonstrating attention to sustainable details across all aspects of production and packaging.

## Sustainability Practices

- GOTS certified organic cotton t-shirts
- Fairtrade certified production
- Labels made from paper derived from elephant and rhino dung
- 100% recycled cardboard for mailbox packaging
- T-shirt off-cuts used as ties

## Sizing

- XS – L

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for fair trade organic cotton basics.
