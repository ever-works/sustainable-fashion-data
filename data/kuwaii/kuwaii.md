## Overview

Kuwaii is an Australian-made Melbourne fashion label that stands as the antithesis of fast fashion. Founded on a philosophy of smaller, slower, and simpler, the brand produces minimal runs and made-to-order pieces with a strong focus on designing clothes you will wear forever.

## Features

- Minimal production runs
- Made-to-order options
- Simple silhouettes flattering on all body types
- Inclusive sizing up to Australian size 16
- Melbourne-based

## Sustainability Practices

- Made-to-order production reduces waste
- Smaller, slower, and simpler philosophy
- Focus on timeless design over trends
- Minimal run production

## Pricing

Premium pricing reflecting Australian-made, made-to-order production.
