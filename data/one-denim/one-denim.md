## Overview

One Denim sells jeans made with significantly less carbon emissions and water than traditional jeans. Their production methods use 98% less water waste and achieve a 92% reduction in greenhouse emissions. Cotton is grown with 65% less water and no pesticides.

## Sustainability Features

- 98% less water wasted in production
- 92% reduction in greenhouse gas emissions
- Cotton grown with 65% less water and no pesticides
- Some fabrics are organic, some recycled
- Recyclable shipping boxes

## Sizing

- 25 – 31

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for eco-conscious denim.
