## Overview

The Nearly New Cashmere Co offers a wide selection of preloved 100% pure cashmere knitwear. The Yorkshire-based team rescues thousands of discarded 100% cashmere items over the course of the year.

## Features

- Wide selection of preloved 100% pure cashmere knitwear
- Range includes jumpers, cardigans, dresses, polo necks, and loungewear
- Items meticulously reconditioned and repaired to nearly new condition
- Affordable repair service for cashmere jumpers to extend garment lifespan
- Wide range of recycled and restored styles and sizes
- Size range: XS–XXL
